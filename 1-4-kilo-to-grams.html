[tex] \frac{3}{4} + \frac{1}{8} = \frac{24}{32} + \frac{4}{32} \\ = \frac{28}{32} or \: \frac{7}{8} [/tex]

[tex] \frac{7}{8} - \frac{1}{2} = \frac{14}{16} - \frac{8}{16} \\ \\ = \frac{6}{16} or \: \frac{3}{8} [/tex]

Answer 3/8 kilograms he used for pochero
