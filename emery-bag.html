Needles - A needle is a small, very thin piece of polished metal which is used for sewing. It has a sharp point at one end and a hole in the other for a thread to go through. Knitting needles are thin sticks that are used for knitting. They are usually made of plastic or metal and have a point at one end.

Fabric - Sheets, pillow cases, blankets, towels, and other uses of fabric in the home - depending on which room you're in - dry things off, screen off views, keep people warmer, rugs on the floor to control noise, or keep a room warmer by covering cold floors - there are so many ways to use fabric.

Ruler - A ruler can be defined as a tool or device used to measure length and draw straight lines.

Thread - They form efficient stitches without breaking or becoming distorted during the useful life of the product. The basic function of a thread is to deliver aesthetics and performance in stitches and seams.
