Manpower is defined as the available person that can perform the task in the given time and chance for service. It is an application of physical efforts to conduct specific actions towards an output.

Materials can be described in different forms according to their uses. Most commonly these are the substances or ingredients used in creating a product or service.

Machines or Tools has built-in internal operations or working mechanism used for different purposes.

Methods are the process or procedure of taking actions step by step in order to achieve a desirable product.

Eggs are one of the foods that provide a lot of nutrients to the human body. It is also known to be one of the producers of protein, vitamin B6, B12, and vitamin D.

Here are some of the most common ways to cook an Egg:

-Soft Boiled

-Hard-Boiled

-Poached

-Sunny Side up

-Scrambled

Ways in processing or making an egg sandwich:

-Prepare all the utensils or kitchen tools that are needed. (nonstick frying pan, Spatula, egg molder, whisk, Slotted spoon)

-Prepare the Raw Ingredients (egg, slices of bread, sandwich spread, cooking oil)

- It is important to make sure on selecting the fresh eggs available to maximize their nutrient contents and can produce a good outcome.

Manpower: based on the given situation above, the manpower or the person in charge to make an egg sandwich is the mother. She is more capable of operating and performing the tasks, especially operating the stoves which is one of the important tools in cooking.

Materials: The materials or items needed to make an egg sandwich are mostly the items necessary to be used to perform the tasks.

These are the following: Fry Pan, Egg molder, Slotted spoon, Plates, Spatula, and a mixing bowl.

Machines or Tools:  It is optional to use an electric hand mixer to make the ingredients well mixed up or else another electrical machine to fry the egg.

Methods in making an Egg Sandwich:

-Initially preheat the frying pan on top of the stove and put the right amount of oil for cooking.

-It is optional to use an egg molder to make the egg in perfect shape.

-Use little condiments to put some flavor on the egg
