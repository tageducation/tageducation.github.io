1. List down the effect the soil erosion in table form


Answer:

There are several effects to the surroundings that we live in when soil erosion occur, some of these negative effects are the following:

effects on plants; when soil erosion occur, topmost layer of the soil that contains several organic materials are eroded, reducing the availability of needed nutrients of the plants.

effects on humans; soil erosion increases the dust that is carried by the wind that carries not only small debris that can be harmful for the human's respiratory, but also several infectious diseases such as tuberculosis.

effects on animals; as soil erosion occur, pollution and sedimentation also increases in bodies of water such as rivers, streams, and lakes, causing several species of fish to die.

effects on landforms; soil erosion can decrease the size of hills and mountains as it continues. It can also cut on grounds, create valleys and even ditches.
