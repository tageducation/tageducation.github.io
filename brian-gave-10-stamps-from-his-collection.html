Peter collected a total of 1 170 stamps. His collection ofPhilippine stamps is 4 times as many as foreign stamps. How many Philippine stamps did he collect?

He collected 936 Philippine stamps.

Step-by-step explanation:

What is asked?

How many Philippine stamps did he collect?

What are given?

total number of stamps - 1170

Philippine stamps - 4 times as many as foreign stamps

Solution:

Let x be the number of foreign stamps.

4x as the number of Philippines stamps.

To find the number of Philippine and foreign stamps Peter collected, we are going to represent the given data as:

x + 4x = 1170.

x + 4x = 1170

5x = 1170

5x/5 = 1170/5

x = 234

Peter collected 234 foreign stamps.

Philippine stamps = 4x

= 4(234)

= 936

Peter collected 936 Philippine stamps.

Checking:

234 + 936 = 1170

1170 = 1170 ✔
