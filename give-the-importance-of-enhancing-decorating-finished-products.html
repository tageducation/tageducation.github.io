17. Give the three importance of enhancing or decorating finished products


Finished products, regardless of how we call it, isn’t considered “finished” unless you decorate it.

Some people want to buy or to purchase products based on its packaging or aesthetic look. It is important to enhance and decorate a product in order to call it a “finished product”. It’s not enough for a product to be just sturdy and useful, it also needs to be aesthetically pleasing. Here are three importance of enhancing and decorating finished products:

1. So that it can attract costumers – people tend to purchase things that aren’t just useful to them, but they also consider the look of it.

2. So that it can add more use and value – enhancing a product is essential because it can add more uses. For example, a pencil was made. It needs to be enhanced (like putting labels, sharpening, adding features for better usage) so that it can create an impact, or it can add value to its consumers.

3. So that is can add more function to the specific product
