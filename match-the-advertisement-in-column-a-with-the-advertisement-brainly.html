13. is there still a place for print advertising along witb other forms like television radio and online adverrising​


Yes, these forms of advertisements are still used worldwide.

Explanation:

Print advertisements are still a common method to reach out to the masses. This is because not all people still have access to the internet or electricity, mostly in 2nd and 3rd world countries like the Philippines, Africa, South America, and so on. Most people (globally) still do not own electronic devices to see advertisements digitally. So to spread information, magazines, newspapers, and other forms of print media ads are still being used.

Print Ads have the lowest maintenance cost, it can be viewed even without electricity. When kept away from environmental hazards like weather and dust it can last longer. This means that Print ads have longer exposure to the target market making people still remember a product, service, or company in the long run. Longer exposure helps a company in branding its business.
