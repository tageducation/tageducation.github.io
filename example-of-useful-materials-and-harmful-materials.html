9. examples of useful and harmful materials


A material is anything that takes a physical form or is tangible. We see them everywhere and are a regular part of our lives. Here is a couple of lists of materials divided into two categories based on their safety.

*Useful Materials: these are materials that are generally around us everyday. We have them in our homes, our cars and at work with little to no negative effects on our health.

1) Wood

2) Steel

3) Ceramic

4) Plastic

5) Glass

*Harmful Materials: are the ones that do not belong anywhere near someone’s home. But in cases when we do need them, they are placed somewhere hidden to avoid or limit any physical contact to lessen any chances of problems.

1) Lead paint

2) Acid

3) Pesticides

4) Rat poison

5) Mothballs

It is hard to completely stay away from all harmful materials in today’s world, so it’s a good idea to at least be aware of most of them to limit one’s exposure.
