4. y²+4y-96=0 answer plzz​


Answer:

Trying to factor by splitting the middle term

1.1     Factoring  y2-4y-96  

The first term is,  y2  its coefficient is  1 .

The middle term is,  -4y  its coefficient is  -4 .

The last term, "the constant", is  -96  

Step-1 : Multiply the coefficient of the first term by the constant   1 • -96 = -96  

Step-2 : Find two factors of  -96  whose sum equals the coefficient of the middle term, which is   -4 .

     -96    +    1    =    -95  

     -48    +    2    =    -46  

     -32    +    3    =    -29  

     -24    +    4    =    -20  

     -16    +    6    =    -10  

     -12    +    8    =    -4    That's it

Step-3 : Rewrite the polynomial splitting the middle term using the two factors found in step 2 above,  -12  and  8  

                    y2 - 12y + 8y - 96

Step-4 : Add up the first 2 terms, pulling out like factors :

                   y • (y-12)

             Add up the last 2 terms, pulling out common factors :

                   8 • (y-12)

Step-5 : Add up the four terms of step 4 :

                   (y+8)  •  (y-12)

9. What percent of 48 is five percent of 96?


Answer:

10%

Step-by-step explanation:

- First let us get the five percent of 96.

let x be the number :

x = 96(5%)

x = 96(0.05)

x = 4.8

- Next we'll get what percent of 48 is x (since five percent of 96 is x)

Let y be the percent :

48y = 4.8

48y/48 = 4.8/48

y = 0.1

because we're finding the percentage multiply the answer by 100.

0.1 x 100

= 10

Thus 10% of 48 is 5% of 96.
