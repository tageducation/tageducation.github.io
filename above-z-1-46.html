1. Find x , y , z so that the numbers 41 , 46 , x , y , z have a mean of 50 and a mode of 45.​


Since the mode has to be 45, then 45 must be the number repeated the most times. Since the only other known numbers are 41 and 46 both repeated only once, then 45 has to be repeated at least twice. We will then say x = y = 45. Since the mean has to be 50, then

[tex]\sf\large{ \frac{41 + 46 + 45 + 45 + z}{5}}[/tex] = 50

Solving for z:

[tex]\sf\large{ \frac{41 + 46 + 45 + 45 + z}{5}}[/tex] = 50

[tex]\sf\large{ \frac{z + 177}{5}}[/tex] = 50

z + 177 = 50 × 5

z + 177 = 250

z = 250 - 177

z = 73

[tex]{}[/tex]

Answer:

x = 45, y = 45, z = 50
