"Gifts for the City" - a poem created by Shirley May

Its meaning

The poem entitled "Gifts for the City" is written by Shirley May de leon. The author wants to share the things that she noticed during her exploration in the city. She has observed that the whole city is facing heavy burdens because of the improper usage of the environment. At the end of the poem, the author persuades the citizens to do simple things such as following the traffic rules, proper disposal of garbage, and plant a tree to make the surrounding be green again.

26. your parents are giving you a very special birthday gift -a day out in any city that you choose after much thought,you have narrowed your choice down to two cities . write about the similarities and differences of the two?​


Both Hong Kong and Singapore are vibrant, bustling cities in Asia that offer plenty of attractions and experiences. They both have iconic skyline views, world-class shopping opportunities, delicious local cuisine, and numerous cultural landmarks to explore.

However, there are some distinct differences between the two cities. Hong Kong is a very modern city with advanced transportation infrastructure and an emphasis on business and finance. On the other hand, Singapore has a more laid-back atmosphere and a large green area that serves as an oasis within its bustling cityscape. In terms of culture, Hong Kong embraces its Chinese heritage while Singapore is more of a multicultural melting pot due to its history as a British colony. Finally, the cost of living in both cities varies greatly, with Hong Kong being one of the most expensive places in the world to live.
