15. What makes water aerobics unique from other forms of physical activities or exercises


Answer:

Water aerobics allows you to work every joint and muscle at the same time. Because water keeps the body buoyant and so does not overstress the joints and muscles, water aerobics can be done regardless of age, weight, or physical fitness level.
