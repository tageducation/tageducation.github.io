9. G9definition of brochureimportant if brochurefunction of brochure​


Definition of brochure

• A small book or magazine containing pictures and information about a product or service.

E.G. - holiday brochures , travel brochures

Important of brochure

• The sole purpose of such an informative paper document is to create awareness about a specific thing in a lucid language, easily understandable by readers.

Function of brochure / Basic function of a brochure

• A brochure handles three functions: to inform, publicize and identify.

20. Definition of -Supplementary brochures -Promotional brochures -Leave-behind brochures -Point of sale brochures -Direct mail brochures nonsense response= immediate report


Suplementary Brochures -

Often compared to booklets. Considered one of the simplest brochures to create, half-fold consists of two internal panels and a front and back cover.

Promotional Brochures -

Produced by making two bends. The size of the panels must be equal.

Leave-behind Brochures -

One of the obvious advantages is this format’s transformability. It offers more room for the designer’s imagination.

Point of Sale Brochures -

The form of this brochure looks like a zig-zag. Put simply, it includes six parts of equal size, created by double bending.

Direct mail brochures -

This type of brochure is also known as window fold. Its construction is similar to a tri-fold booklet.

FISH PORT COMPLEX

Being a tuna capital of the Philippines, any visit to General Santos City will never be complete without experiencing an early morning visit to its Fish Port Complex.

The famed Fish Port Complex is located in Barangay Tambler, General Santos City. The place is 32 km. South of the city proper along the shores of Sarangani Bay.

If you want to see large Tuna as large as the person carrying it, you must be at General Santos City Fish Port Complex very early in the morning. At the city’s fish port, you can witness how they weigh, classify and sort these tuna.

Just remember, short pants are not allowed in the place. You will also be asked to rent a pair of boots at the place, so it would be best to wear your socks for some protection.

SIDE TRIP TO LAKE SEBU

For a traveler like me who would want to maximize my travel, a side trip to Lake Sebu, South Cotabato is a great idea. Lake Sebu is 45 minutes away from General Santos City.
