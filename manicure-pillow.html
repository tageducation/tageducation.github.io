22. It is a rough surface to easily stick to your original nails. A. SHELLAC MANICURE B. VINYLUX MANICURE C. ACRYLIC MANICURE D. AMERICAN MANICURE


C. ACRYLIC MANICURE

A powder and a liquid are combined to make an acrylic nail paste, which is then applied with a brush to your natural nail. When the acrylic nail hardens, it forms a rough surface that is meant to stick to your natural nail and prevent the acrylic nail from coming off. Additionally, the acrylic nail's rough surface serves as a foundation for nail polish and other nail art to adhere to. As a result, the correct response is Acrylic Manicure, option C.

24. How do you manicure? What are the steps in doing manicure?​


What are the steps in doing manicure?

Step 1: Clean your nails with polish remover.

Step 2: Clip, file, and buff

Step 3: Push back your cuticles.

Step 4: Exfoliate your hands

Step 5: Moisturize your hands and cuticles.

Step 6: Apply a base coat.

Step 7: Apply your first coat of color.

Step 8: Apply your second coat of color.

Step 9: Finish with a topcoat

Step 10: Clean up rough edges and let your nails dry
