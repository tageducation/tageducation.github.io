17. DEMONSTRATING WAYS TO BUILD AND KEEP SCHOOL AND COMMUNITY ENVIRONMENT HEALTHY Create poster that will promote safety and health in your community. (10pts) a predisp (May​


Explanation:

Here are some ideas for promoting safety and health in your community through a poster:

Encourage regular hand washing: One of the simplest and most effective ways to prevent the spread of illness is to wash your hands frequently with soap and water. You can include a graphic on your poster illustrating the proper way to wash your hands.

Promote the use of masks: Wearing a mask can help reduce the spread of respiratory infections, such as COVID-19. You can include a graphic on your poster showing people wearing masks in different settings (e.g., at school, at the grocery store).

Encourage physical activity: Regular physical activity is important for both physical and mental health. You can include a graphic on your poster showing people participating in different types of physical activity, such as walking, running, or playing sports.

Promote healthy eating habits: Eating a balanced diet can help support overall health and well-being. You can include a graphic on your poster showing a variety of healthy foods (e.g., fruits, vegetables, whole grains).

Encourage mental health support: It is important for people to seek support if they are experiencing mental health challenges. You can include a graphic on your poster illustrating different ways to seek support (e.g., talking to a trusted friend or family member, seeking help from a mental health professional).

Promote safety practices: You can include graphics on your poster illustrating safety practices that people should follow to reduce the risk of accidents or injuries (e.g., wearing a seatbelt when driving, wearing a helmet when biking).

Encourage respect for others: Building a healthy community involves showing respect for others. You can include a graphic on your poster illustrating ways to show respect (e.g., using kind words, listening to others).

By including these and other messages on your poster, you can help promote a safe and healthy environment for your school and community.
