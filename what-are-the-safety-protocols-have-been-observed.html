11. What are the safety protocols have been observed in cheerdance


Safety protocols to be observed in Cheerdance  

Cheerdance or sometimes refer to cheerleading is a type of sports that has numbers of stunts, pyramid formations, dances and yells to cheer respective teams. It involves lots of physical activities and movements that is why it is considered to be a competition and sports.

Here are some safety protocols to be followed by team members in Cheerdancing: Be in good body shape in all times of the season.  Perform warm up and stretching before, during and after practices and competition. Pyramids must be formed according to the set restrictions to minimize injuries on the members.  Always practice and perform on the floor, courts and venues with landing mats or foam floors.  Always find a qualified coach. A certification must be needed for cheerdance coaches. Always wear the proper and prescribed cheerdance uniform to avoid accidents and injuries.  Do not attempt stunts that are too dangerous.
