11. 2. A tennis ball 0.314 kg, is accelerated at a rate of 164 m/s when hit by a tennis player. Whatforce does the player tennis racket exert on the ball? Show your complete solution. (5pts).​


Force = (mass) x (acceleration)

= (0.314 kg) x (164 m/s²)

= 51.5 newtons

(about 11.6 pounds).

Notice that the ball is only accelerating while it's in contact with the racket. The instant the ball loses contact with the racket, it stops accelerating, and sails off in a straight line at whatever speed it had when it left the strings.

Answer:

A knockout competition with 64 players typically involves 6 rounds of play to reach the grand finals.

In a knockout competition, each round consists of a series of matches in which the loser is eliminated and the winner advances to the next round. The number of matches in each round is determined by the number of players remaining in the competition. For example, in the first round of a knockout competition with 64 players, there would be 32 matches, with the winners advancing to the second round.

To show Filipino values in this kind of situation, you could try to exhibit qualities such as respect, sportsmanship, and fair play. This might involve congratulating your opponents on a good match, shaking hands with them after the match, and following the rules of the game. You could also try to be a good role model for other players and spectators by demonstrating good behavior on and off the court.
