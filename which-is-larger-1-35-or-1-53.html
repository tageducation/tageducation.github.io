4. the sum of two numbers is 53 . their difference id 23 . find the larger number


Answer: The larger number is 38


Step-by-step explanation:

Let x = 1st number

y = 2nd number


We have,

Equation 1: x + y = 53

Equation 2: x - y = 23


Solve for x:

Substitute y from Equation 2 using Equation 1

x - (53 - x) = 23

x - 53 + x = 23

2x -53 = 23

2x = 76

x = 38


Solve for y:

Substitute x

38 + y = 53

38 - y = 23


Subtract both Equation:

2y = 30

y = 15


Check the solution:

Substitute values

Equation 1: 38 + 15 = 53

                  53 = 53 (True)


Equation 2: 38 - 15 = 23

                    23 = 23 (True)

Answer:

38

Step-by-step explanation:

Here are our equations:

x + y = 53

x - y = 23

We can add equation 1 and 2 to get:

2x = 76

x = 38

Then we can solve for y:

38 + y = 53

y = 15

The larger of the 2 numbers is 38.
