1. what is the Characteristics of Wax chalk.​


Answer:

Marks made with our Wax Chalk adhere very well to the material. The Chalk is made especially for coarse and woolen fabrics. Because the marks are difficult to remove, fabrics should be marked on the reverse side. Made from special high quality Waxes.
