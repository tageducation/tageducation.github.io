13. feee pionnn what do you call a female deer and what do you call a male rabbit​


Answer:

for female deer it is a doe,cow or hind

for male rabbit it is a buck

What is a Female Deer Called?Doe is the most common name used for adult female deer. This term is used for smaller deer species, and it’s a term that’s also used for females of other species (rabbits and kangaroos are examples).Hind is the name used for females of some larger species, and is typically the counterpart of the word “Stag” which is used for male Red and Sika Deer (among other species).Cow is the name of females of large deer species such as the Moose, Elk (Wapiti), and Caribou or Reindeer. The male counterpart of a female called a cow is a bull.

What is a Male Rabbit Called?Rabbits live in social groups, and they often dig burrows underground. A system of rabbit burrows is called a warren. A male rabbit is called a buck and a baby rabbit is called a kitten or kit. Although some rabbits are small, they are not rodents.

Step-by-step explanation:

The number of a fraction on the top of the line is called numerator.

2.)Denominator - shows how many equal parts the item is divided into.

3.)Like Fractions - Fractions with the same denominators are called like fractions.

4.)Unlike Fractions - Fractions with different denominators are called the unlike fractions.

5.Mixed fraction - is a combination of a whole number and a proper fraction.
