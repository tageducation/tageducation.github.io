Other Charts and Graphs

A GRAPH is a diagram that depicts the relationship between two variables, often measured along a pair of axes at right angles.

A TABLE, often known as a matrix, is a logical organization of numerical data in columns and rows.

A PICTOGRAM is a visual representation of a specific number of objects.

A LINEGRAPH depicts the evolution of numbers through time. They are used when you have linked data and want to display patterns, such as the average nighttime temperature in each month of the year.

A PIECHART depicts how a whole is split into components. You could wish to display how a budget was spent on various items in a given year, for example.

A BAR GRAPH is a graphical depiction of facts, quantities, or numbers that is made up of bars or strips. It is used to compare and contrast numerical values, frequencies, or other metrics of different types of data.
