28. I am thinking of a number, If I add three to it, multiply by 2, subtract 4 then divide by 7. Two is the number I ended up with. What was the number I first thought of?"


Answer:

6

Step-by-step explanation:

assume the number we are looking for is "x"

therefore

add three to it make it

x + 3

then, multiply by 2

(x + 3)*(2)

2(x + 3)

then, subtract 4

2(x + 3) - 4

then, divide by 7

[2(x + 3) - 4]/7

equals to 2

[2(x + 3) - 4]/7 = 2

compute for x

[2(x + 3) - 4]/7 = 2

2(x + 3) - 4 = 14

2[(x + 3) - 2] = 14

(x + 3) - 2 = 7

x + 3 = 9

x = 6

therefore, the number is 6
