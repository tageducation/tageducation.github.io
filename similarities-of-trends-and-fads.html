2. difference and similarities of trend and fad​


DIFFERENCE AND SIMILARITIES OF TREND AND FAD

SIMILARITIES

Fads and trends are perceptible changes in behaviors that can be seen or felt among a large section of the population with the behavior being followed with enthusiasm for some time. If the behavior of a person, whether it pertains to his attire, use of jewelry, tattoos, music, shoes, hairstyle, or just about anything that can be seen or heard by others is perceived as novel and interesting by others and catches on to be followed by a large section of the population, it can be a fad or a trend. There are people who use the words interchangeably as if they were synonymous.

DIFFERENCES

The difference is that trends have a much longer life span than fads. Trends can be in style for many years. They rise in popularity slowly. Fads come and go quickly. They are short lived.
