Transition Signals

Transition signals are essential part of academic writing. These are also called linking devices, discourse connectors or cohesive devices. These are words or phrases used in writing or speaking to help the reader understand the connection between ideas. They are important aspect of cohesion. Common problems encountered by students are overuse of transitions.
