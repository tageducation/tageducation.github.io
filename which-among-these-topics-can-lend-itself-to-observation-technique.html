19. In your own words, explain why does the payroll process lend itself to batch processing


Answer:

Payroll lends itself to batch processing because it is processed at fixed time intervals which permit some time lag. Processing the payroll file usually involves most employees each time it is processed, which is an efficient use of computer resources and can be accomplished with a single pass through the file.

They can look at ads and attend tradeshows about these hotels and make contact, of course, it goes both ways. I’m sure that the hotel management’s eagerness to promote their business, would do their part in approaching the travel agencies themselves. Then comes the meeting, this is where both parties will talk about placing ads like brochures, posters, and others to promote their businesses.

The agencies armed with discounts from their other clients could go to these hotels to see it for themselves. A real experience will allow them to weigh their options of whether or not to recommend the property to travelers who understandably do not want to be disappointed.

These are just two things that a travel agency can do to familiarize themselves with the hotel property industry.
