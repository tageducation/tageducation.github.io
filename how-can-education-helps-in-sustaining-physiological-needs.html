Physiological needs are the most basic needs of Maslow's hierarchy. These needs are also known as primary needs, such as food, drink, clothing and shelter. Physiological needs include eating, drinking, sleeping and breathing. In addition to meeting nutrition, physiological needs also include clothing, shelter, and warmth
