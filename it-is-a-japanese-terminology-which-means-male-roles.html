1. It is a Japanese terminology which means male role?


Answer

In Kabuki theatre, the terms Aragoto and Wagoto may refer to the male role of an actor. The difference is that an Aragoto actor plays in a strong, fierce, and rough style by doing exaggerated poses. While a Wagoto actor performs in a gentle, tender, romantic, and sometimes comedic style. There are actually three actor roles on a Kabuki play. These are the Onna-gata, Aragoto, and Wagoto. The Onna-gata role are male actors performing a female character in the play.

The term Kabuki is often mistaken for being associated with the meaning of the male role. Kabuki actually means off-balance or unconventional but was later interpreted as Ka - Song, bu - Dance, Ki - Skill.

8. A Japanese word which means protection.


PROTECTION means keeping someone safe from any harm or danger. It could be in a way of defense or care.


The word ’protection’ has different translations in Japanese. Here are some of it:


Mamori - protection or a defense from any harm normally with a charm that is called omamori. Omamori are amulets that can be found in both shrines and temples, that could give luck and protection to someone owning it.

Hogo - is also synonymous to protection, but it’s more of care and shelter

プロテクション (read as purotekushon) - same meaning in English, only borrowed English word formed in Katakana


Additional information: Each Japanese name has its meaning. The name Mamoru means ‘protector’ in Japanese.
