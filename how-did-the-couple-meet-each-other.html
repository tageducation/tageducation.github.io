How Couple’s Meet

There are various ways to meet someone. There would be instances that couple’s do meet through different places like:  

School Office Park Malls Supermarket Church Hospital Neighborhood

Special occasions like:

Birthday Parties School and Office reunions Wedding Baptism Valentine’s Day Christmas Burial

Some couple’s also meet with the help of their friends, common acquaintances or family.

There are a lot of ways that couple’s meet not only to the given examples above. In the older generation, most couple’s meet by being introduced by their parents or some were already destined or arranged to be a couple the moment they were born. People in the older generation were too much afraid to go against their parent’s decision for them and leave them with no freedom or choice to choose who they want to be with.

In our generation today, people have so much freedom to choose whoever they want to meet or be with. With the help of science, people can be connected all over the world. There are also a lot of dating sites online that you can join to, to be able to meet and be connected to someone else.  

In that way, it would be easier for people to look for someone that matches their interests and hobbies and both of them can start their conversation faster. It normally starts as friends, getting to know each other, go on a trip or a date. This would normally the chance for both individuals to share and understand if they both have the same interests or knowing the true character of the significant other. Once they have seen each other’s interests and both of them understands each other most of the time it will then lead to courtship. Courtship depends on the amount of time they would want it to happen. The result could either be they will remain as friends or become a couple.  

Not all couple’s meet with the help of other people or dating sites. Some meet by fate. Fate that none of them expected. For an example, in a bus full of strangers and suddenly a stranger would talk to you and you will then respond to the stranger and would continue on talking. It might not happen every day but it can happen. Once you start engaging yourself to the opposite sex or same sex and emotions would be involved, then you guys will have a high chance of liking each other of falling in love that would eventually make you a couple.  

There are also some people who are also willing to pay for a membership fee to a certain dating application to be able to meet someone.  

Every municipality here in the Philippines is also sponsoring events for single people that is normally called speed dating. Most of the time, admission is free for all the single people seeking to see and meet the potential love of their life. This event often happens during Valentine’s day.
