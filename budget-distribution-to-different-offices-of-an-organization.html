6. what are the difference between budgeting and budget​


Budgeting

is creating a plan to spend your money. Good budgeting is spending less than you are earning as you plan for your financial goals. ... Budgeting is the process of creating a plan to spend and invest your hard earned money wisely to meet your personal and financial goals in life.

Budget

is an estimation of revenue and expenses over a specified future period of time and is usually compiled and re-evaluated on a periodic basis. Budgets can be made for a person, a group of people, a business, a government, or just about anything else that makes and spends money.

Budget distribution to different offices of an organization can be showed by Pie Chart.

What are pie charts

In general, a pie chart is a circular graph that is divided into sections to illustrate percentage proportions. Pie charts have geometric center points that can be drawn to mark different areas with different proportions. The larger the portion size, the larger the portion of the quantity represented. In addition, with the shape of a circle and the division of portions in it, pie charts are also easily adapted into visual metaphors and artistic exploration.

When are pie charts used

Pie charts are used to show component composition or show the parts of a categorical entity. The pie chart has a circle shape that builds a metaphor for a unit or total value, so the percentage of a pie chart must represent 100%. Pie charts should also be used for data visualization that has simple data sets with one variable consisting of several indicators. In addition, numerically the data must be percentageable.

For example, in one company there are three sales divisions based on the products sold. The directors want to see the proportion of each division's contribution to the company's total revenue, so a pie chart is the right choice to visualize sales data. But we have to make sure first that the total of all the proportions represented by the three divisions is 100% of the product sales value. However, Pie charts cannot be used to show variable growth over time. In the context of the example above, the sales growth of each division from month to month within one year. Presentation of data like this should use a bar chart.
