What is modular learning?

• The term "modular learning" refers to the usage of studied modules that allow students to learn on their own. Modular learning is a type of far distance learning that employs Self-Learning Modules (SLM) that are based on the most needed learning skills (MELCS) produced by teachers with the assistance of curriculum developers.

• What is modular distance getting to know, and the way does it work? It is a form of getting to know delivery modality establish through the Department of Education (DepEd) wherein instructors and students do now no longer have interaction face-to-face.

Purpose of modular learning

• The modular method of education allows the student to take charge of his or her own learning and assume greater responsibility for it. It necessitates higher maturity on the side of the learner, and the courses are better suited to older pupils.

• Modular instruction is a type of alternative academic contour that uses custom-designed academic assets tailored to the desires of pupils. Students are endorsed to pay attention to a whole lot of captivating and demanding task if they want to maintain focus and attention, promoting independent study.
