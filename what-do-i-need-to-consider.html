7. What health career will I choose based on my skill and interest?what do I need consider


If you are looking for a heath career that suits your skills and interest, you can base it on the following list of health professions. You can just choose which one suits you best.

1. Mental Care Practitioner- a health worker who offers services for the purpose of improving the mental health of individuals or treating mental illness. Includes psychiatrists, clinical psychologist, clinical social worker, counselors.

2. Maternal and newborn health practitioner - who deals with the care of women and their children before, during and after pregnancy and childbirth. Includes obstetricians, midwives, obstetrical nurses and many others.

3. Geriatric care practitioner - plans and care of the elderly and/or disabled to promote their health, improve their quality of life and maintain their independence for as long as possible. Includes geriaticians, clinical nurse, geriatric aides.

4. Surgical practitioner- a healthcare professional who specializes in the planning and delivery of a patient's perioperative care. Includes surgeon, anaesthetic, surgical assistant.

5. Rehabilitation care practitioner - is a health worker who provides care and treatment which aims to enhance and restore functional ability and quality of life to those with physical impairments or disabilities.

6. Eye care practitioner - care and treatment for the eye and the adnexa may be delivered by the ophthalmologists specializing in surgical/medical care, or optometrist

7. Medical diagnosis providers - responsible for the process of determining which disease or condition explains a person's symptoms and signs. Includes radiographers, radiologists, medical laboratory scientists, pathologist.

8. Oral care practitioner - provides care and treatment to promote and restore oral health. Includes dental surgeons, dental assistants, dental auxiliaries and many more

13. What do i need to consider in health career brainly?


Answer:

Your Motivation to Pursue a Health Care Career.

How Are You Going to Get Education and Training for Your Career.

Skills and Strengths a Fit for the Health Care Industry.

Going into the field of healthcare is a serious decision. There are various motivations like money, title, sense of purpose, passion to help people, etc. At the same time, professionals in that field need a certain skill set not only in critical thinking but also in communication abilities.

There are multiple factors to consider, so you can ask yourself these questions:

What motivates me to work in the field of healthcare? If the core of your Why does not match the passion you have for this career, all else will fall through. How good is my study ethic? Getting that dream job does not happen in an instant, especially in this field - which would take about 10-15 years before you become a doctor with a specialization. If you do not have the grit and resilience to study medicine, you might as well choose another path. Note: because it will take years, think of the costs as well. How good is my work ethic? Another thing to take note of is the irregularity of work hours that professionals in healthcare experience. This is relevant now - when the Covid-19 pandemic is highly affecting nations. Healthcare professionals go out to work to treat people while risking their lives. Do I have the skill set to become a healthcare professional? Every healthcare career demands a high level of skill set and personality traits. We must always remember that treating patients is not just diagnosing the illness and solving a puzzle (which would require critical thinking skills) but also interacting with patients, getting them to trust you enough to tell the truth about what happened to them, and being assured that they will be taken care of (which would require interpersonal skills). What specific field in healthcare do I want to pursue? There are different specialties that a healthcare professional has because there are various fields in medicine. If you want to treat kids, you can be a pediatrician. If you have great precision and stable hands, perhaps you could start exploring surgery. The skill set and interests you have should match the type of field you want to pursue in healthcare.
