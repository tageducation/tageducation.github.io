16. tagapagtaguyod ng Suffragist Movement


The Asociacion Feminista Ilonga (a women group-suffragist movement) was created in 1906 and Asociacion Feminista Filipina (Feminist Association of the Philippines) was founded in 1905  by Pura Villanueva Kalaw.

Pura Villanueva Kalaw was a women's rights pioneer, with Concepcion Felix Rodriguez along with 12 elite women. The organizations were built with an objective, and that is to touch upon socio-civic matters, some of which were: prison reform, improving the education system and healthcare and labor reforms.
