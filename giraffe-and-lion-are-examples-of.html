17. name and body parts used by the following animals in adapting to their environment example giraffe​


Answer:

Giraffe:

Name: Giraffe (Giraffa camelopardalis)

Body Parts Used in Adapting to Environment:

Neck: The giraffe's long neck allows it to reach high branches for food, which is especially important in times of drought when food is scarce.

Tongue: The giraffe's tongue is long and flexible, allowing it to grasp and pluck leaves and fruits with ease.

Legs: The giraffe's long legs enable it to run at speeds of up to 60 km/h, allowing it to escape predators quickly.

Hooves: The giraffe's hooves are strong and broad, providing stability and balance while browsing on tall trees.

Heart: The giraffe's heart is large and powerful, allowing it to pump blood up its long neck to its brain.

These body parts allow the giraffe to adapt to its environment by allowing it to forage for food in tall trees, escape predators quickly, and maintain balance and stability while browsing.
