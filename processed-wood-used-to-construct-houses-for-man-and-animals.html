15. 3. What do you call of the processed wood which is used to construct houses for man andanimals?a. lumber b. woodc. turpentined. rayonsa mga gustong sumagot follow me and ill follow you​


A. Lumber

Lumber is a type of processed wood used to build houses for humans and animals, cabinets, and other structures.

Lumber also referred to as timber, is wood that has been transformed into beams and planks as part of the production of wood. Lumber is primarily used for framing and finishing in construction (floors, wall panels, window frames). Lumber has many uses outside of home construction.

Lumber may be supplied rough-sawn or with one or more of its faces surfaced. In addition to pulpwood, rough lumber is used to manufacture furniture and other items requiring cutting and shaping. Due to its low cost, it is available in numerous species, including hardwoods and softwoods such as white pine and red pine.

Finished lumber is supplied in standard sizes, primarily for the construction industry – primarily softwood, from coniferous species such as pine, fir, and spruce (collectively spruce-pine-fir), cedar, and hemlock, but also some hardwood, for premium flooring. It is more commonly made from softwood than hardwood, and softwood accounts for 80% of all lumber.
