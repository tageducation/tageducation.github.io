1. What would Happen if driver do not use seat belt?​


Driver who do not use seat belts are more at risk of being hit hard in the event of an accident.

When driving the driver must be careful and also secure himself by using a seat belt. Function: When wearing a seat belt, the body will remain suspended in the car seat when an accident occurs. Thus, automatically the position of the head will also be restrained, so that the risk of hitting the head on the steering wheel or other parts can be reduced.

About safety by seat belt

As a safety feature in cars, seat belts must always be worn when driving. Both as driver and passenger.

Some cars have embedded notification features or notifications for using seatbelts, but in fact there are still many who are indifferent and don't use seatbelts properly.

Not only that, seat belts are often only fastened when they meet the police so they don't get ticketed. In fact, seat belts are very important.

Benefits of seat belts

Here are three benefits of always using a seat belt while in the car,

Maintain Driver Safety

The first benefit of a seat belt is to keep the driver safe while driving. Seat belts have the benefit of keeping the body from being thrown forward in the event of an accident.

Maintain Focus While Driving

Maintaining focus and sitting position is one of the reasons seat belts must always be used. A sitting position like this can also maintain optimal body condition during the trip.

10. 3 salient points of seat belt use act


Answer:

RA 8750 or The Seat Belt Act

Explanation:

RA 8750 or the Seat Belt Act mandates that all drivers and passengers both in front and at the back should be strapped with a seat belt when the engine is running on any given road. This law is clear and excuses no one.

The salient points are the following:

Everyone is required to wear seat belts, including the passengers at the back. This is to ensure the safety of all the occupants of the vehicle. This is not a regulation just for the sake of having rules but it is required so that everyone will be safe.Children are not allowed in front passenger seat. Even if they are strapped, they are not allowed. Babies at the back, should be strapped and put in a baby carrier. It would be otherwise illegal. RA 8750's age limit was six years old. A recent law changed this to all children below 12 years old.Car manufacturers are required to install seat belts prior to distributing and selling any vehicles.

Seat belts are also required in buses for front row passengers. Most new buses now are also fitted with lap belts for all passengers which complies with the law as well.

Always remember that most deaths occur because passengers and drivers refuse to wear seat belts. For your own, buckle up for safety--it's the law.

28. why do seat belts help protect stops quickly?​


Answer:

A seatbelt helps to prevent injury in the event of a car crash by reducing the velocity of a body as it experiences a sudden decrease in speed. Due to the body's inertia, which is its 'resistance to a change in speed or direction of travel', a passenger in a vehicle will want to continue traveling forwards once the car has reached a sudden stop.
