21. What is the meaning of “ I am not beautiful like you, I am beautiful like me. ”​


Answer:

The phrase "I am not beautiful like you, I am beautiful like me" means that the person speaking does not compare their own beauty to someone else's. Instead, they believe that they are beautiful in their own unique way. This can be a powerful message of self-acceptance and self-love. It can also be a way of rejecting the idea that there is only one standard of beauty that everyone must conform to. Beauty is subjective, and what makes one person beautiful may not be the same for someone else. It is important to embrace and celebrate our own unique qualities and characteristics.
