Which of the following is not a principle of the Cell Theory?

The fact that very few cells replicate is not a premise of cell theory. This is one of the functions of cells. They will continue to reproduce indefinitely. Actually, it is critical that they reproduce because if they do not, the various tissues and organs will struggle to survive.

It is necessary for them to multiply often in order to ensure that fresh cells are always accessible and that the body remains healthy. When difficulties arise with the body's various cells, complications may arise. People's immune systems, for example, are harmed.

The three fundamental principles of cell theory are as follows:

1. Every cell is made up of pre-existing cells.

2. One or more cells make up every living creature on the planet.

3. Life's basic, functional, and structural unit is the cell.
