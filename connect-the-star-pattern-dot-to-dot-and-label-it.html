6. Activity 1.3 CONNECT THE DOTS Connect the step of scientific method to the exact investigation activity by connecting the dotspa help plss​


Answer:

Making observation - Observing that plants grow best under the sun.

Asking question - Raising questions if the sun is a factor in the growth of plants.

Making Hypothesis - If plants are placed under the sun, they will grow fast.

Hypothesis Testing - Putting up two set-ups for the experiment.

Analyzing and Interpreting Data - Keeping record of plant growth daily.

Drawing Conclusion - Basing on observation, it was found out that plants grow best under the sun.

Communicating Results - Making a graph showing growth of the plants.

Explanation:

The scientific method is a logical step-by-step process that answers questions or tries to find a solution to a problem. The question may arise from making observations of the things around and asking questions about this observation. Out of the question, we come up with a tentative answer to the question known as the hypothesis. To test the hypothesis, one conducts an experiment. Through the experiment, we get to see how the independent variable affects the dependent variable while all other variables are kept the same. Results of the experiment are collected and analyzed. The analysis leads to the answer to the question which is the conclusion. Lastly, the results of the experiment are shared so that others would know.
