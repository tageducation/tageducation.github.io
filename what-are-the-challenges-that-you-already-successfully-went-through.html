Challenges are stones that we have to step on and overcome for us to be better persons and to further prepare us for bigger roles in the future.  They serve as learning mechanisms for us to grow.  In order to overcome them, we have to learn to face them courageously first.  Fear should not limit our ability to be victorious over them.

Facing our challenges can never be tough enough once we get to it.  It may seem that we are finally overcoming them until we find ourselves sulking over the sufferings and trials once again.  Overcoming is not possible if we do not break and hurt and lose for a while.  We overcome by constantly moving forward even if the steps are hard to see and grasp.

We can never overcome our challenges if we let them break us and never bother to stand up once again.  It is in bravery and perseverance that we become victorious.  We do not give up, but we learn when to pause and rest.  We fight but then we also choose the right battles to fight for.
