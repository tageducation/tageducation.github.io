7. John is 2 time as old as his friend Peter. Peter is 5 years older than Alice. In 5 years John will be three times as old as Alice. How old os peter now ? Please even just equation.​


Let 'x' be John's age
'y' be Peter's age
'z' be Alice's age
x = 2y ---equation 1
y = z + 5 ---equation 2
x +5 = 3(z + 5) ---equation 3
Substitute both equations 1 and 2 to 3
x + 5 = 3(z + 5)
2y + 5 = 3y
3y -2y = 5
y = 5

Therefore Peter is 5 years old

Identify:

Peter's age now

let,

(age now)

alice = x - 5peter = xjohn = 2x

(in 5yrs)

alice = x - 5 + 5

= x

peter = x + 5john = 2x + 5

Equation:

" In 5 years, John will be three times as old as alice. How old is peter now?"

2x + 5 = 3x

Solve:

2x + 5 = 3x

2x - 3x = -5

-x = -5

-1x/-1 = -5/-1

x = 5 <---- Peter's age

Since, x represents Peter's age now, Peter is 5 years of age now.

2x + 5 = 3x

2(5) + 5 = 3(5)

10 + 5 = 15
