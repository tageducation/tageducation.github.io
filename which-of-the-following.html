24. 1. Which of the following are corresponding angles?2. Which of the following are alternate interior angles?3. Which of the following are alternate exterior angles?4. Which of the following are same-side interior angles?​


ANGLES

corresponding angles: ∠1 and ∠5, ∠2 and ∠6, ∠3 and ∠7, ∠4 and ∠8 alternate interior angles: ∠3, ∠4, ∠5, ∠6alternate exterior angles: ∠1, ∠2, ∠7, ∠8same-side interior angles: ∠3 and ∠5, ∠4 and ∠6

FURTHER EXPLANATION

Types of Alternate Angles

The alternate angles are divided into two categories based on the positions of the angles, respectively

Alternate Interior Angles: The pair of angles on the inner side of the two parallel lines but on the other side of the transversal are known as alternate interior angles.Alternate Exterior Angles: The pair of angles on the outside of the two parallel lines but on the other side of the transversal are known as alternate exterior angles.

Corresponding Angles Examples and Types

Any angles that are generated on the other side of the transversal serve as examples of the matching angle. It is important to remember that the transversal might cross two parallel or two non-parallel lines. Thus, there are two types of equivalent angles:

Corresponding angles formed by parallel lines and transversalsCorresponding angles formed by non-parallel lines and transversals

Same Side Interior Angles

Same-side interior (within the parallel lines) and same-side external (outside the parallel lines) angles are created when a transversal intersects two parallel lines. Same-side angles are supplementary because the alternate interior and alternate exterior angles are congruent and because linear pairs of angles are supplementary.
