17. A. Did the paper's color change or stay the same?SPSCB. How is the side of the construction paper with sunblock lotion different from the side without sunblock lotion?C.How is the side of the construction paper with sundass different from theside without sunglass?D. Describe how sunscreen and sunglass protect us from UV radiation​


This experiment was conducted to determine the effect of sunglasses and sunscreen to protect an object from UV rays.

A. Did the papers color change or stay the same?

The color of the paper on the construction has changed. But changes only occur on the part of the paper that is not protected by sunscreen or glasses. When exposed to sunlight, the UV light breaks down the chemical bonds in the color of the paper, causing the color of the paper to fade.

B. How is the side of the construction paper with sunblock lotion different from the side without sunblock lotion?

The side without sunscreen will change and become very white, even in a short time of less than an hour. However, the design of the sunscreen will still be vibrant. The sun's UV rays bleach the paper, but the sunscreen agent protects the color, allowing the designs of the sunscreen to still be visible.

C. How is the side of the construction paper with sundass different from the side without sunglass?

Just like sunscreen, the part covered by UV protector glasses will protect the paper from UV rays so that the color on the paper does not change where the other side of the paper will experience a significant discoloration.

D. Describe how sunscreen and sunglass protect us from UV radiation?

Sunscreen is generally used by humans to protect the skin from sunlight during the day. This is based on the fact that sunscreens containing carbon sunscreens will form a thin protective film that absorbs UV radiation before it penetrates the skin. Each sunscreen has an SPF number, which is the Sun Protection Factor, which shows how well it protects the skin from UVB rays.

While some types of glasses can also protect us from UV rays. Anti-UV glasses lenses are lenses that are equipped with a special coating that can block up to 99% of exposure to UV radiation from the sun. By using an anti-UV coating on your eyeglass lenses, you no longer need to wear dark glasses to protect yourself from the sun's rays.
