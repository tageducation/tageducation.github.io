16. What is the best-case scenario for employees to ensure the security standards in their virtual workspaces are the same as the physical office?


Several ways that can be done to ensure the security standards in their virtual workspaces for employee are 

1. Creating a security standard operating procedure (SOP)

2. Creating access and authorization schemes

3. Designing crisis management

Creating a security standard operating procedure (SOP)

The beginning of all company data protection efforts is the existence of ground rules for employees who work from home. Rules such as what equipment is allowed to be used, how to secure the network, etc. must be made in detail.

Creating access and authorization schemes

Each employee has different duties and responsibilities. Companies can take steps to assign different access and authorizations to each employee. Make sure employees only get access to what they need to do, but still give them access to the resources they need.

Designing crisis management

With various preventive measures for data security as mentioned above, companies must still think that there is still a chance of conceding. Therefore, it is necessary to create a crisis management plan. Make sure who will lead the team in solving the problem, who should do digital forensics to find the entry point of the attack and so on. The existence of a crisis management design is expected to make companies act more quickly and limit losses or damages that occur.

22. what are the 5 national standards for physical education?​


Answer:

Standard 1: The physically literate individual demonstrates competency in a variety of motor skills and movement patterns.

Standard 2: The physically literate individual applies knowledge of concepts, principles, strategies and tactics related to movement and performance.

Standard 3: The physically literate individual demonstrates the knowledge and skills to achieve and maintain a health-enhancing level of physical activity and fitness.

Standard 4: The physically literate individual exhibits responsible personal and social behavior that respects self and others.

Standard 5: The physically literate individual recognizes the value of physical activity for health, enjoyment, challenge, self-expression and/or social interaction.
