To put the bird in play. (badminton)

To put the bird in play refers to serving the shuttlecock in order to start the game. The term “bird” was used in reference to the feathers that occupy the bottom of “shuttlecock”. By putting the bird in play is equivalent to releasing the bird or letting it fly which actually means the start of the game by first hitting the shuttlecock.  

So “to put the bird in play” simply means to serve or to start the game.

Badminton is a racket sport that is played on a court partitioned by a net five feet high.  The game is played with a shuttlecock ("flying creature"). Can be played as singles or pairs. The object of the game is to hit the shuttlecock over the net so that in the end the adversary can't restore the shot.  Badminton started in India in the mid-1800's. Its ubiquity expanded when English armed force officials positioned in India shipped the game to England.  In England it was designated "badminton". Eventually it spread to the United States.  And to put the bird in a play means to serve the bird.

12. The shot in which the bird follows the net in a short flight. (badminton)


WHAT IS ASKED?

Identify the shot in badminton in which the bird follows the net  in a short flight.

ANSWER

The shot in badminton in which the bird, in a short flight follows the net is the Net Flight. Therefore, the answer is Net Flight.

OTHER SHOTS IN BADMINTON

Drop shot

Any shot that drops immediately after crossing the net is a drop shot. The  overhand is the easiest stroke to use.

Net shot

Any shot that drops immediately after crossing the net is a net shot. The  overhand is the easiest stroke to use.

Drive

To have the opponent run from  side to side, the shot is kept as low as possible is identified as a drive shot.

Smash

This is the basic offensive shot in badminton. It is a very powerful stroke  if the purpose is to get the shuttle to land on your opponent’s  side of the court as quickly as possible.

As we age, the synovial fluid in our joints which prevent friction of connecting bones becomes less making as prone to arthritis. We will be having problem with mobility when we have this condition.
