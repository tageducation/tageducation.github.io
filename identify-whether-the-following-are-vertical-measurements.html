Explanation:

Horizontal measurements refers to measuring that initial

value starts from the left side and it continues going to the right up to

the endpoint.

Vertical Measurements refers to the measurement of an object or

parts of the body which the initial and ending value can be

found either Top or Bottom part of the subject.

Circumferential refers to a measurement that has an enclosing

connection of start and end value that can be used in around

or curved objects.
