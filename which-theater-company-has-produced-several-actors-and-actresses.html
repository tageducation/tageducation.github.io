1. which theater company has produced a number of actors and actresses


Repertory Philippines

The Repertory Philippines was established in 1967 headed by its theater director, Zenaida Amador. She founded Repertory Philippines to fulfill her dream of bringing the best of Broadway and London's West End to Filipino audiences.

Together with actress Baby Barredo, Amador established Repertory Philippines, a company that not only staged English-language plays and musicals year-round but trained actors and actresses as well.
