13. people who lead sedentary lifestyles are often referred to as a. couch potatoes b. athletes c. physically fit d. low health-risk people


Sedentary Lifestyle

This is a kind of a lifestyle that involves minimal or no physical activity at all. Normally a person who is always sitting or lying down while doing an activity for an example, watching television, playing video games, reading or even using a mobile phone or computer for the whole day would be called a sedentary lifestyle.

So, regarding the question:

People who lead sedentary lifestyles are often referred to as:

a. couch potatoes b. athletes c. physically fit d. low health-risk people

My answer would be A – Couch potatoes

Reason for choosing that answer would be the following:

Athletes

These are people who is proficient in sports and also all forms of physical exercise like jogging, swimming, dancing, running, etc. Normally, these kind of people also are determined to be able to reach a certain goal.

Physically Fit

Having a sedentary lifestyle would not be a healthy lifestyle for people like us. Often times having a sedentary lifestyle would lead you to a certain diseases like cardiovascular disease, diabetes and obesity. Along with these diseases it increases the risks for getting cancer, high blood pressure, osteoporosis, depression and lipid disorders.

Low health risk-people

Being on a sedentary lifestyle increases the risk for getting sick as I have mentioned above. It attacks our immune system and our body requires exercise to be able to increase our immune system that prevents us from getting sick.

Meanwhile, couch potato is an informal word that somehow describes a person that doesn’t do anything or spends little or no time exercising. Normally, a lot of people would use it as an expression to people who has a great deal of time just by watching television.

Not only by watching television but people who also loves to kill their time by just lying down all day. It also means that you are an “idler” or “layabout”. In simpler words, if an individual is lazy they are being called as couch potato.

If you wonder where the couch potato word come from, it came from Tom Lacino of Pasadena, California. He coined the term by the year of mid-70s. The “potato” comes from the the term “boob tube” in which is it a slang for television.  

If you stay on being a couch potato, it increases the feeling of being sad. People who do exercise at least one to two hours per week have a greater chance of lowering the risk for depression comparing it to those who are on a sedentary lifestyle.  

If you are on a sedentary lifestyle, you will definitely have a lot of hours of inactivity that would leave you to gain more weight. No exercise at all or any physical activity that can let your body perspire can weaken your body and once it has starting to get weak, the higher chances that you will get acquire different kinds of diseases. Your body would also be easily invaded by bacteria or viruses that can be dangerous.
