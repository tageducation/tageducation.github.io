This question probably refers to what is the "a place offering favorable opportunities​".

A place offering favorable opportunities is a definition of HAVEN.

EXPLANATION:

Haven is a place to rest for a while to release fatigue, or boredom. Toilet during long distance travel. These resting places are often found on toll roads or on national roads where long-distance drivers rest. There are also many restaurants on primary arterial roads that function as rest areas. These restaurants are mostly used by long-distance truck drivers or intercity buses to rest.

In the laws and regulations concerning Road Traffic and Transportation there is a provision which states that every time you drive a vehicle for 4 hours you must rest for at least half an hour. This is to relieve fatigue, take a nap or drink coffee, eat or go to the restroom/toilet. To improve traffic safety from vehicles passing through the haven with vehicles going in and out of the rest area, it must be planned in such a way.

So that conflicts can be minimized, especially at the haven which is placed on one side of a two-way road because there will be cross conflicts for vehicles that cut the entrance to the rest area. This situation is a big problem on national arterial roads where the traffic flow is already high but there is no median. On toll roads, rest areas are equipped with acceleration lanes and deceleration lanes so that vehicles entering or leaving the rest area can adjust their speed to the accelerating or decelerating lane. slowdown.
