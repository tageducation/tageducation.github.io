Answer:

QUESTION

Which statement is not true of all electromagnetic waves?

B. They cannot travel in a vacuum.

ANSWER

Yes indeed, the statement that is not true about electromagnetic waves, is letter B. They cannot travel in a vacum.

Electromagnetic waves can actually travel through vacuum of space or without a medium unlike mechanical waves, some examples of electromagnetic waves are light, radio waves, gamma radiation, ultraviolet radiation, infrared and many more.

These waves can travel even without a medium like air, mechanical waves like sound and seismic waves on the other hand cannot travel through vacuum or without a medium.
