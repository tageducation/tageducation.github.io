21. What factor affects the poultry meatâs tenderness and juiciness?a. ageb. cookery c. chicken cutsd. sex​


The correct answer is a. Age is a factor that affects the poultry meat's tenderness and juiciness. As poultry meat ages, its muscles break down and become more tender, which can improve its tenderness and juiciness. In contrast, cooking, chicken cuts, and sex do not have a significant effect on the attachment and juiciness of poultry meat.

Cookery is cooking poultry meat, such as grilling, roasting, or frying. While the cookery method can affect the flavor and texture of the essence, it does not significantly affect its tenderness and juiciness.

Chicken cuts refer to the different parts of the chicken that are used for meat, such as the breast, leg, or wing. While chicken cuts can affect the flavor and texture of the essence, they do not significantly affect its tenderness and juiciness.

Sex refers to the gender of the poultry. While the sex of the poultry can affect the flavor and texture of the meat, it does not significantly affect its tenderness and juiciness.
