The first step in making an interesting speech script is to determine the topic, in determining this topic it is very helpful for the orator to master the material, find supporting material, add illustrations, and include evidence to strengthen the reason.

Selecting a topic is a step taken to give an impression and reaction to the audience or listeners. The selected topic usually contains an issue or theme of the event.
