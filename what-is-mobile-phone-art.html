• Computer generated images (CGI)

The use of computer graphics in the production of pictures for works of art, print media, videogames, television shows, advertising, and videos.Visual scenes can be two dimensional and either dynamic or static (2d)A term used to describe scenes or special effects produced with 3d computer graphics for movies and television.Used in international motion pictures like as avatar, pirates of the caribbean, the incredible hulk, and others.
