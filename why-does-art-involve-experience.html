Art involve Experience

Other than the creativity and imagination capabilities of an individual, in order for you to actually maximize your talent and skill and with the thought of sharing it with others Experience is important.

Art is about the creativity of a person, his or her ability to create something beautiful a masterpiece that is also known within the areas of art. Fully expressing yourself by also using your limits as an advantage to create art. Though the word art is quite a vague word it includes:

Performing- here includes dancing, music, acting/theater Visual- sculpting, painting, drawing, architecture, photography Literary-includes poems and dramas

Each and one of these branches is considered Art in variations; they are learned and taught in schools. It may be in forms of entertainment or in forms of material expression like painting and sculpting. Every Art that is performed or done is from something they experienced or learned.  

Experience is necessary for an Artist since this will his fuel to create Art. Like if a person is acting, in order to fully grasp the character himself he needs a background or he needs to have experience in that particular field to express it in a perfect way. Or a painter or a sculptor, they can’t fully do the taught brush strokes or the very idea of sculpting if not for gaining experience or they won’t be able to fully express themselves because they have limited understanding and experience on that field. It may either in the real-life or they were taught about those essentials. Art can be delicate, it can be rough but overall it is something not anyone can do. Though creativity and imagination are in all of us not much of the population will or somehow pursue Arts as the main category of their decisions.  

Other learning about Art is that it comes in many styles and compliance with our vast living society. Although there isn’t that much limit in using or creating Art we are sometimes forced to comply with the normal related standards the society is giving us.  

Examples:

Freedom in music- music enables the composer or singer to involve whatever he wants to say or convey as long as it is within the guidelines of fair use and completely friendly lyrics and it won’t run through sensitive stuff. Painting- painting is the very expression of an Artist, his emotion, his life experience, even his thoughts can be put into a visual presentation of colors and figures. But to some closed-minded people, having free reign to paint anything even bodies should be censored and not displayed to public places.

Art can be many things small or big, colorful or not as long as it conveys something or it can be used as a tool to express human emotions and be shared with the other people then it is Art whatever the others say.
