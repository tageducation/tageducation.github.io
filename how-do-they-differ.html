30. How do different cultures view moral differently?​


Different cultures have different perspectives and values that shape how they view morality. Some cultures, for example, place a strong emphasis on individual freedom and autonomy, whereas others prioritize community well-being over individual rights. Furthermore, cultural beliefs, religious traditions, and historical experiences can all influence moral perspectives. As a result, even on universal ethical issues like honesty, fairness, and responsibility, what is considered moral or immoral varies greatly across cultures.
