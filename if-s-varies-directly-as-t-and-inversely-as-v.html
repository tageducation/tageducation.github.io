17. if s varies directly as t and inversely as v, then which of the following equations can describes the relation among the three variables s,t,and v?


DIRECT PROPORTION AND INVERSE PROPORTION

The relation among the three variables s, t, and v is s = kt/v.

FURTHER EXPLANATION

You can use a proportional symbol, a direct or inverse proportion, or both to represent the relationship between two quantities.

Signs of Direct and Inverse Proportion

X ∝ Y  --- This is how directly proportional symbol denotesX ∝ 1/Y  --- This is how inversely proportional symbol denotes

We use the terms "X is Directly Proportional to Y" or "Y is Directly Proportional to X" to describe two quantities when they are directly proportional to one another. We use the terms "X is Inversely Proportional to Y" or "Y is Inversely Proportional to X" to describe two quantities when they are inversely proportional to one another.

Specifications of direct and indirect proportions

Direct Proportion

The other quantity also rises when the first one does.The other quantity also declines when one quality does.The corresponding ratios are always fixed.

If two quantities a and b change together, they are said to be in direct proportion. In other words, their respective values' ratio does not change. This implies that

a/b = k

It is argued that the amounts a and b vary directly when k is a positive number.

If the values of b1, b2, and b in this situation correspond to a_1, a_2, and a, respectively, then it becomes;

a_1/b_1 = a_2 /b_2

Indirect Proportion

The other amount also lowers when one quantity does.When one amount falls, the other one rises as well.Always, the inversely varying ratios are present.

It is claimed that a and b have an inverse relationship. Quantity a will go down with an increase in quantity a, and quantity b will go up with a drop. To put it another way, the sum of their corresponding values ought to be constant. It is also referred to as inverse variation at times.

In other words, a and b are said to vary inversely if

ab = k

If b1 and b2 are the values of b in this situation that correspond to a1, a2 of a, then

a_1 · b_1 = a_2·b_2 or a_1/a_2 = b_2 /b_1

GIVEN QUESTION

if s varies directly as t and inversely as v, then which of the following equations can describe the relation among the three variables s,t, and v?

Solution

s varies directly as t, then

s ∝ t

In other words, their respective values' ratio does not change. This implies that

s = kt

s varies inversely as v, then

s ∝ 1/v

In other words, to vary inversely if

s = k/v

Therefore, equations can describe the relation among the three variables s,t, and v is

s = kt/v
