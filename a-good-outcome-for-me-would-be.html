This passage describes a person who is feeling trapped and panicked as they are running through a crowded square. They have a sense of urgency because they are trying to save something precious, possibly a person or a situation. The person is aware that they are not running fast enough, and the passage uses the metaphor of a clock ticking to emphasize the sense of time running out. The person also mentions that they are surrounded by dangerous enemies. They are aware that they are probably too late and that they are going to die. The person is also willing to die, because the person or situation that they were trying to save is more important than their own life. The passage also mentions Alice and the sunlight which could indicate that the person is trying to save Alice from the dangerous situation.
