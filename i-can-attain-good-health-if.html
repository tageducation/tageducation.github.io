1. i can attain good health if?


You can attain good health if you practice good eating habit, exercising regularly and maintaining a healthy lifestyle in general.

A well-balanced diet consisting of fruits, vegetables, proteins, and carbohydrates is recommended. Junk foods and processed foods should be avoided.

Cardio exercises and strength training are also important to keep your body fit. Generally, you should also aim to keep an active lifestyle and move throughout the day.

You should also commit to a healthy lifestyle by not smoking and limiting your intake of alcohol as well as getting 6-8 hours of sleep every night.
