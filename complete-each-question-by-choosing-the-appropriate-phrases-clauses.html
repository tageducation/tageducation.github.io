PHRASES

Verb Phrase: walking quickly to the mall

Prepositional Phrase: on the chair

Infinitive Phrase: to play outside

Gerund Phrase: travelling around the world

CLAUSES

Explanation:

PHRASES

Noun Phrase in a sentence: Zizi is reading books about animals.

Verb Phrase in a sentence: She was walking quickly to the mall.

Infinitive Phrase in a sentence: Uzaki wants to play outside.

Gerund Phrase: Travelling around the world is a good way to expand your worldview.

CLAUSE

Dependent Clause in a sentence: When the president arrives, please greet him for me.

Independent Clause in a sentence: I am not tidying the dishes unless Peter helps.
