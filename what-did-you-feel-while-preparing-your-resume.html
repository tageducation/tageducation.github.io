C. Your resume should create a good impression of you within twenty seconds.

A strong and impressive resume is important because it is often the first point of contact between a job candidate and a potential employer. In many cases, an employer will only spend a few seconds looking at a resume before deciding whether or not to move forward with an interview. Therefore, the strongest reason to create a powerful and impressive resume is that it should create a good impression of you within those twenty seconds. This means that it should be visually appealing, easy to read, and clearly highlight your qualifications, skills, and achievements in a way that makes you stand out as a strong candidate for the position.
