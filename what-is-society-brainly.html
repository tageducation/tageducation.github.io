Answer:

1. What is the element of a story which tells about the series of events and actions that relate to the central conflict?

Answer: Plot

A plot is a series of events and character actions that relate to the central conflict.

2.What do you call an element of a story which is a person, or an animal that takes part in the action of a short story?

Answer: Character

A character is a person, or sometimes an animal, who takes part in the action of a short story or other literary work.

3. What is the part of a story that depicts the struggle between two persons or things in a short story?

Answer: Conflict

The conflict is a struggle between two people or things in a short story. The main character is usually on one side of the central conflict.

4. What is the part of the story that tells the time and place in which the story happens?

Answer: Setting

Setting is the “where and when” of a story. It is the time and place during which the story takes place.

5. What is referred to as the central idea or belief in a short story?

Answer: Theme

The theme is the central idea or belief in a short story.

6. What element of a plot does not only tell about the problem in the story but also shows how the character(s) struggles?

Answer: Conflict

7. What element of the plot contains the highest point of the story?

Answer: Climax

The climax - the high point of interest and suspense in the story.

8. What element of the plot tells the end of the story?

Answer: Resolution

Resolution (Conclusion) - Final outcome of events in the story.

9. What element of a plot consists of the events that lead to the climax?

Answer: Rising Action

Rising Action - Events in the story become complicated; the conflict is revealed. These are events between the introduction and climax.

10. It is an element when the tension decreases and everything begins to settle down.

Answer: Falling Action

Falling action occurs towards the end of the story, when the tension decreases. Often during the falling action phase, conflicts may be resolved and everything in the story begins to settle down.

11. It is an element of a plot that introduces the character (s) and the setting of the story

Answer: Exposition

The EXPOSITION introduces the setting (time and place), characters and plot. It may include background information on these elements. The CONFLICT is the problem or challenge the main character has to face.
