25. Jenny has $250 in her savings account she plans on Saving $15 per week from her job. Claire has only $200 in her account and can save $20 per week. How many weeks will it take before the amount in each savings account is the same?


Answer:

10 weeks

Step-by-step explanation:

Define x:

Let the number of weeks needed be x

Find the amount of money saved by Jenny in term of x:

Total amount saved = 250 + 15x

Find the amount of money saved by Claire in term of x:

Total amount saved = 200 + 20x

Find the number of weeks needed for them to save the same amount:

250 + 15x = 200 + 20x

20x - 15x = 250 - 200

5x = 50

x = 10

Answer: It will take 10 weeks for them to have the same amount.
