1. The Presentation app included in Google Drive is called? a. Google Docs b. Google Sheets c. Prezi d. Google Slides


The answer is letter D. Google Slides .

Google Slides is a web based software, and a free presentation program offered by Google. It allows user  to create and edit presentations online.

Google Docs is a web-based software office suite offered by Google within its Google Drive service. It is a free word processor program. It allows users to create and edit files online. It is available as a web application, and also as mobile app. The app is compatible with Microsoft Office file formats. Service also includes Google Sheets and Google Slides.

Google Sheets is a web-based program offered by Google within its Google Drive service. It is also available as a web application, and mobile app. It is a free spreadsheet program. It allows users to create and edit files online.

Prezi is a short term for “presentation” in Hungarian. It is a presentation software company founded in 2009. Presentations features a map-like overview, unlike a traditional slide based presentation. It allows users to pan between topics, and zoom in on details, and pull back to reveal context.

5. differences and similarities about the common online collaborative tools;1.Trello2.Podio3.Asana4.Google Apps5.Yammer​


Answer:

Trello, Podio, Asana, Google Apps, and Yammer are all common online collaborative tools that are used for project management and team collaboration.

Similarities:

All of these tools allow for the creation of tasks, projects, and to-do lists.

They also offer features such as file sharing, commenting, and notifications to keep team members informed and on track.

All of these tools have a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to navigate and organize tasks.

They also allow to assign tasks to team members and set due dates.

They also provide a platform for team members to communicate and share ideas.

Differences:

Trello uses a Kanban-style board to organize tasks, while Asana and Podio use a more traditional list format.

Google Apps offers a suite of tools such as Gmail, Google Drive, and Google Calendar to help teams collaborate, while the other tools focus primarily on task management.

Yammer is more focused on social collaboration, allowing team members to share ideas and information in a more informal setting.

Podio is more customizable and flexible, allowing users to create their own workflows and apps to fit their specific needs.
