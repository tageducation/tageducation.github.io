Why you are interested in making interactive media and thinking about its implications on users?

Interactive media is a form of communication in which the program's outputs are influenced by the user's inputs, and the user's inputs influence the program's outputs in turn. Simply expressed, it refers to the various ways in which people process and share information, as well as the various ways in which they communicate with one another. People can engage with others through interactive media, whether it's people or organizations, by becoming active participants in the media they consume through text, graphics, video, and sound. Interactive media's goal is to engage and connect with users in ways that non-interactive media cannot. Traditional media such as television and radio did not require active participation at first.

Inverse. The inverse of an implication is an implication with the antecedent and consequent negated. For example, the inverse of (p ⇒ q) is (¬p ⇒ ¬q). Note that the inverse of an implication is not logically equivalent to the implication.

Market pricing in economic decision making

Economic theory says that the price of something will tend toward a point where the quantity demanded is equal to the quantity supplied. This price is known as the market-clearing price, because it “clears away” any excess supply or excess demand. Market clearing is based on the famous law of supply and demand.

29. What is the implication of this Platonic idea in today’s democracy?


Answer:

As explained in Understanding Culture, Society, and Politics, Plato proposed the appointment of a council that would serve as a watchman who would make sure that good laws are created and observed by everyone. A watchman is independent of the ruler of the state and has to work impartially.

However, the roles assigned to everyone - including the watchman - might be monopolized by the ruler of the state. Consequently, this does not guarantee that the decisions made by certain roles are correct and just. Should injustices arise from the decisions of these important roles, those who will investigate and watch over them remain unclear.
