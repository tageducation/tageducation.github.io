2. The total price paid for a resources used is called?


The total price paid for a resources used is called selling price.

What is the selling price

In simple terms, the selling price is the final price set by the seller or the amount to be paid by the customer for a product. When you want to sell something, this final price is what you will offer potential buyers. There are 3 basic things that become a factor for a business in determining the selling price of products such as,

The price a buyer is willing to pay. The price the seller is willing to accept. Competitive prices in the market.

Generally, this selling price also depends on the type of business you are running. Because every type of business has different forms of products and customers. So there is a possibility that later you will prioritize one factor over the other.

Why is it important to determine the selling price of the product

As you know, the selling price is the amount you are looking forward to receiving when the product sells. However, determining the selling price is not just a determination of the fee that must be paid by the customer. In fact, pricing is a business process where decisions are critical. As for because the price determines the future of the product and affects how the acceptance by customers of the product itself.

Also, what makes this important is that price also indicates how feasible the product is for your business and for customers to pay for. Simply put, the selling price gives a real picture to the customer of whether the product is worth their money and time.
