10. A healthcare provider that offers medical services that are availed through a prepaid amount of money


Answer:

Health Maintenance Organization or HMO

Explanation:

Health maintenance organizations (HMOs) provide health insurance coverage for a monthly or annual fee. An HMO limits member coverage to medical care provided through a network of doctors and other healthcare providers who are under contract to the HMO. These contracts both allow for premiums to be lower than for traditional health insurance—since the health providers have the advantage of having patients directed to them—but they also add additional restrictions to the HMO's members.

28. Directions"Fill in the blanks with the appropriate word to complete the thought of the sentence." 1. are places or institutions that offer healthcare services. 2. is financial agreement between an insurance company and an individual or group. 3. are individuals who are licensed to practice medicine.


Answer:

1. Health facility are places or institutions that offer healthcare services.

A health centre is any site that provides healthcare. Hospitals with extensive emergency rooms and trauma centers are as varied as modest clinics and doctors' offices. This includes Medical Nursing Homes that provide short-term or long-term medical therapy to inpatients that hospitals do not provide. Pharmacies and drug stores that sells prescription and nonprescription pharmaceuticals. Lastly a medical or clinical laboratory that conducts fundamental or applied research to help advance medical understanding.

2. Insurance is financial agreement between an insurance company and an individual or group.

Term in law and economics. People buy it to avoid losing money. Insurance buyers pay a "premium" (typically monthly) and vow to be careful (a "duty of care"). In exchange, if anything bad occurs to the insured person or item, the insurance company reimburses the money.Companies sell insurance products to protect you and/or your property from loss, damage, or theft.

3. Health professionals are individuals who are licensed to practice medicine.

A health professional who has been trained in the health area. A health practitioner has often been trained in a certain medical or psychiatric sector. These include doctors, therapists, and lab technicians, and a lot more. Also, the word "health professional" includes more than simply physicians and nurses. They also do research, consulting, and population health.
