1. Keep illness or injury from happening


The best way to keep illness or injuries from happening is by taking care of our body. This means taking the necessary precautions so that we are not hurt. It also means that we need to be healthy and strong. Accidents can happen at any time so our mind must also be alert all the times to respond accordingly. In cases we get into an accident, we need to assess ourselves, apply first aid and if needed, go to the hospital.  

How do we keep ourselves healthy?

When we say being healthy, it means being physically and mentally healthy. Get enough sleep. Think positive. Take multivitamins. Eat properly and exercise. These are some of the basic things that should be done. This also means avoiding vice, gambling and unhealthy thoughts. Being exposed to vice could increase the risks of being injured as most people who drink and smoke engage in risky activities due to the reduced inhibition brought by alcohol.

How do we keep ourselves safe from injuries?

We want to make sure that our home is safe from hazards. This means that fire safety precautions are in place such as having a fire extinguisher and a smoke detector. If these are not present, a good fire exit that is accessible at the least should be present. Also, when going out, stay in well-lit areas, avoid dangerous places and stay alert. Do not use your phone in public so that robbers would  not have the chance to get it.  

What do we do if we are sick or injured?

The first thing to do is to take note of you symptoms. The symptoms will reveal what your sickness is. Most of the time, it could just be the usual flu and cold, but sometimes it could be something serious. So if something strange is happening to your body, go to the doctor. If you are injured, perform first aid. If it is a wound, wash the wound with running clean water and soap. Keep it dry and apply antibiotic ointment to prevent infections. If the wound is big, then go to a doctor for stitches.
