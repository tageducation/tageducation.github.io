13. what is eustress and distress?


EUSTRESS AND DISTRESS

In our daily life, we often use the term "stress" and we tend to describe it as negative situations which leads many people to believe that all stress is bad for you, which is not true.

We have the so-caled eustress and distress, which eustress is a term for positive stress, and distress, which refers to negative stress.

Eustress, or positive stress, has the following characteristics:

Motivates, focuses energy. Is short-term. Is perceived as within our coping abilities. Feels exciting. Improves performance.

In the contrary, Distress, or negative stress, has the following characteristics:

Causes anxiety or concern. Can be short- or long-term. Is perceived as outside of our coping abilities. Feels unpleasant. Decreases performance. Can lead to mental and physical problems.
