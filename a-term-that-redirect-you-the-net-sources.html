10. what is folder Redirection​


Explanation:

English

The folder redirection is the way to keep a profile folders to a network location or other location in the local computer. Typically user profiles and settings are stored in local profile. By redirecting folders, you can access to data regardless of which computers you are logs in.

Tagalog

Ang pag-redirect ng folder ay ang paraan upang mapanatili ang mga folder ng profile sa isang lokasyon ng network o iba pang lokasyon sa lokal na computer. Karaniwan ang mga profile at setting ng gumagamit ay nakaimbak sa lokal na profile. Sa pamamagitan ng pag-redirect ng mga folder, maaari kang mag-access sa data anuman ang mga computer na iyong nai-log in.

26. Differentiate relaxation from redirection


Relaxation is the removal of stress. It is usually done by closing your eyes while positioning your body in a very comfortable position. This is a very neglected part of everyday life, and with all the talk about working hard as you can, relaxation can be mistaken for being lazy even though it is actually essential for good health.

Redirection is different in from Relaxation because it still involves movement, but towards something that will relieve stress and produce a more positive outcome. It is a way of changing what your mind is looking at, hence the word redirection. If done with the aid of a therapist, a patient can figuratively calibrate his or her focus, behavior, and attitude towards a more positive direction.
