3. simbolo ng half Note​


Answer:

That is half note

Explanation:

Half notes are probably the second most common type of rhythm after quarter notes. Half notes last for 2 counts, or twice as long as a quarter note. You can think of half notes as half of one measure, or the same thing as two quarter notes.
