1. the following applies the concept of potential energy EXCEPT!​


POTENTIAL

The following applies the concept of potential energy?

ANSWER

Potential Energy is the energy being stored and just waiting to be used up or consumed. An object that has potential energy, is usually object that is at rest, momentarily frozen and not moving.

EXAMPLES

• A ball sitting on the top of the chair that is at rest has potential energy.

• A mango fruit that is hanging atop the tree has potential energy.

• A book on top of the table has also potential energy.

Note:

If the object is moving, that is no longer a concept of potential energy, that is kinetic energy.
