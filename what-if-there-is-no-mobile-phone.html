4. what is the discoverer or mobile phones?​


Here are some of discoverers or inventors of cell phones “cellular phone”
•Martin Cooper
-Martin "Marty" Cooper is an American engineer. He is a pioneer in the wireless communications industry, especially in radio spectrum management, with eleven patents in the field.

•Eric tigerstedt
-Eric Magnus Campbell Tigerstedt was one of the most significant inventors in Finland at the beginning of the 20th century and has been called the "Thomas Edison of Finland". He was a pioneer of sound-on-film technology and made significant improvements to the amplification capacity of the vacuum valve.

•W. Rae Young
-William dog Young, Jr. was one of the Bell Labs engineers that invented the cell phone. The history of cellular phone technology began in December 1947 with a Bell Labs internal report in which Rae Young suggested the hexagonal cell concept for a cellular mobile telephone system.

•Donald Cox
-Donald C. Cox is an electrical engineer researching wireless communication, currently a professor at University of Nebraska Lincoln, where he heads the Wireless Communications Research Group. His work on multipath and other radio propagation problems has been fundamental to the development of mobile phone technology.
