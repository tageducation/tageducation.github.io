21. compose a one paragraph argument or claim about "academic freeze"​


Answer:

An academic freeze, or the suspension of academic activities for a certain period, can be a useful tool for educational institutions to address unforeseen circumstances such as natural disasters, pandemics, or other emergencies. However, it is important to carefully consider the potential consequences of implementing an academic freeze, including the impact on students' progress, financial obligations, and mental health. While an academic freeze may provide temporary relief for some students and faculty, it should not be used as a substitute for comprehensive and sustainable solutions to address systemic issues that affect the academic community.
