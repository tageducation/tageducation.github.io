4. Describe how a microwave oven uses microwaves to quickly heat food


Microwaves are produced inside the oven by an electron tube called a magnetron. The microwaves are reflected within the metal interior of the oven where they are absorbed by food. Microwaves cause water molecules in food to vibrate, producing heat that cooks the food.

Benefits of microwave

Various advantages of the heating effect caused by microwaves make these waves used for food processing, health, and in the industrial sector.

1. Food Processing

In food processing, this wave is used to make kitchen equipment such as microwave ovens. This tool was first introduced to the public in early 1947 by Spencer. There is a unique history before Spencer made the kitchen tools we still use today, even though at that time they were relatively larger than the tools we use today. In 1945 Spencer researched the radar wave capabilities of the Raytheon defense agency.

2. Health Sector

In the health sector, microwaves are used to treat patients with cancer or tumors known as hyperthermia. The principle is similar to an oven, microwaves are emitted to the patient right in the area with cancer or tumors. The exact location can be determined with certainty using these waves, the principle is similar to radar.

The tumor absorbs microwaves and a heating effect occurs resulting in atomic ecstasy. This process will destroy cancer cells or tumors that are owned by the patient without damaging other healthy tissues. Because it does not require surgery, microwaves can be an alternative to healing cancer or tumors without surgery.

3. Industrial Sector

In industry, microwaves are used to heat materials. In the food industry, this wave is used to ripen food such as biscuits. So that the resulting biscuits are perfectly cooked with a low level of possibility of scorching because heating occurs evenly. In the car industry, microwaves are used for heating the car body. This is because microwaves are more energy efficient than using conventional autoclave systems.

4. Telecommunication Field

At first, microwaves were a type of radio waves. It's just that these waves operate in high-frequency areas. If you are familiar with wifi or bluetooth then you are familiar with the use of microwaves in the field of telecommunication. Both of them utilize microwaves as a data transmission medium. In contrast to ovens and car body manufacturing because of the heating effect caused by microwaves, for telecommunication these waves are used because of other advantages.

5. Defense and Security Sector

Radar is often used on fighter aircraft to detect incoming enemies. Radar (Radio Detection and Ranging) is a system that utilizes electromagnetic waves that are used to detect an object, measure the distance between the transmitter and the object, create a map of objects such as motorized vehicles, airplanes and weather.
