2. Three blessings for which I am most thankful to God?


Here are three blessings for which I am most thankful to God.

This might differ from everyone else, but, as a believer of God and his creations as well as His religion, I must say that there are infinity list of things that we must be grateful for. Often times, we forget about this simple fact and go complain most of the times. Here, I would like to list several blessings that I consider important.

Without further ado, below are three blessings for which I am most thankful to God:

Life. Life is given to us as a blessing from God. Without life, we will not have the opportunity to say thanks to Him. Even, I would not be able to type the answer to this question. Therefore, we must use this life to our best ability by doing our best and not take everything for granted.Belief and Faith. As we all know, there are those who believe in God and those who are not. We must be thankful to God that we are blessed because we believe in Him and have faith in Him. I could not imagine myself living without this blessing, as that would equal me being lost.My family. Even though I must admit that my own family has their own shortcomings and is not perfect, I am grateful I was born into them. I feel like they deserve more, especially my parents. They take care of us since we were little, and they shower us with unconditional love even when we feel like we are a failure.

In conclusion, actually there are tons of things that we must be thankful to God for. However, those three above are, I think, what most people forget because we often take them for granted.
