Answer:

There are two categories of members in PhilHealth, these are:

1. Direct Contributors

2. Indirect Contributors

Explanation:

For Direct contributors, it is composed of employees with Employees with formal employment, kasambahays, self- earning individuals, Filipinos living abroad, lifetime members, and all Filipinos aged 21 and above and those who are able to pay the monthly premium of PhilHealth.

While, the Indirect contributors is composed of Indigents, 4P's recipients, Senior citizens, PWD, Sangguniang Kabataan official, Identified point-of-service of LGU, and all Filipinos aged 21 yrs old and above who are not capable to pay the monthly premium of PhilHealth.
