17. hawk and chicken game rules​


Answer:

Rules of the game:

One person acts the HAWK, one person acts the HEN, others act CHICKEN. The HAWK tries to catch CHICKENS, the HEN tries to prevent the HAWK, and CHICKENS try to avoid being caught. Any CHICKEN caught by the HAWK will become the new HAWK and the HAWK will become a new CHICKEN in the next round.
