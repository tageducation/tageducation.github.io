30. ACTIVITY 1: PARAGRAPH WRITING (20 PTS) Direction: Given the situation, write a short paragraph. It should include a Title, Supporting Details and Conclusion. It should contain mimimum of 10 sentences.​pa help po


Answer:

Title: Stranded on a Deserted Island

Supporting Details: I woke up one morning to find myself stranded on a deserted island. The scorching sun and relentless heat left me feeling hopeless and helpless. But, that was the least of my worries as I soon discovered that I was not alone. A huge, terrifying shark was patrolling the shores, making it impossible for me to venture into the water to search for food or supplies. Every time I tried to leave the safety of the trees, the shark appeared, its razor-sharp teeth gleaming in the sunlight. The days passed by slowly, and I was running out of options. Hunger and thirst were setting in, and I didn't know how much longer I could hold out.

Conclusion: The situation seemed hopeless until one day, I spotted a rescue boat in the distance. With the help of a large branch and my remaining strength, I managed to signal the boat and was finally rescued. The shark had become my constant companion, but I was grateful to leave the island and the fear of becoming its next meal behind. This experience taught me the value of survival skills and the importance of never giving up hope in the face of adversity.
