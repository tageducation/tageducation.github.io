. NAIL CARE SERVICES

2. LESSON 1:Use of Nail Care Tools,Materials and Equipment LEARNING OUTCOMES: LO 1. Prepare the necessary tools and equipment for the specific nail care activity; and LO 2. Use nail care tools and equipment.

3. TOOLS are the articles used in any nail care service which are durable or permanent and are hand-held. Tools are also referred to as implements.

4. Callous Remover is a tool designed to strip off calluses and corns.

5. Cuticle Nail Pusher is a tool used to push back and loosen the cuticles.

6. Cuticle Nipper is an implement used to cut the cuticles.

7. Cuticle Scissor is an implement to cut stubborn cuticles.

8. Finger or Manicure Bowl is a small bowl used for soaking the fingers to soften the cuticles.

9. Foot file is made of metal or sandpaper, with a rough file on one side to remove calluses and a fine file on the other to smoothen the feet.

10. Foot Spa Basin is a large rectangular container used for bathing and soaking the feet when giving a pedicure.

11. Manicure Tray is a flat container where all the necessary tools and implements are placed for use by the manicurist.

12. Manicure Nail Brush is a plastic handled brush with nylon bristles used to clean dirt and cosmetic residue from the nail surface and skin.

13. Mixing bowl is a small open-top, rounded cup-like container used for mixing the aromatic oils and other fluids for the hand or foot spa.

14. Nail Buffer is an implement used for smoothening and polishing the nails.

15. Nail Cutter / Nail Clipper / Nail Trimmer is a hand tool made of metal used to trim fingernails and toenails.

16. Nail File is an implement used to shape the free edges of the nail with the coarse side and bevel the nail with the finer side.

17. Orangewood Stick is an implement with pointed and rounded ends to remove excess polish.

18. Pedicure Nail Brush is a tool used to remove cosmetics and cleanse nails.

19. Plastic Container for Cotton is a small receptacle where cotton balls are put.

20. Pumice Stone is a type of volcanic rock, which is actually frothy lava solidified to form a porous rock to remove dry and annoying skin of the feet as well as calluses.

21. Supply Tray is a flat container that holds the necessary cosmetics and implements.

22. Trolley is a wheeled cart that is pushed by hand and used for transporting manicuring and pedicuring tools and materials.

23. MATERIALS are the cosmetics and supplies that are consumed and should be replaced from time to time. They are also called consumables.

24. Alcohol is an organic compound used as sanitizer.

25. Antiseptic Solution is an agent that reduces or prevents the multiplication of micro- organisms.

26. Apron is an outer protective garment that covers primarily the front of the body to protect clothes from dirt or stains.

27. Assorted Colored Nail Polish is a lacquer applied on fingernails and toenails to decorate and/or protect the nail plate.

28. Base Coat is a clear layer of polish applied to the nail plate before the application of colored polish to smoothen surface for color and promote healthier nails.

29. Benzalconium Chloride is a yellow-white powder prepared in an aqueous solution used as surface disinfectant and topical antiseptic.

30. Bleaching Soap is a soap formulated to whiten the skin.

31. Cling Wrap is a thin plastic film used for sealing the paraffin during a hand spa.

32. Cotton is a soft white downy fiber for dubbing or cleaning the nails and removing nail polish.

10. what are the nail care materials, equipment?​


Answer:

Quality Nail File Kit

These are basic supplies and they are necessary. Again, keep an eye on quality. Your clients will notice if you don't.

Cuticle Exfoliator

This helps get rid of dead skin and avoid problems with cuticles in the future.

Reusable Nail Forms

You need these for gels, acrylics and French manicures.

Diamond Nail File

Who doesn't love diamonds? This is a tool that helps you give the best results, due to the strong properties of diamonds.

Sable Brush

A good sable brush is a necessity. Cheap brushes will shed and mess up your work.

Towelettes

Towelettes are daily use items that you will go through quickly. With that said, don't skimp on quality here, either. It's tempting, but you need products that stand up to use and don't leave fibers all over newly-polished nails.

Toe Separators

Don't leave home without these. They are necessary and help eliminate the risk of messed up polish on toes!

Cuticle Oil

Good cuticle oil helps soften and moisturize, and makes clients feel pampered!

Pedicure Foot File

Smooth those rough edges with a good foot file.

Nail Cleanser

A good nail cleanser cleans nails and stops bacterial and fungus growth. Use it every time!
