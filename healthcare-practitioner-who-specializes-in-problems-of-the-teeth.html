DO YOU KNOW?Healthcare practitioners and allied health professionals are also considered health care providers. “A healthcare practitioner is an independent healthcare provider who is licensed to practice on a specific area of the body” (Meeks et al., 2011). Some examples of healthcare practitioners are podiatrists (who specialize in the problems in the feet), dentists, and optometrists. On the other hand, “an allied health professional is a trained healthcare provider who participates under the supervision of a physician or healthcare practitioner” (Meeks et al., 2011).
