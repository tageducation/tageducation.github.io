2. Among the different stock which one easiest to prepare


Answer:

Among the different types of stock, which one is the easiest to prepare?

a. brown stockb. fish stockc. white stockd. vegetable stock

The vegetable stock is the easiest to prepare.

Explanation:

Four Basic Types of Stocks in Cooking

White StockThis stock is made from white meat or bones. The preparation for this stock usually takes five to six hours for meat and eight to ten hours for bones.

Brown Stock

Brown stock is made with beef, veal bones and poultry meat. In this kind of stock, the bones should be roasted until golden brown in color but not burnt. Once the bones are roasted, cold water would be added again until boiled. Preparation for this takes eight to ten hours.

Vegetable Stock

This kind of stock is composed of vegetables. All parts of the vegetables are used for preparing this stock since they will not be eaten. The vegetable stock needs to be simmered for only 30 to 45 minutes because they are just soft and that is why it is the easiest stock to prepare.

Fish Stock

This stock is limited because fish produce different kinds of stock. Some produce better quality of stock than the other fish. Preparation takes almost one hour

12. What is the importance of stock in the preparation of soups?


Answer:

Stocks are often called the chef's "building blocks.” They form the base for many soups and sauces. A stock is a flavorful liquid made by gently simmering bones and/or vegetables. This extracts the flavor, aroma, color, body, and nutrients of the ingredients.
