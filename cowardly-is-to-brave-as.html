9. What is the solution of the cowardly bat story


Answer:

In a grassland in Africa, a lion is eating a meal. Suddenly an eagle flew low and grabbed the lion's food. "Crazy", said the lion. The king of the forest was so angry that he ordered all the animals to gather and declare war on the birds. "From now on all kinds of birds are our enemies, drive them all away, do not leave!" said the Lion.

Other animals agree because they feel they have been treated the same by the birds. As night begins to arrive, the birds return to their nests. The opportunity was used by the lions and his men to attack. Scattered birds ran away.

Fortunately there are still owls that can see clearly at night so they can all escape the attacks of lions and their men. Seeing the birds lose, the bat feels anxious, so he rushes to meet the king of the forest.

The bats say, "Actually I belong to the rat race, even though I have wings, so allow me to join your group, I will risk my life to fight against those birds". Without a second thought the singapun approved bats into the group. The next night the group led by the lion again attacked the group of birds and managed to drive them away.

The next morning, in the morning, when the lions were resting, the group of birds attacked them by throwing lion groups with rocks and nuts. "Watch out for the rain," cried the lions as they fled. The bat is so worried about it that he thinks of re-joining the group of birds. He encountered the bird king, the Eagle. "Look at my wings, I am a bird like you". Eli accept the bat with pleasure.

The battle continues, the monkeys riding elephants or rhinoceros while holding bows and arrows. Their heads are covered with a hat from a coconut shell so it will not work with stones. After the lions win, what does the bat do? He back and forth in favor of the winning group. The cowardly and unassociated nature of the old bat has been discovered by both the lion and bird groups.

14. Courage the cowardly dog characters names


Answer:

Voiced by Howard Hoffman (pilot) and Marty Grabstein (series)[1]

Courage is the title character and protagonist of the series. An overly frightened pink beagle dog who lives in Nowhere, Kansas, Courage was abandoned as a puppy after his parents were sent into outer space,[2] but was adopted by Muriel Bagge. Her husband Eustace regularly mistreats him. Ironically, given his name, Courage is a genuine coward and he often expresses his distress with over-the-top, piercing shrieks. Regardless, he still goes to great lengths to protect his owners. To the end, he often gets injured, sometimes quite brutally, and only surviving through his determination and/or pure luck. Despite his cowardice, Courage is very clever and resourceful when the situation demands it, outsmarting the villains most of the time. He also often displays super strength, carrying Muriel and Eustace with relative ease. Aiding him at saving the day is a self-aware, sarcastic and seemingly omniscient computer that he keeps in the attic. He got his name when Muriel found him as a puppy alone in an alley and remarked that he must be quite brave to be there by himself.[2]
