Physical change is a change in form, size, and sha D. Physical change does not involve phase change Which does NOT show a physical change in matter?​


Answer:

Physical changes are concerned with energy and states of matter. A physical change does not produce a new substance, although the starting and ending materials may look very different from each other. Changes in state or phase (melting, freezing, vaporization, condensation, sublimation) are physical changes.

I hope it helps.

Which of the following describes a physical change?

  C.Physical Change is a change in form,size and shape

Explanation:

A physical change is a change in one or more physical properties of matter without any change in chemical properties. In other words, matter doesn't change into a different substance in a physical change. Examples of physical change include changes in the size or shape of matter
