A code B driving license allows you to drive a vehicle of a certain size. According to the law, code B licenses are for motorized vehicles whose weight does not exceed 3500kg or minibuses or buses whose gross vehicle weight does not exceed 3500kg. As we all know, there are specific Driver's License Codes that are needed to legally drive either a motorcycle or a tricycle in the Philippines. Depending on what kind of 2- or 3-wheeler it is, you will need Code A or Code B printed on your license, lest you risk being apprehended and your vehicle impounded.
