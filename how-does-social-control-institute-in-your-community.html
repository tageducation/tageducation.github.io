7. Identify and describe the different social Institution in your community.​


Answer:

The Family

Besides the individual, the family is the smallest unit in the field of sociology. The family institution serves as a child's first exposure to society. They will use the lessons and skills learned within the family in the larger community.

Economic Institutions

Economic InstitutionsThe next level of social institution is the economy, or market institutions. Market institutions cover the industry that buys and sells goods.

Religious Organizations

Societies may include more than one religious institution, but the purpose of this social institution is the same no matter what religion it is. Religion reinforces the norms and values of a society, and its members generally contribute positively to that society.

Educational Institutions

Educational InstitutionsEducational institutions teach skills and knowledge to individuals.They help to prepare the next generation for most professions in society, including educational careers.

Government Institutions

Government InstitutionsGovernment, or the state, is another major social institution. Every society has a ruling class of some kind that makes decisions for citizens of the states.
