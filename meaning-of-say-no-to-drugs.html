30. meaning in acronym for SAY NO TO DRUGS​


SAY NO TO DRUGS

It's customary to use the phrase "SAY NO TO DRUGS" as a reminder not to consume or abuse illicit or hazardous drugs. The term serves as a call to action to make a deliberate decision to abstain from drug use and to reject drug use as an alternative. Young people are frequently the target of this message as part of public health campaigns that aim to raise their understanding of the harmful consequences of drug use and addiction.
