14. what is 75 diminished by 40?​


Answer:

75% of 40 is 30

Let's start by setting up a relationship that we're solving for. If a number x is 75% of another

number, than their ratio would be 75/100

So:

x/40=75/100

Now we cross multiply to get rid of the denominator:

100.x=75⋅40

100⋅x=300

Divide both sides by 100:

100⋅x/100=300/100

And are left with x:

x=30

Question: What is 70 percent of 40?

Percentage solution with steps:

Step 1: Our output value is 40.

Step 2: We represent the unknown value with $x$.

Step 3: From step 1 above,$40=100\%$.

Step 4: Similarly, $x=70\%$.

Step 5: This results in a pair of simple equations:

$40=100\%(1)$.

$x=70\%(2)$.

Step 6: By dividing equation 1 by equation 2 and noting that both the RHS (right hand side) of both

equations have the same unit (%); we have

$\frac{40}{x}=\frac{100\%}{70\%}$

Step 7: Again, the reciprocal of both sides gives

$\frac{x}{40}=\frac{70}{100}$

$\Rightarrow x=28$

Therefore, $70\%$ of $40$ is $28$
