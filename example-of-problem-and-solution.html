25. Problem and solution example


An example of the problem is climate change.

Examples of solutions to problems:

1. Planting trees or reforestation.

Trees and plants function to absorb carbon dioxide and produce oxides. Many trees and plants will help reduce the carbon dioxide produced by vehicles or factory activities. This will help reduce ozone layer depletion and prevent climate change.

2. Reduce the use of plastic.

Plastic waste is a type of waste that is very difficult to decompose. Plastic waste will release a lot of greenhouse gases into the atmosphere that can cause climate change. One way to reduce plastic waste is to use shopping bags.

3. Doing Reduce, Reuse, Recycle.

Reduce is an effort to reduce the accumulation of waste. For example, avoiding the use of disposable cutlery.Reuse is an attempt to reuse items that are still in use.Recycle is an attempt to recycle to make use of waste. For example, managing organic waste into fertilizer.
