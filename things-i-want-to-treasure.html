21. What is the most important thing or event in your life right now that you treasure?


Answer:My education

Explanation: for a few reasons.

I treasure and value my education more than anything because I’m aware that there are so many people around the world who aren’t able to / don’t have the same access to an education.

The main reason I treasure my education the most is because I know that it’s the one thing that I can never lose. Ever since I was a kid I’ve always gone head first into my academics. I would spend hours locked away in my room reading to my dog, I just loved everything about learning. It’s been the one constant in my life where I know I can get something out of it, excel, and won’t lose it at some point.

My family life has been constant turmoil with abuse, neglect, and toxicity ever since I was a kid. As a result, I no longer have a relationship with my family. Because of this, I know firsthand that you can lose anything: family, jobs, friends/boyfriends, apartments, cars - literally anything.

The one thing that I know I can never lose, that no one can take away from me is my education. The things that I’ve learned, the work I’ve put into it, what I’ve gained from it - these are things that I’ll always have and I’ll treasure them forever.
