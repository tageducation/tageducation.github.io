There are a lot of vehicular accidents that happens every day. So, if we are not paying attention to the signs and reminders along the road and continue to violate the rules, we are putting ourselves in danger as well as the people around us.

Along with the danger, motorists will also be held liable by law with whatever accident that they may encounter on the road. Even if it was not their fault when an accident happens, they will still be held accountable and liable for the damages they have caused.

That is the reason why we really have to be responsible with every action that we take especially if we are on the road. Both motorists and people must pay attention to road safety. In this case, it will lessen the danger that could cause a loss of life.

There is also a saying that it is better to be safe than sorry. Our life is precious and incomparable to anything. So do not just let it be taken by just being irresponsible. For the motorists, your life matters as well as the lives of the people around us. If you are not confident on driving, make sure to always bear in mind the big responsibility behind the wheel. 22. reminders about safety while cleaning​


Answer:

• Clear the clutter. Before you begin the actual tasks of scrubbing, vacuuming, and dusting, go around the home and pick up the clutter from the stairs, landings, and floors. Ideally, nothing should be out of place and in your path, but that rarely happens.

• Secure electrical and telephone cords. Safely tuck the cords next to walls or under the furniture so that they will not be in the path of traffic.

• Encourage family members to put their things away after they are finished using them. At the very least, encourage your family to help with the cleaning chores by picking up and putting away their items on cleaning day.

• Whenever you are using the stairs, keep one hand free on the railing side of the stairway. If you do lose your balance, at least you will be able to grab the railing and stop your fall.
