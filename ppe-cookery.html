1. What are the PPE in Cookery


Answer:

Personal Protective Equipment (Cookery)

PPEs are clothing and equipment that are designed to protect a person or minimize the exposure to hazards or harm that may be present in the person's workplace environment.

There are different kinds of PPEs for different kinds of work and the list of PPEs below are what you gonna need in your Cookery subject. The PPEs when cooking does not only protect the cook but also ensures that the food isn't mixed with unwanted things.

Apron - protection from stains and hot liquidsHair net - holds the air in place to not mix with the foodMouth mask - prevents saliva from going to foodSafety boots/shoes - prevents slipping on the floorGloves (optional) - for a little more cleanliness. Use pot holder when handling hot pots :)

9. 1. What is PPE? 2. When PPE is necessary?​


Answer:

1.Personal protective equipment is protective clothing, helmets, goggles, or other garments or equipment designed to protect the wearer's body from injury or infection. The hazards addressed by protective equipment include physical, electrical, heat, chemicals, biohazards, and airborne particulate matter.

2.PPE is used to reduce or minimize the exposure or contact to injurious physical, chemical, ergonomic, or biological agents. Remember, a hazard is not “gone” when PPE is used, but the risk of injury may be reduced.
