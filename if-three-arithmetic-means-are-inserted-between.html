12. If three arithmetic means inserted between 11 and 39. find the second arithmetic means


If three arithmetic means are inserted between 11 and 39, then 11 is the first term and 39 is the fifth term.

The three arithmetic means are the second (a₂), third (a₃), and fourth (a₄) terms.

The second arithmetic mean is the third or middle term.

You may solve for the second arithmetic mean (a₃) or the middle term in two ways:

Option A:

a₃ = (a₁ + a₅) / 2
a₃ = (11 + 39) /2
a₃ = 50/2
a₃ = 25

Option B:

Find the difference of the given sequence, then solve for a₃:

[tex] a_{n} [/tex]= a₁ + (n-1)(d)

Where:

[tex] a_{n} [/tex] = 39
a₁ = 11
n = 5

39 = 11 + (5-1)(d)
39 = 11 + 4d
4d/4 = (39 - 11)/4
d = 7

Solve for a₃:
a₃ = 11 + (3-1)(7)
a₃ = 11 + 14
a₃ = 25

ANSWER:  25
