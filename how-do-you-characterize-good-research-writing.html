1. How do you characterize good research writing


Answer:

The main characteristics for good quality research is listed below:

It is based on the work of others.

It can be replicated and doable .

It is generalisable to other settings.

It is based on some logical rationale and tied to theory. ...

It generates new questions or is cyclical in nature.

It clearly states the variables or constructs to be examined. Valid and verifiable such that whatever you conclude on the basis of your findings is correct and can be verified by you and others. The researcher is sincerely interested and/or invested in this research.

10. How would you characterize quantitative research​


Quantitative research is statistical: it has numbers attached to it, like averages, percentages or quotas.

Answer:

Quantitative research deals in numbers, logic, and an objective stance.

Explanation:

Quantitative research focuses on numeric and unchanging data and detailed, convergent reasoning rather than divergent reasoning [i.e., the generation of a variety of ideas about a research problem in a spontaneous, free-flowing manner].

There are 7 or 8 characterististics of Quantitative research:

•Generation of models,theories and hypotheses.

•Collecting imperical data.

•Modelling of data.

•Analysis of data.

•Experimental control.

•Variable manipulation.

•Development of instruments.
