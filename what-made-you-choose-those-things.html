24. Processing Questions:1. What made you choose those things?2. What would happen if you don't bring those things?​


1. What made I choose those things (to go traveling):

I choose to bring my travel bag, clothes, toiletries, medicine, documents, and money because this is very crucial.

2. Explanation what would happen if I don't bring those things?:

1. Travel Bag

Travel bags are definitely a must-have to carry my belongings. The bag can also be customized based on my needs, starting from a suitcase, backpack or carrier bag.

2. Clothes

Next, there are clothes that you clearly must bring because this one item is a basic need. I should also bring a change of clothes that can be adjusted to my purpose. For example, if I want to go on vacation to the beach, it would be better if I bring a swimsuit that is suitable for playing in the water.

3. Toiletries

Don't forget to bring my own toiletries, starting from shampoo, towels, soap, toothbrush and toothpaste. Because when traveling in a clean body condition, it will certainly be much healthier and more comfortable.

4. Medicines

It is preferable that you bring medicines that can help you perform first aid when something untoward happens. For example, wind oil, balm, bandages, wound plasters, and other first aid kits.

5. Documents

To make my trip safer, don't forget to carry important documents such as driver's license, ID card, passport and visa when you go abroad. However, I still have to keep the privacy of the document as good as possible so that it doesn't get misused.

6. Money

The other most important thing that must be carried when traveling is of course money. If I don't have money, I run the risk of causing trouble for others which will eventually turn my supposedly enjoyable trip into a nuisance.
