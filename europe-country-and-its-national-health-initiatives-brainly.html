27. In Europe the main generating countrie are thoe in the ------- of the continent while the main detination countrie are in ------- Europe.


Answer:

In Europe, the main generating countries for electricity are those located in the western and central parts of the continent, while the main destination countries for electricity are located in eastern and southern Europe. This is due to the fact that western and central Europe have a higher concentration of industrial and commercial activities that require a large amount of electricity, while eastern and southern Europe have a lower concentration of these activities and therefore have a lower demand for electricity. Additionally, the geography of Europe plays a role in this distribution, as western and central Europe have a higher density of hydroelectric and nuclear power plants, while eastern and southern Europe rely more on coal and natural gas for electricity generation.
