13. an infancy related song from kalinga


The infancy related song from Kalinga is called Wiyawi. The term “wiyawi” is the Kalinga’s word equivalent to lullaby in English. This is the song or chant sung by the Cordilleran to make their babies sleep. In the lullaby, the word “wiyawi” is being repeated and its sound is effective in making the baby fall asleep immediately.
