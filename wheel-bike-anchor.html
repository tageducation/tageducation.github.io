10. For a 26" bike, the wheel diameter is 26 inches. Calculate how far the bike travels when the wheel has completed 350 revolutions (1 inch - 2.54 cm)​


Step-by-step explanation:

The diameter of the wheel is 26 inches, so we can use the formula for the circumference of a circle:

C = π * d

Where C is the circumference and d is the diameter.

C = π * 26 inches

C = 82.068 inches

Next, we multiply the circumference by the number of revolutions:

82.068 inches * 350 revolutions = 28,736.8 inches

Finally, to convert inches to centimeters:

28,736.8 inches * 2.54 cm/inch = 73,457.6 cm

Answer:

So the bike would travel a distance of 73,457.6 cm after the wheel has completed 350 revolutions.
