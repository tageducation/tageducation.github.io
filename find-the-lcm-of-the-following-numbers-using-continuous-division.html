= 450 / 5

= 90

Therefore,

LCM(10, 45) = 90

3.) LCM(4, 12, 30) =

LCM(LCM(4, 12), 30)

Working from the innermost parentheses outward:

LCM(4,12) = (4 × 12) / GCF(4,12)

= (4 × 12) / 4

= 48 / 4

= 12

LCM(12,30) = (12 × 30) / GCF(12,30)

= (12 × 30) / 6

= 360 / 6

= 60

Therefore,

LCM(4, 12, 30) = 60

4.) LCM(10, 15, 60) =

LCM(LCM(10, 15), 60)

Working from the innermost parentheses outward:

LCM(10,15) = (10 × 15) / GCF(10,15)

= (10 × 15) / 5

= 150 / 5

= 30

LCM(30,60) = (30 × 60) / GCF(30,60)

= (30 × 60) / 30

= 1800 / 30

= 60

Therefore,

LCM(10, 15, 60) = 60

5.) LCM(8, 20, 40) =

LCM(LCM(8, 20), 40)

LCM(8,20) = (8 × 20) / GCF(8,20)

= 160 / 4

= 40

LCM(40,40) = (40 × 40) / GCF(40,40)

= 40
