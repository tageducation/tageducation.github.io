Speech Context

Dictates and affects the way people communicate. This is critical because as a receiver, it tells you what importance to place on something. What assumptions to draw (or not) about what is being communicated. Most importantly, it puts meaning into the message. The hardest thing about communicating effectively is knowing how to “set the context”.
