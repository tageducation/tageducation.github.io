Problem:

what if two boys pull the heavy cabinet at the same time and same directions with 5 N of force each what is the net force acting on the cabinet.

The net force acting on the cabinet will be 10 N since the the two boys  are pulling the cabinet in the same direction.

                       Force            

Boy 1               5 N                    

Boy 2             5 N  

                       10 N

Since the two boys exerting Force on the the same direction then it will be called an unbalanced  fore or a net force. If net force is acted to an object, the object will move if its at rest, or it will stop if its in motion.

Law of inertia states that:

An object moving will continue moving unless acted upon by a net force. An object at rest will remain at rest unless acted upon by a net force.
