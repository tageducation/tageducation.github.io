5. make a comics about prejudice and bias in english


Answer:

Two characters, one of whom is prejudiced against the other, are forced to work together on a school project. Over the course of the project, the prejudiced character realizes that their assumptions about the other person were based on stereotypes and that they have more in common than they thought.

A character witnesses a friend making prejudiced remarks about a group of people. The character initially avoids confronting their friend because they don't want to upset them, but eventually realizes that standing up to prejudice is more important than avoiding conflict.

A character experiences discrimination themselves and is forced to confront their own biases when they find themselves feeling prejudiced against another group of people. They realize that discrimination and bias are harmful and that everyone deserves to be treated with respect.

These are just a few ideas, but the possibilities for a comic about prejudice and bias are endless. The important thing is to create a story that shows how harmful and damaging these attitudes can be, and how important it is to stand up against them.
