23. Settings:Characters:Plot:Conflict:Theme:Point of view:of Sinigang by Marie Aubrey J. Villaceran​


Sinigang by Marie Aubrey J. Villaceran​ an Analysis

Sinigang by Marie Aubrey J. Villaceran back in 2001. The story is about the acceptance of the main character to the situation that involves her father’s unfaithfulness towards her mother and their family. The story tackles sincere apologies and the act of forgiving.

Settings: The story is set in Liza's home.

Characters: The main characters of the story were Liza, Liza’s parents, her Aunt Loleng, Sylvia (her father’s mistress), and Lem (Her half-brother from Sylvia).

Plot: It is about Liza, retelling her meeting with her father’s mistress, to her Aunt Loleng while they are making Sinigang. She told about their interaction at her half-brother’s funeral, Lem. Conflict: The conflict of the story is the main character’s path to acceptance that her father was an unfaithful man and had an affair and child with another woman.

Theme: The theme of the story is that there is no such thing as perfection in humans. At the end of the day, we’re bound to make mistakes.

Point of view: The story was in first person and is narrated by the author herself, Marie Aubrey J. Villaceran.

27. A DIRECTIONS: Identify the slx (6) elements from the short story Sinigang.1. Setting :2. Characters :3. Plot :4. Conflict :5. Theme :6. Point of View :PA SAGOT NAMAN PO :((​


Answer:

1. Setting : Liza's home

2. Characters : Liza, Tita Loleng, Sylvia and Sylvia's dad

3. Plot : The story starts with a girl named Liza, and her Tita Loleng, preparing Liza's favorite dish, Sinigang. Usually, it was never Liza's turn to help her Tita Loleng in cooking sinigang. But not today. Liza was the one tasked by her Tita Loleng to pick out the vegetables to use in the Sinigang.

4. Conflict : man vs himself

5. Theme : Forgiveness is something hard earned

6. Point of View : First Person point of view

Explanation:

A character is a person, or sometimes even an animal, who takes part in the action of a short story or other literary work.

   

The setting of a short story is the time and place in which it happens. Authors often use descriptions of landscape, scenery, buildings, seasons or weather to provide a strong sense of setting.

 

 

A plot is a series of events and character actions that relate to the central conflict.

 

The conflict is a struggle between two people or things in a short story. The main character is usually on one side of the central conflict.

On the other side, the main character may struggle against another important character, against the forces of nature, against society, or even against something inside himself or herself (feelings, emotions, illness).

   

The theme is the central idea or belief in a short story.
