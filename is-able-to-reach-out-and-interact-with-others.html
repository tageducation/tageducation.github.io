EXAMPLES OF BEING ABLE TO REACH OUT AND INTERACT WITH OTHERS:

1. Always smile.

2. Create a welcoming body language.

3. Be Sincere.

4. Ask questions about themselves.

5. Be more open-minded.

FURTHER EXPLANATION:

Humans are social creatures who cannot be separated from group life and social activities, one of which is interaction. Here are some good ways to interact with other people:

1. Always smile to someone or people

Create a happy atmosphere is very important in interacting one way is to smile. Even by forcing a smile, it will make you feel better. Smiling is very important if you are trying to attract someone, because it shows that you are a positive and fun person.

2. Create the welcoming body language.

Try not to look busy or annoyed that people leaving will leave you. Do not cross your arms and make wrinkles on the forehead. Create body language that describes you as happy and ready to talk to other people. Make eye contact with the person you are talking to.

3. Be Sincere

You should always show a genuine interest in the conversation to make the person you are talking to more enthusiastic about interacting. avoid doing other things in the conversation such as playing on the cellphone, it will make the person you talk to feel offended.

4. Ask questions about themselves.

Don't always talk about yourself, your interlocutors also want to talk about them. Ask how they are, how he feels or occasionally ask him personal questions, it will make them feel cared for.

5. Be more open mind.

Do not close yourself to accept anyone who wants to open up to you. Don't judge other people before getting to know them. Talk to the person several times to get a good read of their personality.
