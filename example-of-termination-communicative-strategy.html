1. examples of termination in communicative strategy?


Termination is one of communication strategies that provide the use of verbal and non-verbal cues to signal an end to an interaction. Some examples of termination are the following:

1.    Prolonged clarificatory statements

2.    Further questions

3.    Continuation of topic already discussed
