6. Define career health plan?


Answer:

Career health plan is a career guide for those who wants to pursue a health career. Included in the career plan maybe the career pathways, the self-assessment plan for health career and the goals on health career (when and how to attain it). It is an important part in planning one's health career because it serves as a framework for your chosen field. It starts from the moment you assess yourself if you fit into the carer, pursuing the degree and until you graduate and pursue the short-term and long-term plans with regards to your chosen field. It is very crucial because it serves as your plan guide and wrong career health plan might mislead you in your future career.

25. what is health career planning


Answer:

Health Career Planning is designed for students to be familiarized with medical profession and allied health services. Some of which are doctors, nurse, medical assistant and patient care technician.

Explanation:

In choosing a future profession, it involves continuous learning and self-assessment. It is necessary to understand one's capabilities and skills to choose a career. But why choose a health career? It is because, it will surely make a difference in people's lives. Aside from having a good salary and job security it is a self-fulfilling because of helping others. But not all have a given talent in health industry. It is important to discover personal strengths through self-assessments. Learn also the pros and cons in choosing a career because it is a great factor in career fulfillment in the future.
