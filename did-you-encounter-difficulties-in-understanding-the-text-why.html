1. did you encounter difficulties in understanding the text? why?​


Causes of difficulty in understanding the Text

Reading comprehension is the process of both uncovering meaning and creating it via engagement with written language. It deals with the procedure for identifying the text's concepts and meaning.

Some of the factors causing low comprehension of reading texts are: Limited vocabulary proficiency.
The most crucial element in conveying meaning is vocabulary. This is so because the text contains a lot of terminologies. Consider whether there is a significant mismatch between the reader's knowledge of the word's meaning and the word used in the text if the reader is having trouble understanding what he or she is reading.Reading fluency issues.
Some people frequently encounter these issues because they read poorly. Students who read too quickly or too slowly frequently don't comprehend the content adequately. Instead of allowing complete attention to be focused on high-level ideas and concepts inside the text, slow reading has the tendency to restrict cognitive capacity to the low-level letter and word processing. Fast reading, however, may compromise word recognition accuracy and cause you to overlook crucial information.Lack of expertise in the field.
At the initial meeting, the teacher typically presents a direct interpretation of the course material. In reality, you should give information beforehand using different media like films, posters, brief lectures, etc. But it appears that many instructors conduct initial lessons.The text's degree of difficulty.
The fundamental determinant of whether or not a text can be read with comprehension is its degree of difficulty. Readers find it challenging to comprehend texts that are complex in terms of subject, vocabulary, phrase length, and sentence structure. Students must read at their level in light of this.The use of efficient and insufficient reading techniques.
This results in the usual challenges that arise during the teaching and learning process. Actually, because the teacher serves as a manager and facilitator in the classroom, kids need a variety of tactics from him or her. To increase comprehension, reading strategies are essential.Poor verbal judgment.
This challenge results from pupils' poor integration of new knowledge with existing knowledge. However, it depends on the student's capacity for logic when reading. The degree of that person's IQ will determine this.Information processing issues.
Each person has a unique amount of working memory. When reading, the reader must have sufficient memory to comprehend the message. The reader must have the ability to retain important information in working memory and build the required connections between concepts in order to sustain the sense of the text as sentences and paragraphs accumulate.Issues with information
Retention following reading Recall depends on the student paying close enough attention during the reading assignment and being aware of the crucial facts. Students frequently run into issues that make it difficult for them to comprehend the content. The power of memory is increased when readers connect new information in the text to their prior understanding and experience, as well as when they repeatedly think about important passages. Students can benefit from using mind maps or graph organizers to help them retain knowledge from texts.
