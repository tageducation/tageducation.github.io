3. for a construction worker injury may be


For a construction worker, injury may be always a risk because of the work environment. Injuries can come from various sources such as falling debris, misuse of the equipment, fellow co-workers, among others. It is critical that construction workers follow safety regulations. Aside from that, their companies must also institute workplace safety practices. Below are some guidelines to prevent injury in the workplace.

Wear PPE or Personal Protective Equipment

These are meant to either prevent or lessen the damage to a worker's body. Also, construction sites have strict regulations on wearing these so workers must follow. If the construction sites do not enforce this, they must be reported to the proper authorities.

Exercise caution

Always exercise caution especially when operating machinery. Also, do not attempt to operate machinery that you are not certified to operate. Also avoid operating machinery or even driving when taking medications that cause drowsiness.

Know first aid

It is best that workers know first aid so that immediate help can also be given when an accident happens on-site. Many of these accidents would involve crushed bones, lacerations and wounds so it is best to train for those specific injuries.  

Attend and understand safety training

Every year, workers are supposed to be updated on the current workplace safety standards. Attend these training sessions and make sure that you understand the reason behind it.  

Attend drills

Fire and earthquake drills are important as well since anytime an earthquake or a fire could happen in the workplace. If we are not prepared, this could result in injuries and death.  

Identify hazards

Construction workers are prone to falling and crashing. Hence, it is best that you identify possible hazards associated with this. If you notice anything, always bring it to you foreman's attention. You can also report to the site safety officer so that that hazard could be removed and the situation corrected. Again, if no action is taken, then go to the relevant government agency.

Workplace safety is very critical and is everyone's responsibility. It is not only the construction worker who is responsible but also the company and the clients.
