-copy the first term.

-put the two factors (4t and 4t).

-then copy the last term.  

[tex]t^{2} + 4t + 4t + 16[/tex]

group it.

[tex](t^{2} + 4t) + (4t + 16)[/tex]

find the gcf of [tex](t^{2} + 4t) and (4t + 16)[/tex] and put it outside the parenthesis.

divide their gcf to themselves.

t (t+4) + 4 (t+4)

then, the remaining are t and +4. put them also inside the other parenthesis. (t+4).

(t+4) (t+4)
