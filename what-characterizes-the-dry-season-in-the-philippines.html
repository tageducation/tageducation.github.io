2. What characterizes the dry season in the Philippines


Answer:

During the dry season, humidity is very low, causing some watering holes and rivers to dry up. This lack of water (and lack of food supply) may force many grazing animals to migrate to more fertile spots.

WHAT CHARACTERIZES THE DRY SEASON IN THE PHILIPPINES?

3. What characterizes the dry season in the Philippines?


Dry season in the Philippines spans from November through to May, and these months are considered the best time of year to visit this tropical archipelago. It's worth bearing in mind that temperatures and humidity levels are particularly high in April & May.
