THE FASTEST PLANET

∆ MERCURY

The planet Mercury is the closest planet from the Sun and also the fastest planet due to its position and proximity to the Sun.

Mercury revolves around the sun for just jawdroppingly 88 Earth days!, traveling and accelerating through space at roughly 112,000 mph or 180,000 km/h, the fastest speed of any other planet in the Solar System.
