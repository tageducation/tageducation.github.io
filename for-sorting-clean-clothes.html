8. Why do we need to sort out the clothes before washing?​


Answer:

We sort clothes by color and by fabric.  

We need to sort out the clothes before washing for the following reasons.

Explanation:

1.Saving soap

Usually, when we wear white clothes, we are more careful from dirt than when we are wearing colored clothes. This is one of the reasons why we need to sort out clothes before washing because we need to wash first the clothes that have less dirt (usually white). If we wash first those colored or dirty ones, we could not use the water anymore to wash the white.

2.Painting clothes

Some colored clothes might paint the white clothes if there is no sorting of colors.

3.Sensitive fabric

Some clothes are washable by washing machine while other clothes are sensitive that they should be washed by hands or else they will be torn or damaged.

12. give the important consideration in sorting clothes and linens into a pile​
