8. how do motivation,self awareness, self-efficacy, empathy and social dexterity influence career success


Motivation is the drive we exhibit towards achieving our goals and dreams.  In order to attain career success, we must have the right amount of motivation in pursuit of those goals and dreams.

Self-awareness allows us to identify our strengths to pursue greater opportunities, and our weaknesses which we have to develop to acquire the success that we are after.

Self-efficacy is our ability to believe in our confidence in making our plans work.  We use this quality to control our own motivation, behavior and environment that would affect our career success.

Empathy is our ability to relate with others genuinely and without personal biases.  We cannot achieve success on our own, not based on our pride.  We need to relate and to learn from other people.  Through empathy, we develop the essence of compassion and kindness.

Social dexterity is a skill that plays an integral role in our career success.  It is our ability to work well with other people.  Staying in a workplace requires us to establish interpersonal skills because we are going to work with others toward our personal successes.
