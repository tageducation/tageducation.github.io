Answer:

1. 4 beats to complete, answer is Quarter Note

2. 2beats to complete, answer is Eight Note

3. 3. beats to complete, answer is Dotted Quarter Note

4. 4 beats to complete, answer is Dotted Half Rest

Explanation:

Whole Note/Rest - 4 Beats

Half Note/Rest -2 Beats

Quarter Note/Rest- 1Beat

Eight N/R- 1/2

Sixteenth N/R- 1/4

and so on.

with the dotted, simply divide each beat then add to original beat.

example:

Quarter Note is 1 Beat. If it is Dotted QN, simply divide the 1 Beat so it will become 1/2. Then that 1/2 add to original beat of Quarter Note which is 1.

So , 1 + 1/2 = 1 1/2 Beat - Dotted Quarter Note

Same goes to the rest notes and rests.
