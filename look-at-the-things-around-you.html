29. Look at the things around you. List at least five example of kinetic energy?


Five Examples of Kinetic Energy:A moving carSomeone bicycling  A fan that is circulating airA person who is walking The sounds moving from your speaker through your ears.  

What is Kinetic Energy?Kinetic energyIt is something that refers to the energy that is somewhat closely related to displacement. It is known as “moving energy.” And the power obtained when an object starts to move (brainly.ph/question/2394629).  It is an energy that is not instilled or at rest. Also, it is dictated by a force of motion and found in converted potential energy.Kinetic energy is not present when an object is at rest.All things that are about it are moving.Kinetic energy increases when a particular object accelerates.

Three Subcategories of Kinetic Energy:

Rotational- these objects are found in a moving axis. And the energy used here depends on how fast it is, the weight, and where the center of the location is. One example of it is the Earth, where it rotates around its axis and the sun.

Translational- the cause of it is where two objects start to collide. One example of it is the golf club that strikes a golf ball.

Vibrational- this is caused by some objects that are vibrating. One example is a smartphone that is shaking/vibrating.
