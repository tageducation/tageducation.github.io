22. A fewer number of respondents is needed in a study to minimize the error.


A fewer number of respondents is needed in a study to minimize the error. [TRUE]

Definition of Research Sample

The sample is a representative or part of the population that has the same characteristics as the population that is representative and describes the population so that it is considered representative of all the populations studied. ampling techniques can help researchers generalize to the population represented.

The sample is a small part of the population members taken according to certain procedures that can represent the population. The sample is used if the population under study is large and it is impossible for the researcher to study the entire population. These constraints can occur due to the limited cost, energy, and time of the researcher. The sample to be used from the population must truly represent the population under study.

There are three stages that must be passed in the sampling process, which are as follows:

1. Define the target population. The target population should have been found by the researcher when he found problems and research problems. The target population is a collection or element that has research information. The results of the research from the population will produce an inferential conclusion for the group or population.

2. Define a sample frame.  sample frame or sample frame is representative of the target population collection or elements. For example, a map of the province with the name of the district or a bibliography with the title of the book and the author.

3. Determine the number of samples. This is the element to be included in the sample. The size of the sample is strongly influenced by various factors, including the purpose of the study. If the research is descriptive, it usually requires a large number of samples. However, if only to test the hypothesis, the number of samples need not be large. The larger the sample size, the greater the statistical power. On the other hand, the smaller the number of samples, the smaller the statistical power, so that it will affect the results of the study.

25. the sum of two numbers is 8 find the numbers if the sum of their cubes is to be minimal


[tex]\tt{\huge{\red{Solution:}}}[/tex]

Step 1: Represent the two numbers.

Let

x = the number

y = the other number

S = sum of the cubes

Step 2: Translate the first sentence into equation.

x + y = 8

y = 8 - x (Eq. 1)

Step 3: Translate the second sentence into equation.

S = x³ + y³ (Eq. 2)

Step 4: Substitute Eq. 1 to Eq. 2

S = x³ + (8 - x)³

S = x³ + 8³ - 3(8)²(x) + 3(8)(x)² - x³

S = x³ + 512 - 192x + 24x² - x³

S = 24x² - 192x + 512 (Eq. 3)

Step 5: Differentiate Eq. 3

S' = 48x - 192

Step 6: Set S' to 0 and solve for x.

S' = 48x - 192 = 0

48x - 192 = 0

48x = 192

[tex]\boxed{x = 4}[/tex]

Step 7: Substitute the value of x to Eq. 1.

y = 8 - 4

[tex]\boxed{y = 4}[/tex]

Therefore, the two numbers are 4 and 4.
