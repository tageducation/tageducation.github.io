10. What do you call the famous puppet shadow play in indonesia?


Famous Puppet Shadow Play in Indonesia

Wayang Kulit is an Indonesian kind of famous puppet shadow that captivates the audience with captivating plots and expert puppeteering. In order to make it easier for the audience to discern between the various characters, the puppets are constructed of buffalo skin and meticulously designed down to the smallest detail of outfit and color.

The dhalang uses skillful hand motion and narrative to portray tales of kings, princesses, ogres, and knights. Electric bulbs or other light sources are now employed in performances instead of cotton sheets and oil lamps, which were once used to create the play of light. The Hindu epics Ramayana and Mahabharata serve as inspiration for many of the themes. Wayang Kulit was recognized by UNESCO in 2003 as a Masterpiece of Oral and Intangible Heritage of Humanity.
