C. Kristiyanismo

Ang “kristiyanismo” ang tawag na kapangyarihan ng mga prayle na pamahalaan ang Edukasyon at relihiyon sa pilipinas. Ng dumating ang mga prayle, ipinalawak nila ang kristiyanismo dito sa pilipinas kaya ang relihiyon ng ibang tao sa pilipinas ay krisyano.

2. ano ang ibig sabihin ng salitang frailocracia​


Answer:

Frailocracia

La Frailocracia Filipina is a pamphlet written in 1889 by Filipino writer Marcelo H. del Pilar. The pamphlet was an answer to a pamphlet entitled Los Frailes en Filipinas, which was written by a Spaniard.

Del Pilar’s arguments are in five parts:

Article I: Filibusterism

Article II: How much of a Spaniard is the Friar?

Article III: The Friar’s Role in the Civilization of the Philippines

Article IV: The Influence of the Friar on the Filipinos

Article V: Aspirations of the Filipinos

La Frailocracia Filipina (Barcelona: Imprenta Iberica de Francisco Fossas, 1889) was published under del Pilar’s pen name Plaridel.

The title can be translated into English as The Filipino Frailocracy.

Frailocracy is a form of government in which the friars are the rulers. The priests during the Spanish colonial period were so powerful that they practically ruled the Philippine Islands.

The Spanish term can be transliterated into Tagalog as praylokrasya.
