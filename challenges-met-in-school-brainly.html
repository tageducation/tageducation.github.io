19. challenges met in school ​


Explanation:

1. Difficulty in understanding subject

- It's not always the students fault to not understand but it can be the teaching style of the teacher. Students differ from each other in terms of their level in understanding so a teacher must be flexible. Students must learn to take initiative to ask the teacher that they don't understand so that the teacher can repeat teaching again.

2. Need to take care of younger siblings while studying

- This is mainly the hardest problem of the oldest child in the family with a younger siblings because we are being quarantined and most students are in modular form of teaching so the only one capable of teaching is the oldest. You need to have a proper time management in teaching others and learning from your studies.

3. Lack of conductive space for learning

- This is also my problem and from my experience it's hard to learn without a proper space from yourself you can't focus and you'll get disturb. So, I bought a small table where I use it in a corner when studying that's the only solution I can think of. If you want to study then you have to be tough on yourself.

4. Not enough food for family

- Most students will just work for the sake of the family but for my opinion you can work and study at the same time. After graduating you can have a better future and a better job so you have to be strong and not let go of studying and think for the future. Even if today you can't eat find a way so that you can eat.

5. Struggling to adjust in distance learning

- It's every hard especially in major subjects to study by yourself but there's a way you can find tutorial videos in YouTube and read.

6. Procrastination or lack of motivation to study

- Especially when your at home and all things can possibly distract you are inside of your house, you can avoid it but if you to pass and have a good grade then you should fight procrastination. You don't have to study for 24 hours. You just need to review for an hour or 2 a day depending on you

7.Distructed by a lot of things at home (tv,house chores,social media,online games,etc.

- Avoid all of these things when it's time to study. Those things aren't gonna run out of your house you can easily use it anytime. You just have to know when to use and when not use it. If you have something to do like homeworks then don't use it.
