4. what is nature of the teenagers problem​


Answer:

What is the nature of teenager’s problem?

Adolescence is the stage to determine your identity. Identity means how a person sees himself in relation to his world. The adolescent learns to negotiate new responsibilities, evolving relationships, and a new sense of self. The process of the adolescent development can become quite challenging and sometime overwhelming.

Teenagers Problem

As you grow up, being healthy is not only your concern, it is also changes in the following matters that may occur in adolescent development:

• Physical Changes

• Mental Changes

• Emotional Changes

• Social Changes

• Moral-spiritual Changes

• Environmental Changes

Here are some examples of changes and problems related to the teenagers:

Emotional Changes

1. Erratic emotions, behaviors, and mood swings.

2. Hormonal balance that cause regression to more childish behavior patterns.

3. Lack of self-esteem

4. Easily offended and sensitive to criticism

5. Boys are sexually active, therefore, they are prone to sudden increase in hormones that may cause of being aggressive.

6. Girl’s become self-conscious because of the changes that are happening to them, giving them the feeling of insecurity.

Body Odor

1. During the early teen years, the sweat glands become more active that’s why when the teenagers play actively or engage in sports or other physical activities, they may tend to perspire profusely.

2. If the teenagers do not know how to manage their body hygiene, it may cause unpleasant body odor that can affect not only other people but also them.

3. In order to stop and prevent having body odor, the teenagers must always take a bath everyday, apply soap and rinse with water properly, apply deodorant or antiperspirant daily, and be sure to bring extra clothes and change when needed.

Dental Problems

1. The more common dental problems of teenagers include dental caries or tooth decay, halitosis or bad breath, and gingivitis or swelling of the gums.

2. Not brushing, poor tooth brushing skills and drinking carbonated drinks and eating sweets of the teenagers frequently may cause to proliferation of dental plaque, which is made up of colonies of microorganisms. Dental plaque hardens to “tartar” if not removed.

3. That’s why, tooth brushing after every meal and flossing at least twice a day should take at least a minimum of three minutes to remove all food particles lodged between the teeth.

All of those problems of the teenagers may affect not only their health but also their studying, social status, family, spiritual beliefs, and also their dreams in life.

Who are the people that can help them in dealing with their problems?

In every problem, there is always a solution. Having a teenage problem are very difficult and burden to every teenager. That’s why if they have any problem, they can always ask some advice and help to the following:

• Parents

• Teachers

• Relatives

• Friends

• Love ones

• God

What rights protect the teenager?

They have the right for special protection and care

What would you ask yourself before buying health product to avoid some experience?

Before buying some health products, you need to assess and check first if it safe, proven, and useful to you as a consumer and a teenager. Make sure to think and always ask yourself the following questions:

• The health product is safe or not?

• Can I use the product for my body hygiene?

• What are the health benefits of this health products?

• Is there an advantage if I use this heath products?

• What are the effects of this products to me as a teenager?

• Are the health products being advisable to use by the doctors to the people not only to the teenagers or not?

As a Grade 10 student, how can you help the teenager?

Students can also help their classmates especially that they are the same teenagers. If I am a Grade 10 student, I will be a good model to them using only those safe and proven health products, I will always make sure that I am clean, have a good posture, healthy teeth, good grades, and especially healthy body. In that way, I can already advise and tell them if what are the right things to do and if what are the advantages that they can get using different health products. Finally, if all of us are already doing the right things and following the correct ways in life, we can already prevent any problems especially in our health.’
