1. examples of acts of human and human acts


Acts of Human and Human Acts are two different things. Acts of Human are processes that happen naturally in the body, without a person's being conscious about them. Examples of Acts of Human are breathing and beating of the heart. Meanwhile, human acts are acts that require the creation of choices, and usage of responsibility of a person. Examples of Human Acts are telling the truth and going to work.

Acts of Human vs. Human Acts Acts of Human - These are processes that do not require a decision-making action from a person. They happen naturally in the body, without a person's being conscious about them. Acts of Human is neither right or wrong. Human Acts - These are actions that require a person to make a choice, use utmost responsibility, and use his/her conscience. Human Acts can either be right or wrong. Examples of Acts of Human

Here are some examples of Acts of Human:

breathing beating of the heart perspiration growing of the hair growing of the nails etc. Human Acts

Here are some examples of Human Acts:
