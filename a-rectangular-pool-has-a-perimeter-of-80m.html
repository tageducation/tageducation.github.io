3. A rectangular pool haw a perimeter of 80m. it has a width of 12m. what is its length​


Answer:

The length of the rectangular pool is 28 m.

Step-by-step explanation:

The perimeter of a rectangle is P = 2(L + W)

Given: P = 80 m, W = 12 m. Let us substitute.

80 m = 2(L + 12 m)

Divide both sides by 2

40 m = L + 12 m

Subtract both sides by 12 to isolate variable L.

L = 40 m - 12 m

L = 28 m

Therefore, the length of the rectangular pool is 28 m.

23. the perimeter of a rectangular vegetables garden is 36m.the area is 80m² find the length and width of the garden​


Answer:

The rectangular garden has a length of 10 m and a width of 8 m.

Step-by-step explanation:

Step 1: Formulate an equation using the formula for the perimeter of the rectangle.

The perimeter of a rectangle is given by the formula:

P = 2l + 2w

where: P - perimeter

l - length

w - width

Substitute the value of P which is 36 in the equation

36 = 2l + 2w

Simplify

18 = l + w

Equate to w

w = 18 - l

Step 2: Formulate an equation using the formula for the areaof the rectangle.

The area of a rectangle is given by the equation:

A = l x w

where: A - area

l - length

w - width

Substitute the value of A which is 80 in the equation

80 = l x w

Equate to w

w = 80/l

Step 3: Compare the two equations and solve for length.

w = w

18 - l = 80/l

Simplify

18l - l² = 80

Equate to zero

l² - 18l + 80 = 0

Factor the equation

(l - 10)(l - 8) = 0

l1 = 10 | l2 = 8

Considering that length is the longest side of the rectangle, length = 10 m

Step 4: Solve for w using any of the two equations

w = 18 - l

w = 18 - 10

w = 8

The rectangular garden has a length of 10 m and a width of 8 m.
