5. Where can you find Philippines in the distinction?​


The Philippines is located in Southeast Asia.

     Southeast Asia's Philippines are situated along the Asiatic Mediterranean's eastern edge. The South China Sea, the Pacific Ocean, the Sulu and Celebes Seas, and the Bashi Channel form its western, eastern, southern, southern, and northern borders, respectively. Manila is the nation's capital and primary entrance point. Cebu, located 562 kilometers south of Manila, is the nation's second-largest international entry point.

    The Philippines are a group of 7,100 islands with a combined land size of about 300,000 square kilometers. It contains three main island groups: Mindanao is located further south, Visayas is in the center, and Luzon is in the north. Additionally, the Philippines is home to renowned natural wonders including an underground river and rice terraces, amazing diving locations with an abundance of wildlife, exciting festivals that highlight its vibrant culture, and hospitable inhabitants.
