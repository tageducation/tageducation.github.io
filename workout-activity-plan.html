An activity schedule is used as a basic time management tool, consisting of a list of times by which tasks, events or actions are intended to take place or of a series of events in chronological order when they are intended to be performed.

Wake Up Early

For those of you who are Muslims, you can wake up early when the dawn call to prayer rings out so you can perform the dawn prayer. For others, you can continue with various morning activities so that your day is fresher. Not only that, by getting up early, it becomes more time to start activities.

Study

You can absorb a lot of knowledge when studying in the morning, especially for some things that require high concentration. You can set the study time from 09.00 to 11.00 am.

Learn new things

From 11.00 to 12.00 noon you can try to find out new things to enrich your knowledge. Can be through surfing in cyberspace, searching via Google, or reading a book that you like.

Rest

You can rest from 12.00 noon to 02.00 noon for naps and other activities.

Completing college assignments

You can complete your homework in the afternoon. That way your task will be completed immediately.

Relax with family

Do not ignore this one activity. Although it sounds simple, but this is very suitable and important for you to do.

Curfew

08.00 to 10.00 pm you can use to relax and leave the tiring daily activities. You can also do things that can cheer you up.

8. Night Sleep

When entering 10 pm, it's good for you immediately fall asleep. This is because sleeping early is proven to be very good for your physical and psychological health. In addition to keeping your body healthy, your mental health will also be healthier.

Those are simple things that you can manage every day to make your day more organized and not neglected. Start organizing these simple things before you hope to lead others. Because one of the benchmarks for the success of a leader is the ability to organize and lead yourself.
