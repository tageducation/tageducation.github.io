8. why does round pizza come in a square box?


It is easier to make a round pizza than a square one, due to the dynamics of the dough when it is shaped. And it is FAR easier to make a square box than a round one. ... Square boxes are easier to make than round ones. Round pizzas are easier to make than square ones.


9. Gary ordered 30 pizzas for a party. 60% of the pizzas have 12 slices each. The remaining 40% of the pizzas have 10 slices each. Complete the model. Then complete the statements to find the total number of slices of pizza.


60% of 30 = 60/100 (30)

60% of 30 = 18

18 x 12 = 216

40% of 30 = 40/100 (30)

40% of 30 = 12

12 x 10 = 120

120 + 216 = 336

18 of the 30 pizzas which have 12 slices each have 216 slices in all.

12 of the 30 pizzas which have 10 slices each have 120 slices in all.

There are 336 overall slices of 30 pizzas Gary ordered.
