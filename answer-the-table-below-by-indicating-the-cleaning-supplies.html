Answer:

DIFFERENT TOOLS AND EQUIPMENTS with Materials Used and Safety Precautions while using.

Explanation:

Tools/Equipment

1. Blender -

Supplies and Materials Used are Glass Pitcher, Strong Blade for Blending Purposes, and the bottom part that called Housing which has a high-speed electric motor and control of speed.

Safety Precautions to be Observed: Make sure not to touch the Strong Blade while in use, it may cut a finger due to its strong spinning movement.

Always place the Blender on a dry and flat surface to avoid untoward movement while in use.

2. Iron -

Supplies and Materials Used are: Made from a combination of Plastic and Metal which is Aluminum and Steel.

Safety Precautions to be Observed: Never touch the flat surface when in use, it may cause a skin burn that can also, damage the layers of the skin.

3. Ironing Board-

Supplies and Materials Used are: Teflon cotton cloth on the top parts and "The Face" part is made of Metal or Wood.

Safety Precautions to be Observed: Always make sure that before using for Ironing A Clothes, the secured lock that makes the board stable are all connected and tight.

4. Stethoscope

Supplies and Materials Used are: used as Medical devices to listen to the internal sounds of humans or animals.

Safety Precautions to be Observed:  Never soak the device in any liquid materials and avoid placing it on an extreme heat surface.

5. Thermometer

Supplies and Materials Used are: hollow glass tube  or the metal part as mercury

Safety Precautions to be Observed: Never wash the entire device with hot water and make sure that the level is always at below 35 degrees Celcius

6. Sterilizer-

Supplies and Materials Used are: One example is a Medical Sterilizer which is used to sterilize medical types of equipment, can be made of

Metal and plastics.

Safety Precautions to be Observed: Never touch the Device while in use due to heat that it releases for sterilizing process.
