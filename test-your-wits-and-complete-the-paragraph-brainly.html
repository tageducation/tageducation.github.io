10. christians are witnesses of jesus through simplicity of life Brainly​


Answer:

It is a belief held by many Christians that they are called to be witnesses of Jesus through the simplicity of their lives. This means that they strive to follow Jesus' teachings and example by living humble and unassuming lives, focusing on the things that are truly important and letting go of material possessions and earthly desires that may distract from their relationship with God.

Christians may seek to embody this witness through various ways, such as by living modestly, sharing their resources with others, and serving those in need. By living simple and selfless lives, Christians hope to demonstrate the love and grace of Jesus to those around them and to inspire others to follow him.

This belief is rooted in the teachings of Jesus, who encouraged his followers to prioritize spiritual matters over earthly ones and to live lives of selflessness and service. In the Sermon on the Mount, Jesus said, "Do not lay up for yourselves treasures on earth, where moth and rust destroy and where thieves break in and steal, but lay up for yourselves treasures in heaven, where neither moth nor rust destroys and where thieves do not break in and steal" (Matthew 6:19-20).

By living simple and selfless lives, Christians hope to follow Jesus' example and to share his message of love and redemption with others.

21. a fall into a pit, a gain in your wit.​


Answer:

A Fall into a Pit:

“A fall into the(a) pit, a gain in your wit” was translated from a famous Chinese proverb that means "learning from mistakes".

A Gain in your Wit:

A fall into the pit, a gain in your wit. This proverb's message is: Having gone through a setback, one will have gained experience and wisdom, which will be useful if only one can take warning and learn something from the setback.
