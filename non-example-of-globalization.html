Globalization is a process that involves interactions between individuals, groups, and countries that are interrelated, dependent, and influencing each other across national boundaries. The process of globalization occurs in various fields, ranging from economics, politics, society, culture, communication, science and technology, transportation, and so on. Globalization is things that cover the whole world or as a whole. The existence of globalization has many positive impacts, but on the other hand, there are also negative impacts. Therefore, we must be able to pick and choose the impact of globalization on us.

Forms of Globalization:

Economic Globalization

Economic globalization is a form of globalization that has an impact on the economy, such as buying and selling online via e-commerce, making export and import activities easier from one country to another, and cooperation in the economy by world countries.

    2. Cultural Globalization

Giving influence on the social and cultural fields of humans. The culture of one region is more quickly entered and accepted by other regions or countries due to the influence of globalization, such as international fashion and fashion trends becoming more desirable.

   3. Science and Technology Globalization

The globalization of science and technology is a form of globalization that has an impact on the fields of science and technology, such as access to information that can be done easily and quickly via the internet.

     4. Transport Globalization

Transportation globalization is a form of globalization that has an impact on the field of mobility and transportation, such as the movement of people from one country to another more easily,
