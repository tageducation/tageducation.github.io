5. What difficulty will you encounter if you only have data from two recording stations in grade 10 Activity 1:Find the Center


To pinpoint the exact epicenter of an earthquake, a minimum of three data from three different recording stations are required. A collection of only two data is insufficient and inaccurate if we were to pinpoint an epicenter. This is because two intersecting circles would form a Venn diagram, with two point intersections. One of these points is the epicenter. But you will not know which is which without the third data. So it would be invalid and inaccurate to pick between the two intersecting points as guessing is not tolerated in the field of science.
