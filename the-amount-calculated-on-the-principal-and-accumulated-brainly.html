since accumulated value ang hinahanap, meaning future worth in 5 yrs.

F = future worth

P = principal

m = periods per year

n = year

since quarterly, m =4

In Business mathematics, the basic interest amounts that are discussed are simple and compound interest. Simple interest is the interest amount accumulated for a given number of years added to the principal. The compound interest, on the other hand, is the interest amount accumulated due to the compounding periods.

Types of Basic Interests AmountsCompound InterestSimple Interest

The compound interest is the interest amount accumulated due to the compounding period.

27. Determine the amount of money that will be accumulated if a principal of PHP 500 000 is invested at an annual rate of 3.1% for 10 years compounded annually. *


The amount of money accumulated is Php 680,093.398

[tex]\\[/tex]

Step-by-step explanation:

[tex]\\[/tex]

Given:

P = Php 500,000

r = 3.1 % or 3.1/100 = 0.031

t = 10 years

n = 2

[tex]\\[/tex]

Required:

Future value

[tex]\\[/tex]

Equation:

[tex]FV\:=\:P{(1\:+\:\frac{r}{n})}^{nt}[/tex]

where:

FV - Future value

P - Principal

r - interest rate

n - compounding periods per year

t - years

[tex]\\[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]FV\:=\:Php\:500,000{(1\:+\:\frac{0.031}{2})}^{(2)(10)}[/tex]

[tex]FV\:=\:Php\:500,000{(1\:+\:0.0155)}^{20}[/tex]

[tex]FV\:=\:Php\:500,000{(1.0155)}^{20}[/tex]

[tex]FV\:=\:Php\:680,093.398[/tex]

[tex]\\[/tex]

Answer:The amount of money accumulated is Php 680,093.398
