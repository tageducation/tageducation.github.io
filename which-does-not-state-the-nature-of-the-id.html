18. Your mom told you to clean up your room because visitors will be coming to your house. State what would ID, SUPEREGO, and EGO will say. ID - SUPEREGO - EGO


Answer:

Your mom told you to clean up your room because visitors will be coming to your house. State what would ID, SUPEREGO, and EGO will say.

ID: Okay, I will clean it.

EGO: Okay, I will clean it so that when the visitors arrive everything is tidy and at the same time mother will not get angry to me for not following hear commands.

   

SUPEREGO : Okay, I will clean my bed because it is just rightful for me to clean my room when its dirty and because it is also my obligation to do so.

 

Explanation:

The id is the most basic part of the personality. It also represents our most animalistic urges, like the desire for food and sex. The id seeks instant gratification for our wants and needs. If these needs or wants are not met, a person can become tense, anxious, or angry.The ego deals with reality, trying to meet the desires of the id in a way that is socially acceptable in the world. This may mean delaying gratification and helping to get rid of the tension the id feels if a desire is not met right away. The ego recognizes that other people have needs and wants too, and being selfish isn't good in the long run.  

The superego develops last, and is based on morals and judgments about right and wrong. Even though the superego and the ego may reach the same decision about something, the superego's reason for that decision is based more on moral values, while the ego's decision is based more on what others will think or what the consequences of an action could be on the individual.
