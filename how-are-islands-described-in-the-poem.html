area of responsibility

Combatant commanders of the Unified Command Plan (UCP) are assigned an Area of Responsibility (AOR) that is used to define an area with specific geographic boundaries where they have the authority to plan and conduct operations, and for which a force or component commander bears a certain responsibility. The term is widely used in various countries throughout the world, but it originated with the US Armed Forces. All military troops in the AOR, regardless of branch of service, are under the responsibility of a single commander who uses this system to exert command and control.

The United States President signed the U.S. It was announced on December 17, 2008, that the United States' newest Command would have an updated Command Plan (UCP).
