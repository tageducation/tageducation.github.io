1. how many sixths are there in 42/6​


There are 42 sixths in ‌42⁄6‌.

Step-by-step explanation:

Division of Fractions

Fractions represent a part of a whole. It has two parts, the numerator which tells the number of parts taken from a whole and the denominator which tells the total number of equal parts. To know how many sixths there are in ‌42⁄6‌, you have to divide it by sixths. Sixths is ⅙ in fraction. Below are the steps in dividing fractions:

Give the reciprocal of the second fraction or turn the fraction upside down.Change the sign to multiplication.Multiply the numerators and the denominators across. Simplify, if possible.

Solution:

‌42⁄6‌ ÷ ⅙

= 42⁄6‌ × 6⁄1

= 252⁄6‌ or 42

Final Answer:

42
