Explanation :

Affinal Khinsip is a type of relationship or relationship of individuals based on marital status. This type of relationship is not like family ties in general which are influenced by blood relations, but refers to the legality or contract of marriage. In its application, forms of social kinship arise due to marital relations such as the status of mother-in-law, sister-in-law, brother-in-law , stepchildren etc.

Some researchers classify social kinship as the connection of a community or religious interaction. Kinship rules and titles are factored in by cultural preferences. In essence, when a couple of husband and wife officially marry, both partners will be directly connected to become one community with their partner's family so there needs to be adjustments in behavior, culture and principles to avoid family problems.

Covenant marriage is a legally distinct kind of marriage in three states (Arizona, Arkansas, and Louisiana) of the United States, in which the marrying spouses agree to obtain pre-marital counseling and accept more limited grounds for later seeking divorce (the least strict of which being that the couple lives apart from each other for two years).
