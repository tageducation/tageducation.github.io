10. what will happen to the other bulbs in series connection when bulb is remove?​


Answer:

When one light bulb is removed from the series circuit, the other two light bulbs go out

The bulbs in the series will NOT GLOW if you remove one bulb from it (if you expect a detailed answer please read the following explanation)

Reason

Imagine a series connection with 3 bulbs. As it's series connection, the negative terminal of bulb 1 is connected to positive terminal of the bulb 2, then the negative terminal of bulb 2 is connected to positive terminal of bulb 3.

As we remove one bulb (For example bulb 2) it removes electrical connection between bulhb 1 and 3. hence circuit will not be complete.

Hence none of the bulbs in series will glow.

In simple terms when we remove a single bulb from a series, No Bulb Glows
