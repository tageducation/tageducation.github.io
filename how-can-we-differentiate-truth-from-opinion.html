EXPLANATION:

The characteristics of opinion sentences include:

1. Subjective in nature and usually accompanied by opinions, suggestions, and explanations that explain.

2. Contains opinions about events that occurred.

3. Indicates an event that is not certain to occur or will occur at a later date.

4. Represents the thoughts or opinions of a person or group.

5. The information submitted has no evidence.

6. It is usually indicated by the use of the words: could be, probably, might, should, should.

While the characteristics of truths sentences are:

1. Can be proven true.

2. Have accurate data both time, date, place, and event.

3. Collected from trusted sources.

4. Be objective

5. Usually can answer the question formula 5W+1H.

6. Stating events that are or have occurred and have occurred.
