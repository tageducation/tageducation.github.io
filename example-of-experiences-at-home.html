MAGNETIC SEPARATION AT HOMEAnswer with Explanation:

By using a magnet, there are mixtures at home that can be separated through the method of magnetic separation. Usually, magnetic metals mixed with non-metallic and non-magnetic objects are the most commonly magnetically separated mixture. For example: iron fillings and sugar, pins and marbles, thumbtacks and shredded papers, needles and beads, and staple wires and peebles, paper clips and sand. Since iron fillings, pins, thumbtacks, needles, staple wires and paper clips have magnetic properties which can be attracted by magnet, they can be then separated from mixing with non-magnetic objects like sugar, marbles, sand, shredded paper and beads.

Other methods of separation at home are filtration, decantation, manual separation and sifting.
