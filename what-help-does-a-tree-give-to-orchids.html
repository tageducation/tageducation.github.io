Answer:

What help does a tree give to the orchids? The tree lifts the relatively small plant up off the ground and into the air so it can reach the sunlight. This helps the orchid survive in the dense canopy conditions of the tropical forest.

How about the importance of orchids to tree? Being in the canopy also allows the orchids to receive more sunlight for photosynthesis. However, the orchids receive nutrients from the air and compost on the trees. Thus, they do not harm the plants on which they grow by drawing nutrients from them
