Answer:

1. How important are wood carving in the economic and socio-cultural aspects of the community?

Wood carving is a well-established and essential indigenous relic sector. It has the ability to enhance the living conditions of thousands of citizens.

2. Do you think handicrafts made of indigenous materials like coconut handicrafts still feasible in business ventures today?

Several designers make use of indigenous materials in their design and this could increase its publicity.  

3. What should the Philippine government do to establish a sustainable promotion of the pottery industry?

Encouraging the comeback of production and establishment of pottery can benefit long-term economic development by creating jobs for a larger number of people. As a result, the government can help by encouraging more people to work in this field.

4. What is the best strategy in preserving and conserving our tattoo art culture?​

By maintaining its cultural tradition.
