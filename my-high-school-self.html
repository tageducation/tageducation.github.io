Text-to-text Connection – This connection happens when a learner can connect the text they are reading to another text they have read before. A successful text-to-text connection happens when the learner can apply lessons he/she read from a former text to the current one.

Text-to-self Connections – This connection happens when a learner can connect the text they are reading to a personal experience. The connection may differ with each learner because not everyone has the same experiences.

Tex-to-world Connections – This connection happens when a learner can connect the text they are reading to the current issues in society. A successful text-to-world connection involves the learner and his/her use of the current text she/he reading to fully understand the issues in society.
