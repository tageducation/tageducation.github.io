The expression: He runs faster than the wind.

The expression contains the meaning or figure of speech hyperbole.

EXPLANATION:

Hyperbole is a type of figure of speech in opposition to the figurative language of satire. Hyperbole is a figure of speech that is often used to express opposition. This figure of speech is characterized by an exaggerated statement and goes beyond the existing reality.
