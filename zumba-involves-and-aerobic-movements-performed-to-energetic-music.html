Answer:

4 fitness centers

Step-by-step explanation:

25 children = 1 fitness center

12 children = 4 teens or 3 children = 1 teen

6 teens = 3 adults or 2 teens = 1 adult

If there are 11 adults, 5 teens and 19 children

Convert Adults to teens

11 × 2 = 22

27 teens and 19 children

Convert teens to children

27 × 3 = 81

100 children in total

100 ÷ 25 = 4
