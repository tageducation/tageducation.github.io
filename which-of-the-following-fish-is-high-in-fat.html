Which of the following fishes is low in fat?

a. cod

b. salmon

c. trout  

d. tuna

Answer

The correct answer among the choices is letter a. Cod is a type of fish that has the lowest total fat content. There are different kind of salmon but all of them has higher fat content than cod. Trout fish is considered to have a low-fat or medium-fat content but still has higher fat content than cod.
