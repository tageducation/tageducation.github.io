17. Ang C Major scale ay binubuo ng walong tono. Ano ang hometone nito?A. soB. laC. tiD. do​


Answer:

The syllables for major are DO, RE, MI, FA, SO, LA, TI, DO. (That's pronounced doh, ray, mee, fa, so, la, tee, doh.) So, for example in the key of C major, G is the 5th degree (C, D, E, F, G...); therefore the correct solfege syllable is SO.

26. the hometone or tonal centerof songs in major key is DO​


Answer:

A scale starts with the note that names the key. This note is the tonal center of that key, the note where music in that key feels "at rest". It is also called the tonic, and it's the "do" in "do-re-mi". For example, music in the key of A major almost always ends on an A major chord, the chord built on the note A.
