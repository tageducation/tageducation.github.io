30. Learning Task 3. Answer the following word problems.​


1. 1 49/60 hours

solution:

1. Given ⅔ - hours spent for Math homework on

Monday

¾ - hours spent for Math homework on

Tuesday

⅖ - hours spent for Math homework on

Wednesday

Asked: The number of hours spent for Math

homework from Monday to Wednesday.

Number Sentence: ⅔ + ¾ + ⅖ = N

Answer: ⅔ + ¾ + ⅖ = N

Using the least common denominator of 3, 4, and 5 express the fractions as similar.

(20)(2)/60 + (15)(3)/60 + (12)(2)/60 = N

40/60 + 45/60 + 24/60 = N

85/60 + 24/60 = N

109/60 = N

1 49/60 = N

Therefore, Jose completed his Math homework for 1 hours.

2. 13/16 cm

solution:

Given: 3 7/16 cm of wire

2 ⅝ cm of wire needed for the project in TLE

Asked: The length of wire that Mika needs to cut.

Answer: 3 7/16 - 2 ⅝ = N

Rename the fraction as similar fractions.

7/16 = 7/16

5/8 = 10/16

Proceed with the subtraction.

3 7/16 - 2 10/16 = N

since is greater than we need to rename again the fraction.

2 23/16 - 2 10/16 = N

Therefore, Mika had to cut cm of wire.

3. ¾ kg

solution:

Given: 4 ½ kg of spaghetti

6 families

Asked: The amount of spaghetti that each family can take home.

Number Sentence: 4 ½ ÷ 6 = N

Change 4 ½ to improper fraction.

4 ½ = 9/2

Proceed with thr divesion.

9/2 ÷ 6 = N

Get the reciprocal of 6 and change the operation to multiplication.

9/2 × ⅙ = 9/12

Express the answer in lowest term.

9/12 ÷ 3/3 = ¾ kg

Therefote, each family can take home kg of spaghetti.
