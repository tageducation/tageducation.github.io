THE UNDEFINED TERMS

The undefined terms are points, lines, and planes.  

Points

A point does not have dimension or magnitude. It also does not have a direction. However, it has a specific position. It represents by a dot and denotes by a capital letter.  

Collinear points - points on the same lineNon-collinear points - points not on the same lineCoplanar points - points on the same planeNon-coplanar points -  points not on the same planeLines

A line is a one-dimensional figure that has no thickness nor width. The symbol for a line is a line with arrows in both directions. It is denoted above the capital letters.  

Parts of a Line

Line segment - has two endpoints and all points between themRay - has one endpoint (or starting point) that extends infinitely in one directionPlane

A plane is a flat surface where a line can lie. It does not have thickness but has width and length. It denotes a capital letter before its word.
