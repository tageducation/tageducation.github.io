20. solve each problem involving integers​


Answer:  1. -4°F

                2.  7th floor

Step-by-step explanation:    1. Given: 8°F - temperature reading at 10 am

              3°F - temperature drops every hour

Since 10 am to 2 pm  = 4 hours

4 hours x 3°F = 12°F

8°F - 12°F = -4°F

Therefore, the temperature reading will be -4°F at 2 pm.

2. Given: 3rd floor - current floor where Kate is

              6 floors - Kate goes up

              2 floors - Kate goes down

3rd floor + 6 floors = 9th floor

9th floor - 2 floors = 7th floor

Therefore, Kate is currently on the 7th floor.
