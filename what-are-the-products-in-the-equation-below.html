1. What are the products in the equation below


The products in the equation are located to the right of the chemical reaction

For reaction :

Zn+CuSO₄⇒ZnSO₄+Cu

The products :

ZnSO₄+Cu

Further explanation

In chemical reactions there are several components involved:

Reaction coefficient

shows of elements/atoms that react

Concentration

shows the level of reagent concentration

Reaction speed

changes in concentration during the reaction

Reactants

compounds/elements that experience the reaction, usually there is a decrease in concentration during the reaction and is a substance that is at the beginning of the reaction. Usually placed on the left side of the chemical equation

Product

compounds/elements resulting from the reaction, usually there is an increase in concentration during the reaction and is formed from the reaction between reactants. Usually placed on the right side of the chemical equation

For example in the reaction :

Zn+CuSO₄⇒ZnSO₄+Cu

The reactants :

Zn+CuSO₄

The products :

ZnSO₄+Cu

Step-by-step explanation:

Dimensions:

Width:  x

Length:  y

Perimeter = 2(x) + 2(y)

Perimeter: 90 m

Perimeter of a rectangular lot:

2x + 2y = 90

[2x + 2y = 90]/2

x + y = 45

y = 45-x   (length in terms of x)

Sum of dimensions = (x) + (45-x)

Area of rectangular lot:

Area = (x)(y)

Area = (x)(45-x)

Area: 450 m²

Product of dimensions = (x)(45-x)

Solve for dimensions using area formula:

(x)(45-x) = 450

-x² + 45x = 450

x² - 45x + 450 = 0

Factor the equation:

(x  - 15)(x - 30) = 0

Equate each factor to 0:

x - 15 = 0

x = 15

x - 30 = 0

x = 30

The dimensions are 30 meters long and 15 meters wide.

Check:

Sum of dimensions/Sum of roots:

2(30) + 2(15) = 90

60 + 30 = 90

90 = 90  (True)

Product of dimensions/Product of roots:

(30)(15) = 450

450 = 450  (True)

If its length is 4m more than its width, find the dimensions,​


Answer:

1. a. Information given: Product of 2 consecutive odd integers is 63.

b. Asked to find: Find the 2 consecutive odd integers.

c. Representation: n (n+1) = 63, where n is an integer

d. Working equation: n (n+1) = 63

e. Solve the equation and check the answers:

n (n+1) = 63

n² + n = 63

n² + n - 63 = 0

(n + 9) (n - 7) = 0

n + 9 = 0

n = -9

n -7 = 0

n = 7

The two consecutive odd integers are 7 and 9.

To check, multiple 7 and 9 and you’ll get 63.

2. a. Information given: Area of rectangular garden is 140, length is 4m more than its width.

b. Asked to find: Find the dimensions of the rectangular garden

c. Representation: Width = w, Length = w + 4, Area = 140

d. Working equation: 140 = w² + 4w

e. Solve the equation and check the answers:

w (w + 4) = 140

w² + 4w = 140

w² + 4w - 140 = 0

(w - 10) (w + 14) = 0

w - 10 = 0

w = 10

w + 14 = 0

w = -14

The dimension of the rectangular garden is 10m by 14m

To check, formula for area of rectangle is A = LxW, A = 14x10, A = 140.
