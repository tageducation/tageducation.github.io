11. What is republic Act no. 5708 all about?


Republic Act No. 5708 is also known as "The Schools Physical Education and Sports Development Act of 1969."

This is an act providing for the promotion and financing of an integrated physical education and sports development program for the schools in the Philippines. The Department of Education will handle an integrated physical education and sports development program in all schools in the Philippines. This act aims to help the youth to appreciate and embrace the importance of physical development, as well as mental development through various sports and activities.  Personal discipline, physical growth, and social training for all pupils and students should be addressed. Department of Education should create a Physical Education and Sports Center Development Commission to promote and coordinate the physical education and sports development program.

Program of Activities  A program focused on health education to improve pupils' and students' diets A program focused on general physical fitness for all elementary and secondary students A program focused on competitive athletics A program focused on intramural and inter-unit athletic competition with proper recognition and incentive awards for outstanding performance in competitive activities An annual program focused on athletic competition (regional up to national level)

23. what is republic act 1977 all about?


Answer:

An Act Converting the Miagao High School in the Municipality of Miagao, Province of Iloilo, into a National Vocational School to be Known as the Miagao Vocational School, and Appropriating Funds.

Explanation:

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippine Congress Assembled:

Section 1. The Miagao High School in the Municipality of Miagao, Province of Iloilo, is hereby converted into a national vocational school to be known as the Miagao Vocational School. The said school shall be under the supervision of the Bureau of Public Schools and subject to the provisions of the School Law. Courses of secondary level shall be offered in the said school.

Section 2. The sum of two hundred thousand pesos is hereby appropriated, out of any funds in the National Treasury not otherwise appropriated, for the expenses of the said school during the fiscal year nineteen hundred fifty-eight. The necessary funds for its operation and maintenance in subsequent years shall be included in the annual General Appropriation Act.

Section 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved: June 22, 1957.
