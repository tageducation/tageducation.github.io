3. How can the situation influence the filipino medical Frontliners​


The current situation can influence the Filipino medical frontliners because of the shift. They immediately shifted from what they used to do before into something more. their lifestyles and time management were the things that affected them the most.

In demand frontliners

the massive number of needed frontliners influenced how they live. Because of the massive demand in terms of numbers to accommodate patients and people, they would really need to adjust their schedules, time, and even sleep. Some of them were required to work 24 hours a day and some even volunteered just to supply the needed numbers of frontliners.

The shift to schedule

Before, doctors, nurses, and workers from the medical industry only works the regular 9 hours per day. When the new normal and current situation strikes, they shifted everything into some of the most impossible schedules they ever had in their entire life. This made them one of the busiest industries not just in our country, but also in the entire world.

Ratio and proportion of medical frontliners

As the number of patients exponentially increases almost daily, there were no additional numbers of licensed medical practitioners in the market. This is what makes their job harder and somewhat influences their life the most. even if they would request additional manpower from foreign countries, they know it would be a huge risk and still not enough. The manpower of the world's medical frontliners is not enough to accommodate the population of affected patients. That is why strong regulations are really being implemented at least to pacify the new normal.
