2. How do you do GRESA format? Example please.


Explanation:

Problem solving using the GRESA method

G - Given

R - Required

E - Equation

S - Solution

A - Answer

Ex. Find the density of an object that weighs 250g and with a volume of 50 cm3.

G - mass = 250g

volume = 50 cm3

R - density

E - density = mass / volume

S - 250g/50 cm3 = 5g/cm3

A - The density of the object is 5g / cm3.

Here is another example:

What is the volume of a wooden block with a length of 9 cm, a width of 3 cm, and height of 5 cm?

G - length = 9 cm

width = 3 cm

height = 5 cm

R - volume

E - volume = length x width x height

S - 9 cm x 3 cm x 5 cm = 135 cm3

A - The volume of the block is 135 cm3.

Problem:

How high is he helicopter passing overhead if 4.20 second you hear the sound of its engine?

Note:

The speed of sound in normal condition is 344 m/s The formula of speed is ( s = d / t ). To Find distance, we can use ( d = st ).

Given Data:

t ( time ) = 4.20 s

sound speed = 344 m/s

d ( distance ) = ? unknown

Solution:

The formula of speed is ( s = d / t ). To Find distance, we can use ( d = s × t ).

d = s × t

  = 344 m/s  × 4.20 s

  = 1,444.8 meters

Answer:

The helicopter has a height of or distance which is equal to 1,444.8 meters

16. A 150 J of energy is added to a system that 50 J of work.By how much the internal of the system be raised?Use the GRESA formatPakisagutan po plsss need ko po ngayon


Answer:

A 150 J of energy added to a system that does 50 J of work, by how much will the internal energy of the system be raised?

GIVEN: Q = 150 J (+ since energy was added to the system)

W = 50 J (+ since work is done by the system)

FIND: ∆U = ?

SOLUTION: ∆U = Q - W

= 150 J - (50 J)

∆U = 100 J

FINAL ANSWER: The internal energy of the system raised 100 J.
