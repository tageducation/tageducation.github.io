4. What is the missing element in the problem ,"Cris collected 10 cans of food. if he is 50% complete with his final goal. How many cans does he want to collect?"​


Answer:

The missing element here is the Base or Whole number. In order to solve it, we will use thw formula B= P/R

where :

B= Base (to be solved yet)

P= Percentage (10)

R= Rate (50%)

Step-by-step explanation:

1. Substitute the given to the formula:

B = P/R

B = 10/0.50 Note! Convert 50% first to its decimal form

2. Divide the given

B = 10/0.50

= 20

3. Interpretation

Thus, Cris wants to collect 20 cans.
