Answer:

1. What animals live in water with gills, fins and scales?

A fish is an animal which lives and breathes in water. All fish are vertebrates (have a backbone) and most breathe through gills and have fins and scales.

2. What group of vertebrates is snake?

Reptiles are vertebrates that have scales on at least some part of their body, leathery or hard-shelled eggs, and share a number of other features. Snakes, lizards, turtles, crocodilians, and birds are reptiles. Like all vertebrates, reptiles have bony skeletons that support their bodies.

3. What animals with wings, feathers that love to fly?

Three kind of animals that have wings, or appendages that are most often used for flight. They are birds, insects and bats.

4. What group of vertebrates is a dog?

Mammalia

5. What group of vertebrates is a frog?

Amphibians

Amphibians are small vertebrates that need water, or a moist environment, to survive. The species in this group include frogs, toads, salamanders, and newts.
