Relationships That Exist in an Ecosystem

Living things interact with each other in order to survive. The interaction produces relationships that are either beneficial or harmful. The relationship is harmful when one animal is harmed. The relationship is beneficial when both organisms in the relationship are benefited. Either ways, the relationship still exists and will continually exists in an ecosystem.
