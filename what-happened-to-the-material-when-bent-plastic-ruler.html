Light

Impermeable to Water

Water-resistant or impermeable is one of the most popular plastic properties. Due to this nature, many people make it as a raw material for equipment, especially equipment as a container for eating or drinking.

Easy to shape and print

One of the main points of plastic is used as the main raw material is because of this property. Due to its easy to form/print during manufacture.

Not easy to break

Flammable

One of the disadvantages of plastic raw materials is that it is flammable. If it burns, it will be difficult to extinguish

Flex

One of the reasons plastic is widely used as a raw material is because it is flexible. This property allows objects made of plastic not to break easily. Due to these properties, plastic is the choice for making plastic toys.

See-through

Due to its translucent nature, plastic can be formed into glass, even on roofs.

Heat and electric insulator

Because of these two properties, plastic is the main raw material. Unlike iron, which conducts heat and electricity, plastic is an insulator because plastic is usually used for the outer protection of cables or security.
