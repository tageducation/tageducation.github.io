18. What is Monomial Expression(Your own words or opinion) What is Binomial? What is Polynomial? Your own words or opinion.


Answer:What is Monomial Expression?

Monomial Expression is a term with numbers like 1, 2, 3 and variables like x, y, and z. Monomial Expression only has one term, meaning, we will not find operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

Examples of Monomial Expression are:

2x7y²-5q13mn²x³What is Binomial?

Binomial is an algebraic expression wherein it only consists of 2 terms. We can see from binomial that we use mathematical operations such as addition, subtraction, multiplication and also division.

Examples of Binomial:

2x + 77y² - 5z-5q - 213mn² + 7rx³ - 12xWhat is Polynomial?

Polynomial is an expression with a set of terms. It can have one term, two terms, three, terms or even have 4 terms but there are rules to be followed to classify a polynomial.  Examples of rules are:

1. A polynomial must not have negative exponents.

2. A polynomial must not have denominators of variables.

3. A polynomial must not have radicals or square roots.

4. A polynomial must not have fractional exponents.

Examples of Polynomials:

13mn² + 7rx³ - 12x13mn²5xy + 2x + 3y - 5xyz² - 5x + 2y - 2
