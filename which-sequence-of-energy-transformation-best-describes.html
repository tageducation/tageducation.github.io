Problem:

which sequence of energy transformation best describes what happens when you switch on your battery run radio? ​

The battery have a stored chemical energy which can be converted into some other forms of energy. When you turn-on the radio, chemical energy of the radio converted into electrical energy as current flows in the wire. Electrical energy converted to sound energy using the speaker. As you continue to use the radio, you will observed that the battery and other electrical component of the radio heat-up, which means thermal energy is also produce. If you observed a light in a certain part of the radio, it means electrical energy is converted to light energy.

                                      Thermal energy

                                            ↑

chemical energy → electrical energy → sound energy

             ↓                            ↓

Thermal energy           Light energy  → thermal energy

Which sequence of energy transformation best describes what happens when you prepare scrambled egg using an egg beater.

Answer:

If the egg beater is powered up by electricity:

Chemical energy form power plant → converted to electrical energy → converted to mechanical energy of an egg beater. Heat energy will also be produced in using the egg beater.

If the egg beater is not powered by electricity like whisk:

Chemical energy from a person → Converted to Kinetic energy in beating the egg → Heat energy will also be produce causing a person to sweat.

Which sequence of energy transformation best describes what happens when you switch on your battery-run radio?

Answer:

Chemical Energy – Electrical Energy – Sound Energy

​

Explanation:

The chemical energy that is stored in the battery may be turned into various types of energy. As current runs over the wire, the chemical energy of the radio is transformed into electrical energy when it is turned on. Using the speaker, electrical energy is transferred to sound energy.
