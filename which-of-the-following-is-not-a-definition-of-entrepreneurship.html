27. Give 10 definition of terms in entrepreneurship


Answer:

1. Opportunity Recognition: The ability to identify a problem or need and recognize it as an opportunity.

2. Value Proposition: The unique solution that a business offers to solve a problem for a customer.

3. Business Model: The way a business makes money, by selling products or services to customers.

4. Market Research: The process of gathering information about a target market, in order to better understand their needs and how to serve them.

5. Competitive Analysis: The process of evaluating the strengths and weaknesses of competing businesses in order to gain a competitive advantage.

6.SWOT Analysis: A tool used to evaluate the Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats of a business.

7. Business Plan: A document that outlines the goals, strategies, and financial projections of a business.

8. Funding: The process of raising money to finance a business.

9. Mentorship: A relationship in which an experienced business person provides guidance and support to a less experienced entrepreneur.

10. Exit Strategy: A plan for how a business owner will eventually leave the business.
