Knowledge Process Outsourcing (KPO)Information Technology-Enabled Services (ITES)Back Office Outsourcing

Definitions:

• Knowledge Process Outsourcing (KPO) is the process of outsourcing knowledge intensive activities that are data driven and encompass the process of gathering, managing, analysing and delivering objective insights into businesses.

Examples are: Financial consultants, Research and development (R&D), Business operations (management consulting), Technical analysis.

• IT enabled Services (ITeS), also called web enabled services or remote services or Tele-working, covers the entire gamut of operations which exploit information technology for improving efficiency of an organization.

Examples are medical transcription, back-office accounting, insurance claim, credit card processing and many more.

• Back office outsourcing is defined as the process where the back-office functions of an organization are outsourced to a third-party vendor that is specialized in handling all BPO activities.

Examples are finance and accounting, data entry, payrolls, HR management, marketing, data management, personal assistant roles, and IT management.
