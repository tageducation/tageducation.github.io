23. Non-factual Statement​


Answer:

A sentence is about a non-factual matter if it is about something such that there is no one truth of the matter. That is, we can agree to disagree, saying each of us is correct. 

Statements about non-factual matters are called VALUE or PRESCRIPTIVE statements, because they reflect certain VALUES.
