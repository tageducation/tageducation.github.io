12. what percent of 45 is 9​


Step-by-step explanation:

The percentage of 45 that is equal to 9 is 20%. You can calculate this by dividing 9 by 45 and multiplying the result by 100%. The formula is: (9/45)*100% = 20%.

Concept:

Base Rate and Percentage

where the percentage is a part of a base and the rate is what percent is the part of that percentage

Given:

Base = 45

Rate = x (missing)

Percentage = 9

Formula

Percentage = Base * Rate

Solution

9 = 45x (divide 45 both sides)

x = 0.2

Convert to decimal to rate

0.2 x 100%

= 20%

Therefore, 9 is 20% of 45
