22. How do you now perceive team sports in relation to your life and your family fitness


I perceive team sports in relation to life and family fitness as very important. First, team sports encourage families to be healthy because everyone can participate. Also, on an individual level, any type of sport that can increase cardiovascular endurance is good for the health. Apart from this, a family engaging in team sports will also have quality bonding time. This makes them closer to one another.

The following are sports that are good for families:

cycling yoga tennis basketball volleyball running camping

These sports benefit each individual member because it promotes the body's well-being and health. When we exercise, we also release certain hormones that combat stress. Sports also promote sportsmanship and makes children see the value of working together to achieve goals.  

The family, as a whole also benefit because camaraderie is fostered among family members. Those with conflicts can find a way to work together because of shared interests and goals. It also cultivates the spirit of health competition among family members. Apart from this, there is quality bonding time with the family members.

For example, in camping, while it may not make anyone sweat profusely as with other exercises, it will help each member think of ways to solve a limitation brought about by camping outdoors. They can also find new experience, being that they will be away from the conveniences of the home such as having no electricity and a comfortable bed.

In basketball, the members have individual roles, just like a family. Here they will appreciate that playing your role is critical to the success of the team. A point guard, for example cannot be the power forward, nor the small guard become the shooting guard as it will mess up the game strategy. If one hoards the ball for example, it will ruin the goal of the whole team.
