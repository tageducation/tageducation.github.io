Things that made Athena angry is if she felt that someone was not using their wisdom properly. For example, if Athena saw someone acting foolishly or making a bad decision, she might become angry with them. Another reason why Athena might have become angry is if she felt that someone was not following the rules of war. For example, if Athena saw someone breaking an agreement or fighting unfairly, she would become very angry. Finally, Athena could become angry if she felt that someone was not respecting her skills as a craftsman.

Athena is the Greek goddess of wisdom and war. She is the daughter of Zeus and Metis, and she was born from Zeus' head. Athena is the patron goddess of Athens and she is also associated with other city-states, such as Sparta. Athena is usually portrayed as being calm and wise, but she can also be fierce in battle.
