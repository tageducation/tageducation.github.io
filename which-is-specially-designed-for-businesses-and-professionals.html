8. Why should business professionals consider future business scenarios? Will you make use of this as a business professional in future?


Business professionals should consider future business scenarios because the world of business is unstable. When you are a business professional, you can compare yourself to a sailor. There are many waves that you might encounter and not all the waves are the same. Some waves will let you sail peacefully and others will urge you to challenge yourself and take a great risk to survive the wave.

Considering a real-life scenario, nobody had anticipated the arrival of the COVID-19 pandemic nor the vastness of the damage that it would cost several businesses like restaurants, resorts, theaters, and all other physical stores. If a businessman considered unexpected events like the pandemic, natural disasters, and such, there would be lesser chances of having losses or even bankruptcy.

As a business professional in the future, I would always make sure to consider all possible business scenarios to protect my business at all costs. I would research every piece of information that could possibly help me identify the threats, weaknesses, strengths, and opportunities related to my business. Using the SWOT analysis is only one of the main steps that a business professional can do but there is no limit to a determined person.

I believe that without proper planning and research, a business would not be successful. It is the same as taking an examination without browsing your notes and wishing for a perfect score. Indeed, it is impossible to excel in whatever you do if you do not put much effort into it and businesses are not a piece of cake.
