19. 3. Define hospitality resources. How can they be improved?​


HOSPITALITY RESOURCES:

Human Resources:

ReceptionistManagerSecurity officerHousekeepingRestaurant manager

How hospitality resources can be improved:

Maintaining service qualityCreating a positive work atmosphereImproving the good name of the companyIncreasing Company ProfitProviding training to HR

EXPLANATION:

Human Resources Available in the Hospitality Industry

The existence of human resources in the hospitality industry has many benefits, such as improving service quality, increasing company profits and so on. The human resources in the hotel industry are as follows.

Receptionist or front office on duty to serve guests at the beginning. In other words, it provides information regarding what guests need and directs the use of the facilities according to their needs.Manager in charge of managing all hotel operations. In other words, everything related to hospitality such as services, providing facilities and so on.The security duty that is in charge of ensuring the security of the hotel itself starts from entering the main door of the hotel.Room service or housekeeping is responsible for ensuring that every room in the hotel is always in a comfortable condition before being used by guests.The restaurant manager is responsible for ensuring everything related to the restaurant, such as providing food, setting tables, selecting menus and so on.

Efforts to improve hospitality resources:

1. Guarantee Quality Service

Quality human resources are very influential to improve the quality of services provided to each. In other words, the higher the quality of the employees themselves, the more classy the services received by the guests will be. Well, here the role of the hotel manager is to ensure that every employee has the awareness to do work according to the job description to the fullest. Because the guaranteed quality of service can also minimize the occurrence of complaints from guests.

2. Creating a Positive Work Atmosphere

Another advantage of having quality human resources in the hospitality industry is that it can create a positive work atmosphere. On the other hand, a very positive work atmosphere also greatly affects the performance of the employees themselves. That way a more comfortable work environment will also support the quality of the work done.

3. Improve the Company's Good Name

The existence of quality hotel employees is also very influential to improve the good name of the hotel. Of course, to achieve this as a manager or company owner, you must also pay attention to the employees themselves by giving them their rights. That way employees will be happy and eager to serve guests in a very professional manner.

4. Increase Company Profit

Another advantage of having quality human resources in the hospitality industry is that it can increase company profits. Starting from providing good service to guests, providing very complete and comfortable facilities and infrastructure.

5. The Importance of Providing Training to Hospitality Human Resources

As previously explained, there are many benefits to be gained, if the employees in the hospitality industry are of high quality. To have quality employees is certainly not easy. As a manager when recruiting employees, you must also ensure from the experience they have. After being accepted, the employee must also get more attention such as by providing training, workshops and training.
