11. Give 5 example of I respect others when ​


What is respect?

Respect is when you admire and appreciate others no matter what. It results from the skills, qualities, attributes, or achievements that they possess and hold. Respect is when you give importance to others, and while seeing their differences upon you, you appreciate them. Respect can happen in many aspects of life, and everyone has their way of morals and values representing this approach. Respect can come from different myriads, whether family, friends, loved ones, or even strangers. Sometimes respect occurs mainly in an unexpected person, wherein somehow most people forget to respect themselves and loved ones.  Respect is a significant value, morals, and attitude that we Filipinos most nurture characteristics.

Here are five examples for these attributes:

I respected others when they knew how to put boundaries and limitations with their action. I admire people who love to let people mind their own business instead of forming a false story that isn't right.I respected people who knew how to respect older adults like our Lola and Lolo. They value the Filipino culture like 'mano po'. This is a lovely gesture that I always wanted to see.I respected people when they knew how to control their judgment rather than being insensitive and inconsiderate. People who knew how to manage their thoughts and keep what was inside their heads when they knew it would not benefit others.I respect people who don't waste time and give importance to time management, which is a good characteristic. I do want to be with people who constantly complain and can't accept the mistakes they make.Lastly, I respect people when they can sacrifice their happiness and success for others. That is the most selfless thing that I will always give my high respect, and that is the respect that I will give to my parents.
