1. Pertains to anything used as inputs to production or manufacturing


Factors of Production

Factors of production refers to anything used as inputs to production or manufacturing.

Factors of production can also be interpreted as many things that still have to do with the production process. Starting from input, the production of goods/services and all things that are smooth in the production process.

Factors

• Labor Factors (HR)

The most basic production factor for success is the labor factor or the Human Resources factor. The production process will not be produced if there is no human power. Especially if the form of business is large scale.

• Natural Resources Factors (SDA)

Especially for business actors who need raw materials from Natural Resources. They will definitely depend. Just imagine if natural resources are no longer available, the production process will stop.

• Capital factor

the third is capital.

It should also be noted that the factors of capital production can be in the form of money, machines, and buildings.

• Entrepreneurial Factor

The fourth factor of production is the entrepreneurial factor. It turns out that not everyone has a high entrepreneurial spirit. Not everyone has the perspective of being an entrepreneurial spirit. Therefore, if you want to run a lasting business, it is very important to understand and learn the concept of entrepreneurship.

• Technology and information factors

In terms of internal smoothness, many factors of production are also directly integrated with technology. Especially for large companies, where everything is done using robots and is computerized.

The reason why you have to master information technology in the production process is because it makes it easier, saves time, saves energy or saves labor costs. So when the production process runs and has been programmed by technology, human labor can immediately focus on management.
