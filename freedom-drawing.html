The bird inside the cage simply implies that being wealthy and living a luxurious existence while lacking true freedom, joy, or contentment is not the true meaning of life. Other meaning of the bird in a cage is because of his wealth, she married her husband, and now she lives her lonely existence with him in their empty mansion like a bird in a cage. The cage denotes imprisonment or oppression, whereas the bird stands for freedom or the desire for freedom.

What is freedom?

Freedom is defined as either having the capacity to act or change without restriction or having the strength and resources to pursue one's goals without restriction. The capacity to make our own decisions is freedom.

We are free to express our opinions openly, make our own life decisions, and associate with whoever we choose. This enables us to live distinctive lives and be unique persons. Nobody can attempt to end your life, not even the government, according to this. Furthermore, it implies that the state must take the necessary steps to safeguard life by passing laws that will keep you safe and, in some instances, by intervening to save your life if it is in danger.

Freedom is the capacity to express myself whatever I like. No set standard exists for how my life ought to be. I can pick my own moral compass and make my own decisions if I have freedom. I can express myself freely while I'm free.
