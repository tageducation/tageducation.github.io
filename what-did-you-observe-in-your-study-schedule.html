3c. THE ACTIVITIES ON MY STUDY PLAN I WANT TO IMPROVE:

I accustom myself to learning a lot rather than relaxing too much. Because I believe that the habit of studying hard will slowly emerge in you. So I want to improve it by adding a quality schedule.

What are Study Habits?

Study habits are behaviors that students do repeatedly in following lessons and completing their learning tasks. Study habits are learning behaviors that are formed in students along with the learning process that is carried out because students learn and observe from the surrounding environment, not talents that have been possessed by students since childhood. Through a learning process that will form student learning habits consciously or not and the process takes place continuously. The learning process is carried out by students when they are at home or school. This is done by students to obtain satisfactory learning outcomes.
