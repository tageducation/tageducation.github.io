17. ano ba ang plagiarism​


Plagiarism is the use of someone else's work or ideas without giving them credit. The first form of plagiarism is copying someone's work without crediting them. This is direct plagiarism. Another form is self-plagiarism, which is reusing your own work without crediting yourself. Finally, there is mosaic plagiarism. It is when you use someone else's ideas, but put them in your own words. This can be tricky to spot but it is still considered plagiarism. While plagiarism is often thought of as something done on purpose, it can also be accidental. If you forget to cite a source or misquote someone, you may be guilty of plagiarism without meaning to be. This is why it's so critical to be careful when using sources and to make sure you give credit where it's due.
