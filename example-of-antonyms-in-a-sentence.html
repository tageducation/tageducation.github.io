WHAT ARE ANTONYMS

Words with different meanings.

TYPES OF ANTONYMS

1. Graded Antonyms

These are the collections of word pairs that indicate difference between the two opposites, such as small and large are antonyms,  

Example: Huge, Big, little

2. Relational Antonyms

These are the collections of word pairs that demonstrate the connection between two opposites,  

Example: Front: Back, Uncle: Aunt,

3. Complimentary Antonyms

These are the collections of word pairs that have no meaning at all.

Example: On:Off, Male:Female
