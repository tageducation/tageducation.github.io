Scenario or Situation for each Speech Style

Intimate – these are speech styles that we use with people who are close or dear to us. This style of speech is often without inhibitions and thus usually occurs in private. This can also be grammatically incorrect and informal.

Example:

Soobin found Yeonjun’s shirt in his closet.

Soobin: Hey! Take this back ASAP.

Yeonjun: Wha–Why is my shirt here, you bunny!?

Soobin: I don’t Know, why don’t you ask the one who did the l4undry?

Yeonjun: Oh, right. Thanks, any way! See ya!

Consultative – This speech style encourages the audience to engage and contribute to the communication. This is best used in developing connections and bonds.

Example:

Taehyun observes that Kai is a bit gloomy today.

Taehyun: How are you feeling today?

Kai: Me? Oh, I’m fine, thank you for asking!

Taehyun: Please tell me if you are not feeling well, okay?

Kai: That’s so nice of you, thank you. I will keep that in mind.

Formal – This is a one-way style of speech where the audience is not obliged but they can interact and participate with the speaker.

Example:

Weather Announcements, News Reports, State of the Nation Address

Frozen – A speech style where the audience cannot interact with the speaker. The speech here never changes and uses static l4nguage and sentences that are long and with proper grammar.

Example:

The singing of the National Anthem, Readings of the L4st Will and Testament, Announcement of sentences at the court, etc.

Casual – The speech style used words used here can be easily understood by peers and acquaintances.  This includes a day-to-day conversation with friends and inside jokes.

Example:

JM and MJ talking about the voting season in Kpop.

JM: Hey, did you vote on MAMA yet?

MJ: No, I’m finishing my edits on Tik-Tok first, you know, for promo.

JM: Okay, you should just join the mass baking l4ter then!

MJ: Sure, let me know the time. Let me know if you also want to be tagged in my MSD l4ter.
