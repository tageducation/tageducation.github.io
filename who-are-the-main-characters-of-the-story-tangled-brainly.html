5. who are the main character of tangled story​


The Main Characters of Tangled

The main characters of Tangled are Rapunzel, Mother Gothel, Flynn Rider, Pascal, and Maximus.

Further explanation:1. Rapunzel

She was taken by the witch Gothel, who had been using the flower to stay young forever after being born to a queen who needed the power of a magic flower to come to terms with her. Rapunzel is kept imprisoned in a hidden tower by the witch after learning that Rapunzel's hair now has the flower's power unless it is cut. Until the thief Flynn Rider (Eugene Fitzherbert) tries to use Rapunzel's tower as a hideout, Rapunzel is unaware of her history. She strikes a deal with him to return the Princess' crown he took in exchange for taking her to see the floating lanterns that had come a year on her birthday.

2. Mother Gothel

Mother Gothel is a conceited old woman who hoards the potent healing properties of a magical gold flower in order to live for a very long time and maintain her youthful beauty. Gothel had a bad parenting style and was capable of doing terrible things. She was a villain because she was calculating, crafty, and self-centered.

3. Eugene Fitzherbert, a.k.a. Flynn Rider

The movie's Deuteragonist, a career burglar on the lam after robbing the lost princess of her tiara, ends up trying to skulk in her tower. Without any prior exposure to the outside world, Rapunzel persuades him to go with her to see the lanterns.

4. Pascal

The loyal companion and pet chameleon of Rapunzel. Dives into the Demanitus Device in “Queen For A Day," almost loses his life, and is hurt as he clears the stone blocking the gears.”

5. Maximus

Unfortunately, the Captain of the Guard's horse is more capable than the captain himself. He commits himself to find Flynn at the start of the film in order to stop him from obtaining the tiara. He later serves as the mascot for the Royal Guard.
