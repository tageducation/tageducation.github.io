  The answer is C. Rising of sea level. It is the one of the indication of global warming because when the global temperature increases, sea water expands, which gives ocean base to expand and have more space to increase water levels. The rate of glacier melting will also be faster thus resulting to increase in overall sea level.

Further explanation

  Sea level will continue to rise depends on the rate of carbon dioxide and rate of global warming. If global temperature will continue to warm it has high possibility of sea level increases. And how fast it will rise is depends on the rate of glacier melting.  The effect of increase in sea level includes flood risk, high tide, destruction erosion, agricultural soil contamination, loss of habitat for fish, coral, sea creatures and even land creatures. It may also even wipe out cities which are located in low sea level. This will give serious problem in many coastal communities, and it is only expected to get much worse in the future with continued rising seas. Since we have many nearby houses near on coastal areas many of them will be affected if global warming will continue.

  Sea level rises may cause various impacts to human being. First is global refugee crisis which coastal areas are submerged in water and cannot continue living on that area. Secondly, economies will be destroyed due to infrastructural damage, agricultural will also be damaged and destruction of coastal hubs activities. Third one is food and water crisis because of contaminated of salt water due to submerged of coastal areas. And lastly the health crisis due to related illness in water and sanitation like cholera and diarrhea. Global warming has many effects in all areas. This is the reason why we need to prevent it by starting in ourselves and in our home.
