A character is a person, or sometimes an animal, who takes part in the action of a short story or other literary work.

Answer: Conflict

The conflict is a struggle between two people or things in a short story. The main character is usually on one side of the central conflict.

Answer: Setting

Setting is the “where and when” of a story. It is the time and place during which the story takes place.

Falling action occurs towards the end of the story, when the tension decreases. Often during the falling action phase, conflicts may be resolved and everything in the story begins to settle down.
