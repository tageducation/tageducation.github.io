12. Which of the following ways of telling time made use of sand? (Literal)A.sundialsB. hourglassC. clock D.water clocks​


The ways that can tell the time using sand is ( B.hourglass ).

An hourglass or also called a glass tube is a time measuring device consisting of two round glass cups connected to a narrow tube that will regulate the fall of the sand. An hourglass consists of three parts, the top, the bottom, and a narrow cavity that connects the top to the bottom. To measure time, a certain amount of sand is placed at the top which will immediately flow to the bottom through the narrow cavity. The time is determined based on the duration of the entire sand transfer process from the top to the bottom.

     One of the tubes will be filled with sand, the sand will slowly descend through the narrow tube that connects the two glass balls. Hourglasses usually set the time for 1 hour, if the sand in the top tube moves all the way down then one hour has elapsed. But the hourglass can also set a shorter time, the length of the hourglass depends on the size of the glass and the amount of sand that is put into it.
