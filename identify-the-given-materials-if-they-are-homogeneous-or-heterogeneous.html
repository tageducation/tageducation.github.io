Answer:

Milk: HomogeneousHydrogen: CompoundSand mixed with water: HeterogeneousCarbon Dioxide: CompoundMilk and oatmeal: Heterogeneous

Explanation:

A heterogeneous mixture is a mixture composed of two or more chemical substances in which the different components can be distinguished. For example, oil and water, salt and oil, sand and water. Meanwhile, a homogeneous mixture is a mixture composed of a solution that has the same properties. For example, milk. A compound mixture is when two or more chemical elements are combined like Water (H20), Sodium Bicarbonate (NaHCO3), and etc.
