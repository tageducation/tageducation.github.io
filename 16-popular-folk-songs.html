Examples of Other Folk Music:

Most folk music does not have such a strong national focus, though it may have an easily identifiable national character. Instead some folk songs may tell a simple story (such as "boy meets girl") or express universal themes such as love or regret. Other folk songs may tell a story (either a fictional story or an historical one) while in other examples the meaning of the lyrics may be obscure. Finally another category of folk music is instrumental in nature, and such folk tunes may have originated as dances with no lyrics.
