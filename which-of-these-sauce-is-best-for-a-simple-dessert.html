7. Which of the following sauces is suited to a simple dessert? a. cold sauce b. hot fudge sauce c. light sauce d. rich sauce


Answer:

d. rich sauce

Explanation:

Kinds and Varieties of Sauces

Rich sauce is well suited to a simple dessert.Light sauce is suited to a rich dessert. Hot fudge is a delightful contrast to a cold cornstarch pudding or to vanilla ice cream. Hot sauces are made just before they are to be used. Cold sauces are cooked ahead of time, then cooled, covered and put in the refrigerator to chill.

To preserve the richness of desserts yet keep cooking simple during the holiday rush, stock up on dessert sauces. A vivid, rich sauce dramatically frames simple purchased desserts like angel food cake, frozen yogurt or fresh fruit.
