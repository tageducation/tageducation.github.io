9. compose inverted sentences by adding a verb and a subject to the following phrases. Make sure that the subject and verb agree. 1. inside the chest __________2. seated beside me___________3. scattered along the beach __________4. there_____________5. above the hill ___________​


INVERTED SENTENCESInside the chest, were the treasures of our family. Seated beside me, is my long lost friend. Scattered along the beach, are petals of red roses.  There, are the toys that my sister always wants to grab. Above the hill, resides my favorite teacher in Elementary. WHAT IS AN INVERTED SENTENCE?

An inverted sentence is a sentence where the verb or the predicate comes first before the subject. In a normal or natural way of making a sentence, the subject comes first before the verb or the predicate so when the verb or the predicate comes first, it is called inverted.

EXAMPLE:

Natural sentence

The girl in red won the first prize of the raffle.

Inverted sentence

The first prize of the raffle was won by the girl in red.

Explanation:

The girl in red is the subject.

Was won is the verb.
