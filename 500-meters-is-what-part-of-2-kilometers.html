Step-by-step explanation:

Converting Meters to Kilometers

There are two steps to find out what part of 2 kilometers is 500 meters. First, convert kilometers to meters. Second, divide it by 500 meters.

Solution:

Step 1: Convert km to m.

1 km = 1000 m

2 × 1000 = 2000 m

There are 2000 m in 2 km.

Step 2: Divide.

2000 m ÷ 500 m = 4

Since there are four 500 m in 2 km, it is ¼ of it.
