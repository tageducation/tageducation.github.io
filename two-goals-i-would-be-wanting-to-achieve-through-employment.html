1. two goals I would be wanting to achieve through employment​


Two goals I would be wanting to achieve through employment​:

1. Meeting Social Needs

2. Looking for a Career and Position

EXPLANATION:

Usually the purpose of a person looking for work or job is to earn income. Although the real purpose of working is not just to earn income, but there are many goals and each person has different goals.

1. Meeting Social Needs

There are employees who work to meet social needs by establishing work relationships. Employees with goals like this will think that work relationships are long-term investments that will be very useful in the future. Usually employees like this are very happy if they can establish relationships and mingle with other employees who come from various different backgrounds.

2. Looking for a Career and Position

The ultimate goal of work is to find a career and position. Of course, at work there are employees who have ambitions in pursuing career and position advancement. In general, people work from the lower level first, namely to become staff, then increase to a better position in the company. Later, over time, the abilities or skills possessed by employees will certainly increase and they can gain trust as managers who have subordinates. These employees will usually try to be more active and work optimally in order to get career and position advancement.

14. three influence I would consider in choosing employment​


1. Job offer

You must take into account how much the company can offer you based on your credibility and qualifications.  Does the company give you an offer that can compensate all of your skills, abilities, and given the background that you can offer them in return?

2. Culture

The company’s culture can affect drastically affect your performance over time.  Hence, you have to be observant during your interview.  This shall give you an overview to what you can expect once you become a part of the company.

3. Non-wage satisfaction from the job and the company

This shall include your day-to-day satisfaction while performing your job and meeting the company’s expectations of you.  You may be in a low paying job but the value, growth and fulfillment that you experience are far more sufficient for you.  Are you happy at work?  Do you see yourself growing professionally?
