Explanation:

Gender identities and expressions are the ways in which a person identifies and/or expresses their gender, including self-image, appearance, and

an embodiment of gender roles. One’s sex is usually assigned at birth based on  one’s physical biology. One’s gender is one’s internal sense of self and  identity. One’s gender expression (e.g. masculine, feminine, androgynous, etc.) is how one embodies gender  attributes, presentations, roles, and more.

Equality and freedom from discrimination are fundamental human rights that belong to everyone, regardless of their sexual orientation, gender identity, or sex characteristics. 11. How can you know if your a bisexual or pansexual?You see im like so confused of my gender.. I like boys and girls the same time!!Pls help me guess my gender.Im confusedYung maayos po thank you​


Pansexuality is sexual, romantic, or emotional attraction towards people regardless of their sex or gender identity. Pansexual people may refer to themselves as gender-blind, asserting that gender and sex are not determining factors in their romantic or sexual attraction to others.

Bisexuality is romantic attraction, sexual attraction, or sexual behavior toward both males and females, or to more than one gender. It may also be defined to include romantic or sexual attraction to people regardless of their sex or gender identity, which is also known as pansexuality.
