14. How can I use statistics and probability to help others make informed decisions about diet and health ?


Statistics, which is linked to probability, can help people understand their risks for certain diseases. For example, statistics show that people who have high salt intake are more likely to have raised blood pressure, which is a major risk for heart disease and stroke. This data can help an individual choose if he should decrease his salt intake or not.
