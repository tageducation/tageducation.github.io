It was a blessing in disguise that I arrived late in the meeting10. The newly wed couple has dime a dozen like and dislikes​


-----

1. So far so good -- to express satisfaction with the way that a situation or activity is developing or happening.

2. Let the cat out of the bag -- to reveal  a secret.

3. Hit the hay -- to get into bed and go to sleep.

4. Straight from the horse's mouth -- from the original or most reliable source.

5. Hit two birds with one stone -- to solve two problems with one action or solution.

6. A thing of beauty is a joy forever -- something beautiful will give pleasure long after it ceases to exist.

7. Asia's song bird -- (sadly, I don't know its definition)

8. World of make-believe -- a state of believing things that are not true.

9. Blessing in disguise -- something that at first appears to be bad or unlucky but is actually good.

10. Dime a dozen -- when we want to express that something is of a low value, or very common.
