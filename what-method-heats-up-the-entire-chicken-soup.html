23. In what direction does heat travel through the soup?


Convection because the heat is transferred by the particles themselves. The particles in the soup is moving around. Heat from the bottom part of the water is transferred to the upper part through convection. As the water gets warmer, it expands and become lighter and so it rises to the top of the cooler water. This will then be replaced by the cooler water that goes down from above, which will in turn become warmer and also will rise to the top.
