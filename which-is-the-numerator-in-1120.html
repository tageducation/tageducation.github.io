3. which is the numerator in 11/20?


The numerator in ‌11⁄20‌ is 11.

Step-by-step explanation:

Fractions

The representation of the equal parts of a whole is known as a fraction. A fraction is composed of two parts. They are the numerator and the denominator. The number above the line is the numerator. It tells the number of equal parts taken from a whole. The number at the bottom of the line is the denominator. It tells the total number of equal parts.

Let us use the fraction above as an example. The fraction ‌11⁄20‌ can be illustrated in the figure below.
