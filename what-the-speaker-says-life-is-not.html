2. 1.what the speaker says life is not (A Psalm of Life)


Answer:

The speaker says our life is full uncertainties. Everyday we experience differing situations, sometimes good and sometimes bad, sometimes happy, sometimes not, but life must keep moving on.

Explanation:

To explain further, the phrase/poem A Psalm of Life glorifies life and its possibilities. It is an invocation to the mankind to follow the path of righteousness, the right way to live this life. The poet, Henry Wadsworth Longfellow asks us not to waste our precious time and to make the most out of this short and transitory life.  Thus, it is only ignorance which is the inability of art of enjoyment. God created this world with happiness and misery. All we need to do is live our lives to the fullest rather than save ourselves for the afterlife, using the short time we have here on Earth as a gift. This is also a message for future generations to find work and action that gives them purpose and passion.
