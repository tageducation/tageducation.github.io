15. No. 1: Refer to the discussion above about Plate Tectonic theory . write a short paragraph regarding the movement of tectonic plates​


Answer:

Plate Tectonics

The earth is divided into three main parts – the core, the mantle, and the crust. The crust is the outermost layer of the earth, and it is also the thinnest. There are two types of crust – the denser oceanic crust, and the less dense continental crust. The earth’s crust is further divided into different plates, which move constantly.

Explanation:

When two plates collided with one another, we call its boundary as a convergent plate boundary. There are three main types of convergent boundary:

Continental-continental convergent boundary – this one creates mountain ranges, like the Himalayas Continental-oceanic convergent boundary – this one creates volcanic arcs, like the Andes Oceanic-oceanic convergent boundary – this one creates island arcs, like the Philippines

When two plates move away from each other, we call its boundary as a divergent plate boundary. Lastly, when two plates slide past each other, we call its boundary as a transform plate boundary.

1. Convergent boundaries: where two plates are colliding.

2. Divergent boundaries – where two plates are moving apart.

3. Transform boundaries – where plates slide passed each other.
