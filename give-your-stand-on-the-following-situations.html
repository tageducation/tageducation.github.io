Answer:

 

1. Students must be given all considerations and shall be promoted to the next grade level even if they fail to comply with the necessary academic requirements.

This situation should depend on how much did the student fail. Say, for example, he only failed 3 subjects, summer classes should be enough or additional classes for the next level should be required. But, if the students failed most of the subjects, then he must retain them. So, I disagree that a student should complete not be promoted if he fails the GPA or average.

 

2. Education is the primary key to a better future.

I agree that education is still a key to a better future. Having education gives a person edge for success.
