1. 4 score = ______ mo. ​


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebra and basic mathematics specially in converting different units of lenght and other measurements.

Given:

4 score

Problem:

Convert into months

Solution:

In this case, there is no direct conversion of score to months. You must convert score into years first, the. convert years into months.

A score refers to a span of 20 years.

1 score = 20 years

Convert score to year

4 score × 20 years/1score

= 80 years

Convert year to month

1 year = 12 months

80 years × 12 months/1 year

= 960 months

Answer: 960 months

22. Convert 4 score = ____ mo. ​


Answer:

There are 960 months in 4 scores.

Step-by-step explanation:

This is an example of a conversion unit that deals with time. Remember that in one year, there are 12 months.

For this case, we don't have an actual conversion from a score into a month. We need it to express in years first, then translate it to months.

Some Time Unit ConversionMinutesHoursDayDegreesMonthYear

Note that 1 score is equivalent to 20 years. We can use this conversion to convert a score into a month.

So, there are 80 years in 4 scores, that is, [tex]4 \times 20 = 80[/tex].

Note that there are 12 months in a year. So, we simply multiply 80 by 12.

                   [tex]80 \times 12 = 960\\[/tex]

In other words, there are 960 months in 4 scores.
