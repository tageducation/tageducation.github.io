Here are the features in an academic text.

There are various kinds of text, and one of them is academic text. As the name suggests, an academic text explains academic results or discussions, and most of them are written in the form of articles or thesis. There are certain features that can be ascribed to an academic text, several of which are:

The language use is objective. What I mean by objective language here is that the authors use words that will not incite ambiguity towards the readers. In addition, it can be assumed that there will not be any opinions contained in the text.Everything is scientific. As opposed to literary text, an academic text is mostly scientific, and there are rules that makes a text scientific. For example, it won' delve with opinions of the writers, etc.It aims to inform. By reading an academic text, the readers will get informed about the topic being talked about in the text.
