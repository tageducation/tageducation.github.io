19. 33. In which layer do virtually all weather phenomena take place?A. ExosphereB. MesosphereC. StratosphereD. Troposphere​


₊˚ ଘ Answer

D. Troposphere

Virtually all weather phenomena take place in the troposphere layer.

₊˚ ଘ Explanation

Let's learn about the

TROPOSPHERE LAYER!

The Earth's atmosphere is comprised of 5 layers, namely the troposphere, stratosphere, mesosphere, thermosphere, and exosphere (from bottom to top).

The troposphere is the lowest layer of the Earth's atmosphere; above it is the stratosphere. It is the thinnest layer out of all five, but this is where very important things happen -- it is where we breathe and go on with our daily lives!

The troposphere is also where the clouds are, which subsequently means that virtually all weather phenomena take place there.
