11. Scenario Locutionary (Utterance) Illocutionary (Intention) Perlocutionary (Response) Renato lost his wallet. The children were crossing the busy street. Anton could not find his eraser for the Math test. Mae accidentally slipped on the floor.pahelp​


Answer:

1. Renato lost his wallet.

a.  Locutionary: When was the last time you had your wallet?

b.  Illocutionary: I will help you find your wallet.

c.  Perlocutionary: You must remember all the places you’ve been to when you brought out your wallet.

2. Anton could not find his eraser for the Math test.

a. Locutionary: Don’t you have an extra eraser?

b. Illocutionary: I will lend you, my eraser.

c. Perlocutionary: Tell the proctor that you lost your eraser.

3. The children were crossing the busy street.

a. Locutionary: Tell the children to look right and left for sights of car.

b. Illocutionary: Be careful when crossing the street!

c. Perlocutionary: Watch out for the red light or cars might hit you.

4. Mae accidentally slipped on the floor.

a. Locutionary: Are you hurt?

b. Illocutionary: Here, I’ll help you up.

c. Perlocutionary: Be careful next time!
