20. Activity 3:Mugs of Marriage,Courtship, and Dating. Give at least three (3) meaning and write it on each bowl.Courtship1.2.3.Marriage1.2.3.Dating1.2.3.​


Courtship

1. the act, process, or period of courting.

2. is the period of development towards an intimate relationship wherein a couple get to know each other and decide if there will be an engagement, followed by a marriage.

3. may be an informal and private matter between two people or may be a public affair, or a formal arrangement with family approval.

Marriage

1. is the intimate union and equal partnership of a man and a woman.

2. the state of being united as spouses in a consensual and contractual relationship recognized by law.

3. is the beginning—the beginning of the family—and is a life-long commitment.

Dating

1. going out with someone

2. is a stage of romantic relationships whereby two people meet socially with the aim of each assessing the other's suitability as a prospective partner in an intimate relationship.

3. a form of romantic courtship typically between two individuals with the aim of assessing the other's suitability as a partner
