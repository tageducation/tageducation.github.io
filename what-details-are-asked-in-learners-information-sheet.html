Learner's Information Sheet

The details asked on the learner's information sheet are name, school of origin, LRN (Learner Reference Number), grade and section, sex, birthdate, birthplace, home address, telephone number, father's name and his occupation, mother's name and her occupation, number of brothers and sisters, school last attended, and skills of the learner who wants to enroll at Aplaya Elementary School.Filling in the blanks or spaces contained in the learner's information sheet is very important especially when this form is used to enroll at Aplaya Elementary School. Learner's information sheet is important to fill in because it is used to explain personal data, future plans, achievements, values and abilities and interests of the learners.Explanation

Before a prospective learner enrolls in a school, the prospective learner is required to fill out a sheet containing the learner's personal data for the benefit of the learner's administration at the school. In general, the details that are required to be filled in the sheet given to prospective learners are name, school, LRN (Learner Reference Number), grade and section, sex, birthdate, birthplace, home address, telephone number, father's name and his occupation, mother's name and her occupation, number of brothers and sisters, school last attended, interests, future plans, and skills possessed by prospective learners.

The Learner's information sheet is important to fill out because it is used to explain the personal data, future plans, achievements, values and abilities and interests of the learners.
