9/5 is the mixed fraction in the choices. Why? Let's take a look at every choices.

a. 714

714 is a whole number, right? It doesn't have any numerator or denominator, but it has only 1 number in it.

b. 1/5

1/5 is considered as proper fraction, since it is already simplified and has a numerator and denominator.

c. 9/5

We can conclude that 9/5 is an improper fraction, but do you know that it is also a mixed fraction? It is because this number has a higher value of numerator than to its denominator. If we are going to simplify it, the answer will be: 1 3/5.

d. 5/8

5/8 is also a proper fraction, since it is simplified and has numerator and denominator.
