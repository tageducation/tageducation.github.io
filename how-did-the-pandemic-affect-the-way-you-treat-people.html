I have gained a lot of experience in being appreciated by someone, one of which is the experience of mutual respect in terms of religion, namely tolerance in both the family and community circles. Therefore we must be devoted and appreciate the hard work of both parents who have worked hard to finance all our needs. Because for parents, children are number one for them. Why do we have to respect each other? because we cannot live alone, humans live in society, we live by interacting with others and because we know how it feels not to be appreciated by others, therefore we must first respect others, surely other people will also respect us too.

2. How I keep harmony with others especially during time of pandemic:

I create routines with my family and other people. It's important to establish and maintain some sort of routine. Ensuring regular bedtime, getting up on time, making your bed, and eating nutritious food are also important. Scheduling time off can also prevent conflict between myself and others. Lack of sleep or irregular routines can actually stress me out, which can impact my relationships and those of others.
