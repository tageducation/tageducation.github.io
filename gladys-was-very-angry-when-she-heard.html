23. 1. Mara feels elated when she won the competition. a. angry b. happy C. sad


QUESTION:

1. Mara feels elated when she won the competition.

ANSWER:

B. HAPPY

DEFINITION

The word "elate" means being happy.

EXAMPLE SENTENCES USING THE WORDJessa elates when she get a perfect score in test.Mario and Mark elate if they see their friends happy, too.

TIPS TO FIND OUT THE MEANING OF A UNKNOWN WORD

This sentence is actually "obvious", since we used a technique, which is called "context clues".

What is Context Clues?

It is simply the clue for you to know the word, which is written in what you are reading. There are many forms of context clues like:

synonymantonymroot worddefinition

Context Clues makes the word unknown be known and unravel its meaning.

In our sentence, the word "elate" is the synonym of the word "happy". It is also obvious that if you won a competition, you will feel "happy".
