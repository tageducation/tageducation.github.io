Pinwheel sandwiches are a tea sandwich prepared using bread slices sliced longways form a conventional square loaf which have a substantial well flavoured spread or soft filling. The spread slices are rolled up tightly, covered and rested under refrigeration to set. When set they are cut into slices for service.
