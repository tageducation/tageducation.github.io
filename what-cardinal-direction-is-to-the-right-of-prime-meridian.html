What is the Prime Meridian and why is it in Greenwich?

The Royal Observatory in Greenwich is where east meets west at Longitude 0°.The decision was based on the argument that by naming Greenwich as Longitude 0º, it would be advantageous to the largest number of people. Therefore the Prime Meridian at Greenwich became the centre of world time.
