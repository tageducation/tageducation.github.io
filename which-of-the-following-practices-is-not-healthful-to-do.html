Based on the options given, the practice that is NOT healthful to do is: C. Increase the consumption of fatty foods.

Eat vegetables and fruits daily, limit the intake of sugar and salt, and eat breakfast high in fiber like oatmeal are the healthful practices to do.

EXPLANATION:

High-fat foods are one of the enemies for our health. Unfortunately, this food also has a delicious taste and is liked by many people. Fatty foods are also very easy to find in our daily diet. Therefore, it is very easy for you to consume it and even get too much. Although fatty foods are actually needed by the body, there are certain types of lipids/fat and limits of fat for consumption. Although some foods like avocados have healthy fats that your body needs, you can also eat too much. Consuming too much fat can cause problems with your energy, digestion, and sleep habits. Reporting from Livestrong, there are a number of signs your body will show when you eat too many fatty foods. During sleep, the body usually digests food more slowly. Therefore, it is advisable not to eat too much food before going to bed. This is especially the case with high-fat foods that can make it difficult to fall asleep. Excessive consumption, especially of these foods, can cause various health problems.
