2. things to do to align with pecs of a successful entrepreneur​


Explanation:

What things to do to align with PECs of a successful entrepreneur being a competent

Successful entrepreneurs are the role model of the new aspirants entrepreneurs that is why they should follow the endeavor of those successful so that they will become successful too.

The following are the things to do to align with PECs of a successful entrepreneur:

1. The aspirant entrepreneur should benchmark the personal entrepreneurial competencies that could be learn.

• We should study the characteristics of those successful entrepreneurs such as how they achieved those entrepreneurial success. If those entrepreneurs take risk then you must also take the risk. If they never waste their time to avoid failure

The following are the personal entrepreneurial competencies of entrepreneurs that the successful entrepreneurs that should have

1. The art of influence strategies

• If the person is very influential then that person must also have a power. Successful entrepreneur can establish influence  among the customer and the public.

2. Self confidence

• An entrepreneur believes in his capacity and skills become successful than who does not.

3. Persuasion

• Persuading other people or customers can make them buy or avail products or services that we are offering therefore is is very much helpful characteristics of an entrepreneur because it can boost sale of the business.

4. Assertive

• If we want to become successful we must learn to become assertive, it means that we should instill the quality of being self-assured  and confident. We should prevent being aggressive.  

5. Problem solving skills

• Successful entrepreneurs always engage in action and looking for the solution to problems.

6.  Sees and acts on opportunities

• If there is an opportunities, an entrepreneur, do it as soon as possible

7. Do planning systematically and  concern for high quality outcome  

• An entrepreneur do management planning before anything else to prevent failure.

8. Efficient,persistence, initiative and commitment to work
