12. what is the implication to you of learning the confidentiality of data information brainly


Data confidentiality is about protecting data against unintentional, unlawful, or unauthorized access, disclosure, or theft.

Confidentiality has to do with the privacy of information, including authorizations to view, share, and use it. Information with low confidentiality concerns may be considered "public" or otherwise not threatening if exposed beyond its intended audience. Information with high confidentiality concerns is considered secret and must be kept confidential to prevent identity theft, compromise of accounts and systems, legal or reputational damage, and other severe consequences.

The difference between data and information

That data is the basic form of information, while information is an element resulting from a form of data processing. In the world of computers data is everything that is stored in memory according to a certain format. Information is the result of data processing that can be accepted by the mind of the recipient of the information which can later be used for decision making.

Basically, data is a collection of information or information about something that is obtained through observation or searching for certain sources. The data obtained but has not been processed further can become a fact or assumption.

Data are raw facts or details of events that have not been processed, which sometimes cannot be accepted by the mind of the recipient of the data, therefore the data must first be processed into information to be received by the recipient. Data can be in the form of numbers, characters, symbols, images, sounds, or signs that can be used as information. An information may become data if the information is reused for further information system processing. In the world of computers data is everything that is stored in memory according to a certain format.

Data tends to be a brief explanation or an idea that has not yet explained an event or activity result, data also cannot be used for decision making while information is the result of processing data that can be used for decision making.

Data sometimes cannot be used and accepted by the recipient's mind, while information can be useful and acceptable to the recipient's mind.Data has a more detailed scope and is technical in nature, while information produces explanations that can be used to make decisions.
