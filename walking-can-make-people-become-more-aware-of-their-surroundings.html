13. walking can make people become more aware of their surroundings ______ the cleanliness of the environment, and the friendliness of the neighborhood. A. consequently B. despite C. unfortunately D. such as​


(D.) SUCH AS is the proper transition word for the sentence. “Walking can make people become more aware of their surroundings SUCH AS the cleanliness of the environment, and the friendliness of the neighborhood.” Such as is used to further explain or give examples as described in the near phrase.

TRANSITION WORDS

Transition words are used to link dissimilar thoughts, opinions, ideas, and concepts together. It is also called linking or connecting words. Such as is one of the common transition words used. As explained earlier, such as is a transition word under the Example type.  

Other types of transition words include:

Similarity (i.e., also) Exception/Contrast (i.e., despite) Attitude (i.e., unfortunately) Reference ((i.e., former) Cause and effect (i.e., consequently) Sequence (i.e., first) Additional support/Evidence (i.e., in addition) Conclusion (i.e., therefore) Emphasis (i.e., indeed) Time (i.e., currently) Location (i.e., adjacent)
