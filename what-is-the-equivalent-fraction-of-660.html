23. What is the equivalent fraction of 6/60? A. 4/15 B. 1/15 C. 1/10 D. 3/5


Answer:

C. 1/10

What and How is it an Equivalent Fraction?

An equivalent fraction is a fraction that is equal in value.

In this problem, the numerator and denominator of 6/60 shares a common factor of 6. When divided into 6 and simplified like the picture above, it results to 1/10.

Therefore,

[tex] \frac{6}{60} = \frac{1}{10} [/tex]
