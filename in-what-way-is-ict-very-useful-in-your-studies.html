Ways That ICT IS Useful In Our Studies

ICT (Information and Communications Technology) are useful tools that can be used by both students and teachers in achieving and continuing education process. Especially, nowadays, that pandemic is still happening in our world, the use of technology and communication devices is in-demand and helpful.

Here are the reasons why:  

1. ICT devices can be used to gather information which can be used to researches and homeworks. Internet and electronic devices like computers, laptops, mobile phones can be used to browse the web and search engines to look for information that could answer questions and contribute to activities.

2. ICT devices can be used to ask questions to teachers and can be answered in a faster and easier way.

3. ICT devices can be used to stay updated in news and new information. The use of various sites and social media are helpful to stay updated in all current issues especially education and health issues.
