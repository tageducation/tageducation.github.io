Answer:

A project plan for making a pillowcase might include the following steps:

Gather materials: Assemble all of the materials you will need for the project, including fabric, thread, scissors, and a sewing machine (if you have one).

Cut fabric: Measure and cut the fabric to the desired size for the pillowcase. You will need two pieces of fabric, one for the front and one for the back.

Hem the edges: Fold the edges of the fabric over twice and sew a hem to prevent the fabric from fraying.

Sew the pillowcase together: Place the two pieces of fabric together with the right sides facing each other. Sew along three sides of the fabric, leaving one side open.

Turn the pillowcase right side out: Turn the pillowcase right side out through the open side.

Iron the pillowcase: Iron the pillowcase to smooth out any wrinkles and make it look more polished.

Insert the pillow: Insert a pillow into the pillowcase through the open side.

Close the pillowcase: Sew the open side closed to complete the pillowcase.

Enjoy your new pillowcase!

This is just one example of a project plan for making a pillowcase. Depending on your specific goals and needs, you may choose to add additional steps or modify this plan as needed.
