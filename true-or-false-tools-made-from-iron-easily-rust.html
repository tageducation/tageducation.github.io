2. True or False: Tools made from iron easily rust.


True or False: Tools made from iron easily rust.

Reduction and oxidation cause rust on metals that contain Iron. Induced by the electrolyte liquid which is water, a transformation occurs which turns Iron into Hydrated Ferric Oxide or Rust.

It is worth noting that turning into rust is the natural behavior of Iron, which means that is just the way things are. We humans, because of our structures and mechanisms, do everything we can to stop this by covering it with sealants, wiping oil on the surfaces of machines and tools to slow down or prevent the inevitable. So, the answer is True, they easily rust.
