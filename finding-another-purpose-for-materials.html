Answer:

Finding another purpose for materials is called reusing.

The difference between reusing to recycling is that reusing is simply to use the same thing again. While recycling is crafting something or breaking down unused materials to make new items.

Ex. Taking the tip of a broken hanger to make a hook. - Reusing

      Ripping garbage into pieces to make hollowblocks. - Recycling
