It belongs to Peace.

(One of the gifts or fruits of the Holy Spirit)

It's not the absence of turmoil but the presence of tranquillity even while in a place of chaos. It is a sense of wholeness and completeness that is content, knowing God controls the events of the day.

Once you have prayed and begged God for assistance, let go of the situation and place it in His hands. In Christ Jesus, the Peace of God will guard our hearts and minds, the text concludes.

The Peace that transcends all understanding comes from knowing that no matter what life throws at us, we can still have joy when we know that our victory lies in Jesus Christ and not in our circumstances.

Peace is required to attain the levels of trust, cooperation, and inclusion needed for society to be resilient to shocks, handle conflicts, and adapt to environmental changes.
