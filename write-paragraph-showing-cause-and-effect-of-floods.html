A flood is a body of water that covers land which is normally dry. Floods are common natural disasters that can affect millions of people around the world. They destroy houses and buildings, and carry soil away from valuable farming land. Floods can also contaminate drinking water and lead to diseases. They are often caused by rivers, but overflowing lakes and seas can also cause flooding.

Flooding is an overflowing of water onto land that is normally dry. Floods can happen during heavy rains, when ocean waves come on shore, when snow melts quickly, or when dams or levees break.Floods are natural occurrences in river systems. WRITING CAUSE AND EFFECTS

The Cause and Effect essays, it uses as analysis to distinguish the reasons for the situations and the output or outcome.

It has the objective of attempting and discovering the beginning or root of something, such as the event or a decision, and then connected to the results or outcome that can be properly attributed to it, or both.

When writing the essays, the first part is to focus on determining the reasoning behind the event and the connects it to the outcome.

Strong evidence is important in the cause-and-effect essay due to the complexity of determining connections between phenomena.
