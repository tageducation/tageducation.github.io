The terraces are located in the Philippine Cordillera mountain range on the northern island of Luzon in the Philippine archipelago. Although the historic terraces cover a large area, the inscribed property is made up of five clusters of the most well-preserved and spectacular terraces spread over four municipalities. They're all descendants of the Ifugao ethnic group, a small ethnic group who have lived in these mountains for thousands of years.
