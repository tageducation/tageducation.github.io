30. Execute the dance steps properly ​


Answer:

Swing. Keeping the weight on the right foot, lift the left foot and step down behind the right foot. Then step forward with the left, feet now together. Step to the side, starting with the left foot: side, step (right foot moves to left foot), step (weight is now on the left foot). Step to the right: side-step-step
