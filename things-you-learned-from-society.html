Learn from the society environment

One example of a lesson that can utilize media and learning resources based on a community or social environment is sociology, because in sociology learning there are materials about society which include social interaction, social change, social symptoms, social conflict, social norms, culture, customs. customs that exist in a society.

The process of learning activities for the use of the social environment is carried out by way of field practice or going directly to the community, such as observing, asking questions or interviewing. With this field practice, students can more easily understand or master the material, because students can know the actual situation in the society directly, and students can add experience, with experience students can more easily remember the material they have learned. got.

The results obtained from learning activities using this social environment are that students get learning in the form of factual, learning is more interesting and not boring, students can add new experiences and friends, and can help solve social problems that occur in a society. Therefore learning by utilizing the social environment as a medium and source of learning is very effective to do in sociology lessons.
