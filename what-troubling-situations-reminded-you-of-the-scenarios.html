Answer:

The images in the question show the different scenarios face by the Filipino people during the government-imposed lockdown.

Let us discuss the feelings expressed by each image depicting Covid-19 Scenarios:

For the first image:

The old woman talks about not being included in the Social Amelioration Program of the government. We can not quickly judge why she was not included but it could two things. First is that maybe the old woman is rich enough that the local government did not include her in the list of prioritized people. Second is maybe the local government corrupted the list of people to whom the SAP fund is allocated.

For most of the people with really low income, the SAP is very important for them to survive the pandemic for a few days. Not receiving it although some families they know have received it is really disturbing and it makes me angry that the local government really has the means to make money for the people.

For the second and fifth image:

The father is worried about not receiving relief goods from the government. Another scenario that is really heartbreaking during this pandemic that some fathers or breadwinners are out of jobs. They don't have the means to provide for the family and only rely on the relief goods.

For the third image:

Young people singing fondly about the lockdown. It seems that for fortunate people, the lockdown and ECQ is a way for them to have a break from being physically present at school and in the office.

For the fourth image:

People are not following the social distancing rule in a very crowded market. This is really evident in the market whenever I try to visit the wet market, everything seems to be normal like there is no pandemic happening.

For the sixth image:

Public transportations are unavailable especially during hard lockdowns or Enhanced community quarantine. This rule has handicapped businesses as the employees will not be able to go to the office due to this ruling. At the same time, Jeepney and taxi drivers became out of work.

For the seventh image:

The military personnel is strictly implementing the use of face masks and face shields.

For the eighth image:

Medical front liners are asking people to stay at home so that the virus will not anymore scatter in the Philippines.
