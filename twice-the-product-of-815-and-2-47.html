30. twice the product of 8/15 and 2 4/7​


Problem

Twice the product of 8/15 and 2 4/7

Answer2 26/35

Solution:

The problem states that we multiply the product of 8/15 and 2 4/7 by 2. Then the mathematical representation would be,

2 (8/15 × 2 4/7)

Change the mixed fraction into improper fraction

2 4/7 = [(2 × 7) + 4]/7 = (14 + 4)/7 = 18/7

Then multiply

2 (8/15 × 18/7)

= 2 [ (8 × 18)/(15 × 7) ]

= 2 ( 144/105 )

= (2 × 144)/105

= 288/105

= 2 78/105

= 2 26/35
