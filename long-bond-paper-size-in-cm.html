2. size of long bond paper in cm


A long bond paper has a width of 21.59 centimeters (8.5 inches) and a length of 33.02 centimeters (13 inches).

The typical size of a long bond paper is 8.5 x 13 in inches.

Inorder to convert them to centimeters, we multiply the quantities by 2.54.

1 inch = 2.54 centimeters

With this, the size of a long bond paper is 21.59 x 33.02 in inches.
