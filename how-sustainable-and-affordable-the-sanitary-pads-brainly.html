Among the advantages of pads are:

Varying degrees of absorbencyLowered danger of toxic shockBetter for you than tampons

A few disadvantages of pads include:

Unfriendly to the environmentInclude chemicalsCan be largePossibly irritablePricey

TAMPONS

While pads are absorbent linings intended to adhere to your underwear, tampons are cylindrical inserts that go inside your vagina. Because they are small, almost undetectable, and swim-safe, tampons are a wonderful alternative. However, they can be challenging to insert and come with a risk of vaginal irritation or toxic shock syndrome.

MENSTRUAL CUP

Some benefits of using a menstrual cup include the following:

Eco-friendly and Budget-friendlySafer than other personal care itemsUseful when engaging in sexual activityContains more blood

Although inexpensive and environmentally friendly, there are still a few menstrual cup drawbacks and side effects to consider before switching to a cup:

Finding the ideal fit could be challenging.Not easy to insert or removeAdverse effects from the menstrual cup allergiesProbabilities of vaginal itch

CLOTH PADS

Cloth pads are beneficial to your health, the environment, and your body, Cloth pads provide comfort and economical.

Cloth pads have a few minor drawbacks, Cloth pads need additional effort and can be difficult to maintain.

PERIOD PANTIES

When some women have reported increased discomfort while using tampons, period pants are also known to help with menstrual cramps. On the other hand, if you have a heavy flow, a period panty might not be the best choice. In addition, they may cost more than normal underwear and sanitary pads.
