18. what are the items which you consider to be weaknesses or areas for improvement?


Knowing your weaknesses is as important as knowing your strengths.

4 items that I hold to consider weaknesses for self-improvement:

1. Embrace the weaknesses

2. Start journaling

3. Start doing something for myself

4. Tell the friends or closest people and ask their opinion about me

EXPLANATION:

1. Embrace the weaknesses

Understanding that everyone is different, and understanding how I am different, is the first step to knowing yourself. I try to accept and understand what I am lacking. I wrote a list of my strengths and weaknesses. I ask myself, do I want to change the traits that I lack? For my strengths, I always increase my potential again humbly.

2. Start journaling

Writing a diary or journal may seem too childish for us. But journaling helped me get to know myself better. I have a special book for my journal and only I can read. Then make it a routine every day to record what I'm grateful for, what I learned and what I want to change tomorrow. I freed myself to tell the exciting experience that day.

3. Start doing something for myself

I make time for myself and my hobbies. For example, take part in volunteer activities. If I can do one thing with satisfactory results, that can be my strength. If I find it difficult and cause problems, it could be my weakness. But that doesn't mean I give up! I can fix it with the advantages that I have or there is nothing wrong with asking a friend for help.

4. Tell the friends or closest people and ask their opinion about me

In my opinion, there is nothing wrong with telling friends about ourselves. With this I can know how I look in their eyes. In addition, I also have to be ready to accept their criticism and suggestions to build a better personality.
