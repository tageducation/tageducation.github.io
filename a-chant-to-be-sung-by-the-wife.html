7. the chant is used to be sung by the wife to be during the ceremony​


Music Of Mindanao

LUNSEY/ SA-IL

Is known as an important part for the woman who is also a bride-to-be.

Every bride is very excited to show each and every one its beauty and grace during the most important event in her life. Tying the knot with someone she loves the most and spending the rest of her life with.

Beautiful dress, glowing face, happy moment, and memorable experiences is what made the celebration complete.

Bride to be in the places of Mindanao has a unique tradition and style of expressing their love for their groom and showcasing the love they have for each other.

CHANT- is a lyrical rendition of different improvised texts or scriptures.

The Chant of LINSEY/SA-IL is known as one of the important chants sung in the ceremony by the woman that is about to get married. It is composed of very important messages for the couples that will begin the journey of married life with good advice, suggestions, and expectations of life ahead of them.

Lunsey and Sa-Il is classified as part of the Yakan Vocal Music, which is described in three Types;

Lugu and Other melodies: mainly used tor Quran and religious books items.Kalangan categorize into 2; Jamiluddin and LunseyKatakata is a type of vocal music that is being sung by solo or two groups that Create a connection by giving a response to one another.

It is also their way of delivering the story and history of native people from Yakan.

They are the ethnic Filipino group widely known as the descended from Orang Dampuans a kind of kingdom that is originated from Indonesia and has a very colorful costume.

Sail and Lunsey are famous songs being done during the wedding ceremony. But, there is another type of Sa-IL song that is being sung during the Quranic graduation or also called ‘Magtammat'.
