19. can educational attainment be measured by just literacy and numeracy? why?​


Answer:

Yes, educational attainment can be measured by just a literacy and numeracy.

Explanation:

Because numeracy, like literacy, is essential for students to gain access to and make sense of their surroundings. Being able to quantify and measure their environment in various ways will allow them to make more informed decisions about the types of actions they should take in their lives.
