1. anong meron sa april 1?​


Answer:

Ang Abril 1 ay ang ika-91 na araw ng taon sa kalendaryong Gregorian; 274 na araw ang natitira hanggang sa katapusan ng taon. Ito ang unang araw ng ikalawang quarter ng taon, at ang kalagitnaan ng unang kalahati ng taon. Sa mga leap year, ang ikalawang quarter ng taon ay nagsisimula sa tanghali sa araw na ito.

4. What is the best way to tell a caller that a product that was scheduled to ship on March 15 will not be available until April 1? The product will not be available until April 1 The product will no longer ship on march 15 The product should be available by April 1 The product will be available April 1?


The best way to tell the caller that an item won't be available until April 1 is to say "The product will not be available until April 1". By saying that, we will give clear information to the customer.

"The product will no longer ship on march 15" and "The product will be available on April 1” are probably good choices. But if we say that, the customer may be waiting and if there is a problem that causes the product to still not be available by April 1st, then that will make them dislike it even more.

The thing to note when telling the caller that his item that must be delivered by March 15th is delayed is to apologize in advance and tell him the reason why the product is not available in polite language. After saying "The product will not be available until April 1" don't forget to say that we will notify them as soon as possible when the item is available.
