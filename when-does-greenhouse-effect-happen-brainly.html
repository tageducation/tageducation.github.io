1. when does greenhouse effect happen brainly


Answer:

The greenhouse effect occurs when certain gases in the Earth's atmosphere (the air around the Earth) trap infrared radiation. This makes the planet become warmer, similar to the way it makes a greenhouse become warmer.

Explanation:

The greenhouse effect is caused by greenhouse gases; the most important greenhouse gases in Earth's atmosphere are: water vapor, carbon dioxide(CO2), and methane. When there is more greenhouse gas in the air, the air holds more heat. This is why more greenhouse gases cause climate change and global warming.

17. When does the greenhouse effect happen?


THE GREENHOUSE EFFECT

The term greenhouse effect or the natural greenhouse, refers to the natural process that keeps Earth's surface and atmosphere warm and heated.

During this process the Sun's solar radiation reaches and passes through the earth's atmosphere, some of it bounces back or reflected back to outerspace and the most of it is absorbed and trapped then re-radiated back by greenhouse gases, such as carbon dioxide, methane and ozone.

THE GLOBAL WARMING

But due to the overconsumption of fuels and human negligence, greenhouse gases level within the atmosphere becomes extremely thick, thick enough that it now bolsters and reinforces the effect of the natural Greenhouse of our planet, causing the Earth to experience Global Warming.
