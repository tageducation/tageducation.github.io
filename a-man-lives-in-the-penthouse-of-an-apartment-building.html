Answer:

66 3/4 m

Step-by-step explanation:

Given that the tower has 20 stories and each story is 3 2/10 m high.

Then, 20 x 3 2/10 = 20 x (32/10) = 20 x (16/5) = 64 m.

The penthouse is 2 3/4 m high so the total height of the tower is 64 + 2 3/4 = 66 3/4 m.
