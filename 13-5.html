22. the value of sec B is?a. 13/5 b. 5/13c. 12/13d. 13/12​


Given:

sec B + tan B = r

Formula Used:

sec2θ - tan2θ = 1

a2 - b2 = (a + b) (a - b)

Calculation:

sec2 B - tan2 B = 1

⇒ (sec B + tan B) (sec B - tan B) = 1

⇒ (r) (sec B - tan B) = 1

⇒ sec B - tan B = 1/r

∴ Value sec B + tan B = r then sec B - tan B is 1/r.

29. what is(6×²-5×+1)-(×²+8×-7)? A.-5×²-13×+8 B.5×²+13×+8C.5×²-13×+8 D.5×²+13×-8help me plss thanks^_^​

[tex]\huge\tt\blue{Solution:}[/tex]

• (6x² - 5x + 1) - (x² + 8x - 7)

Step 1.

Change the operation to addition and reverse the sign of each term in the subtrahend.

→ (6x² - 5x + 1) + (-x² - 8x + 7)

Step 2.

Collect like terms.

→ 5x² - 5x + 1 - 8x + 7

→ 5x² - 13x + 1 + 7

→ 5x² - 13x + 8
