13. what is the length of floor plan with an area of 8q³r⁴s⁵--24q²r³s⁴ +48qr⁴s³ square units,if the width is 8qr²s³ units​


PROBLEM

What is the length of floor plan with an area of 8q³r⁴s⁵-24q²r³s⁴ +48qr⁴s³ square units, if the width is 8qr²s³ units?

ANSWER

The length is equal to q²r²s² - 3qrs + 6r²

SOLUTION

A = L × W

A/W = L

L = (8q³r⁴s⁵-24q²r³s⁴ +48qr⁴s³) / (8qr²s³)

L = q²r²s² - 3qrs + 6r²

17. ano ang ibig sabihin ng floor unit number​


Answer:

Unit Number (Vertical Numbering) This refers to the number assigned to a unit within a building, where applicable. The unit number is preceded by '#' e.g. #12-456. The first two digits refer to the floor level while the numbers after the hyphen sign refer to the assigned number of the unit.

Apartments are usually numbered using the floor number postfixed with the "side" designation, where the floor numbers go st. (ground floor), 1., 2., 3. etc and the post-fix "tv" (to the left), "mf" (in the middle) or "th" (to the right).

A room number is a number assigned to a room within a building. Its purpose is to identify a particular room, and help building inhabitants locate that room. Logical and consistent assignment of room numbers is important for efficient everyday building usage, and to allow emergency personnel to quickly and easily find their way to any area of a building.

Room numbers may consist of three digits, but can be any number of digits. The room number is generally assigned with the first digit indicating the floor on which the room is located. For example, room 412 would be on the fourth floor of the building; room 540 would be on the fifth floor. Buildings that have more than nine floors will have four digits assigned to rooms beyond the ninth floor. For example, room 1412 would be on the 14th floor.

29. David measured the floor of his storage units, which is rectangular. It is 12 meters wide and 15 meters from one corner to opposite corner. How long is the storage unit?


Answer:

9 m

Step-by-step explanation:

The formula is a^2+b^2=c^2

If...

a = 12 m

c = 15 m

Then,

a^2+b^2=c^2

144+b^2=225

b^2=225-144

b^2=81

b=9

Length is 9 m.
