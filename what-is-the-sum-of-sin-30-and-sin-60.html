24. what is the value of cos 60°- sin 30°​

Step-by-step explanation:

As we know that

cos 60° =  1/2, cos 30° =  √3/2, sin 60° =  √3/2, sin 30° = 1/2

Substituting all the values in the given expression,

1/2 × √3/2 + √3/2 × 1/2

= √3/4 + √3/4

= 2√3/4

= √3/2

Thus, cos 60° x cos 30° + sin 60° x sin 30° = √3/2
