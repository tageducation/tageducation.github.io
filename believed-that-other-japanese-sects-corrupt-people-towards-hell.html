30. Explain this quote “Power doesn‟t corrupt people, people corrupt power”.


Power is an inanimate concept that in itself isn't evil, such as money is in itself not evil. It is the way they are used that makes them corrupt and malicious. People in power have the choice to use their power for the greater good, and cannot blame their actions on how power corrupted them. It is not what is outside a person that corrupts them, but what is within. It is their greed, pride, and own selfishness that urges people to take advantage of their power in order to satisfy their insatiable desires.
