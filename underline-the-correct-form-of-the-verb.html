17. Underlined the correct form of the verb in parentheses​


SUBJECT-VERB AGREEMENT

What is Subject-verb agreement? It means that the subject should agree with the verb appropriately.

Rule in Subject-verb agreement

When the subject is plural, the verb should be singular; When the subject is singular, the verb is plural.

TENSES IN VERBS

There are 3 official tenses in verbs. These are past, present, and future. They represent the time signature of the subjects verb.
