1. The lower quartile is equal to ______________.


Quartile:

The lower quartile is equal to twenty - five percent (25%) of the distribution which is also known as the first quartile.

Quartiles are the score points which divide a distribution into four equal parts. Twenty five percent (25%) of the distribution are below the first quartile, fifty percent (50%) are below the second quartile, and seventy - five - percent (75%) are below the third quartile.

Sample Problems on Quartile:

In a 70- item test, Melody got a score of 50 which is the third quartile. What does this mean?In a 100 item test the passing mark is the third quartile. What does it imply?

Solutions:

In a 70 - item test, Melody got a score of which is the third quartile. This means that she surpassed 75% of her classmates. Given that Melody's score is the third quartile then it follows that she surpasses those who got a score of 49 and below which comprises 75% of the class.In a 100 item test the passing mark is the third quartile. This implies that the students should answer at least 75 items correctly to pass the test. Given that the test is a 100 - item test then Q1 or first quartile must be 25, second quartile or Q2 is 50 and Q3 or third quartile is 75. And in order to pass the test, a student must answer 76 and above items correctly.
