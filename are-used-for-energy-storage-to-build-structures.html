The correct answer is B. LIPIDS.

LIPIDS are used for energy storage to build a structures and a signal molecule to help cells communicate with each other.

The option A. Carbohydrate is incorrect. Because the benefit of carbohydrate is as main energy sources but not specifically build a structures and a signal molecule.

The option C. Protein is incorrect. Because the benefits of protein is to increase muscle mass and increase muscle strength.

The option D. Nucleic acid is incorrect.  Because the important functions of nucleic acids are to store, transmit, and translate genetic information, intermediate metabolism and energy information reactions.

FURTHER EXPLANATION:

By definition, lipids are organic compounds that occur in nature and are poorly soluble in water, but readily soluble in non-polar organic solvents, such as hydrocarbons or ether. Based on their origin, lipids are divided into two types, namely vegetable lipids and animal lipids. Vegetable lipids are fats contained by plants, for example avocado, durian, and others. Natural fats are formed from glycerol and unsaturated fatty acids while animal lipids are fats contained by animals such as meat, eggs, fish, and others.

The function of lipids is as a source of energy, protecting body organs, forming cells and structures, a source of essential fatty acids, transporting fat-soluble vitamins, conserving protein, giving a feeling of fullness and delicacy, as a lubricant, and maintaining body temperature. This is mainly used for energy storage to build a structures and a signal molecule to help cells communicate with each other.
