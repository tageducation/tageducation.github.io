6. Restate the question​


Answer:

Restating questions means repeating the questions,if it is short,or rephrasing it if it is longer It is important for nonnative speaker of english to restate questions to ensure correct comprehension and to allow thinking time before answering.

Restating the questions helps you look closely at exactly what is being asked.

Example:

1.What's your favorite ice cream flavor?

My favorite ice cream flavor is key lime pie

Or

Key lime pie is my favorite ice cream flavor.

2. In the novel hoot Mullet Finger is my favorite character

Or

Mullet Finger is my favorite character in the novel hoot

15. the purpose of the conclusion is toA. give evedince and detailsB. Restate the attention getter and backgroundC. give new informationD. restate argument and reason​


Answer:

D.

Explanation:

A conclusions purpose is to restate the thesis and the reason for the body is to support the said thesis/conclusion. Therefore, it is D. It cannot be C because a conclusion also restate the whole essay/paragraph rather than present new information or it is not a conclusion. It also cannot be C because it must be presented at the beginning of the essay/paragraph you are working on or called a hook, when a reader starts to read, it must grab the attention at the beginning. A cannot be an answer because the evidence and details must be presented at the body, it supports the conclusion that will be presented in the end of the essay/paragraph.
