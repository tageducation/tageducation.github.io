Claude Monet was the founder of the Impressionist movement in France.

Explanantion:

Claude Monet is the originator of the Impressionist movement in France. As a great and inspirational artist, Monet is one of the important figures who formed an independent art group that became the driving force for the birth of Impressionism. Monet himself prefers the style of painting outside, in the open air, and capturing light events at certain times. The thing about Monet's work that is quite famous is the series of paintings that are produced by painting the same landscape object repeatedly at various times, namely morning, afternoon, evening, and night.

Impressionism is one of the schools in painting that tends to try to highlight the power of lighting by playing with the colors. In addition, impressionism is also more focused on bright lighting, for example, depicting the atmosphere in the morning or afternoon. However, Impressionism tends to pay less attention to the shape of the object. Where the forms that exist in the flow of Impressionism are not clearly described. Even so, Impressionism can still attract art connoisseurs with the power of lighting and bright color strokes.

Impressionism painting is a painting genre that presents a depiction of an object in accordance with the impression when the object is painted. This art flow emphasizes the impression of lighting that is quite strong compared to forming the object itself.

Pictures or paintings from the flow of Impressionism have a simple but slightly dramatic style. In addition, the painting also comes with a different perception and point of view from other realistic paintings, such as Naturalism and Realism paintings. Impressionism paintings will create a blurry and blurry impression, because of the impression of excessive lighting. The painters will also depict a simple impression compared to what is depicted. Painted objects are made as close as possible but do not display accurate details.

In general, Impressionist painters used the technique of painting with thick and short brushes. So that in the painting it will give an impression that makes it easier for painters and art connoisseurs to guess a shape and also an object in the painting.

10. name of national Artist and Year Awarded and Outstanding contribution​


Answer:

 The Order of the  National Artists Award  (Orden ng Gawad Pambansang Alagad ng Sining) is the highest national recognition given to Filipino individuals who have made significant contributions to the development of Philippine arts; namely, Music, Dance, Theater, Visual Arts, Literature, Film and Broadcast Arts,  and Architecture and Allied Arts.  The order is jointly administered by the National Commission for Culture and the Arts (NCCA) and Cultural Center of the Philippines (CCP) and conferred by the President of the Philippines upon recommendation by both institutions.

The Order of National Artists Award  is one of the honors conferred by the Republic  of the Philippines that embodies the nation’s highest ideals in the humanities and aesthetic expression through the distinct achievements of individual citizens.  While the Republic bestows due recognition to these singular achievements, it also honors its own cultural heritage, whose enrichment these achievements have significantly effected, enhanced, and given direction.

These achievements are measured in terms of their vision, unusual insight, creativity and imagination, technical proficiency of the highest order in expressing Filipino culture and traditions, history, way of life, and aspirations.

18. What are the common characteristics of the outstanding Filipino teachers? Cite at least three (3) similarities in their personal qualities and competencies. Use the space below to write your answer.​


Three of the common characteristics of outstanding Filipino are being competent, credible, and compassionate.

Why should a teacher be competent?

This is one of the most important characteristic for a teacher. As a famous saying goes, "You can't give what you don't have". For teachers, they cannot provide or pass on knowledge to their students if they themselves do not possess that knowledge. And being competent is not only about knowing what to teach, but it also means knowing how to teach. Choosing teaching methods that are effective for their learners. Learning new ways of teaching when the old techniques seem to not work anymore. Competent teachers are definitely outstanding teachers.

Why should a teacher be credible?

Teachers are role models, even if they like it or not. And what's the best way to be a good role model? It is being credible, doing what is good and right even if no one is watching or checking. A teacher should be honest professionally, someone who cannot be bought by bribes. A teacher must be intellectually honest in his or her works. They require students to do their own works, do not copy or cheat, it is just fair to require teachers to have the same or even higher standard of credibility.

Why should teachers be compassionate?

Having genuine compassion for others, especially for their students, is what sets teachers apart from other professions. They say, if you want to be rich, don't be a teacher. This is because teachers do not have high salaries, they even spend their own money for their classes sometimes. And this is because of their overflowing compassion for their students and their love for teaching. An outstanding teacher does not only have the brains, but the heart as well.
