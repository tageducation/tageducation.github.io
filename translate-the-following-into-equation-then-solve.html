23. translate each of the following into an equation and solve the equation. 1. the sum of number and 12 is 30.determine the number​


Answer:

Let's call the number we are trying to find "x".

According to the problem, the sum of "x" and 12 is equal to 30. We can translate this into an equation as:

x + 12 = 30

To solve for "x", we want to isolate it on one side of the equation. We can do this by subtracting 12 from both sides of the equation:

x + 12 - 12 = 30 - 12

Simplifying:

x = 18

Therefore, the number we were trying to find is 18.

Answer:

1.x + 17 = 64        the value of x is 47

2.2x - 8 = 36      the value of x is 22

4.3(x + 5) = 45   the value of x is 10

5.8/x = 2            the value of x is 4

Step-by-step explanation:

1. To solve for x, we can subtract 17 from both sides of the equation:

x + 17 - 17 = 64 - 17

x = 47

Therefore, the value of x is 47.

2. To solve for x, we can add 8 to both sides of the equation:

2x - 8 + 8 = 36 + 8

2x = 44

Then, we divide both sides by 2 to isolate x:

2x/2 = 44/2

x = 22

Therefore, the value of x is 22.

3. To solve for y, we can multiply both sides of the equation by 6:

3y = 15 * 6

3y = 90

Then, we divide both sides by 3 to isolate y:

3y/3 = 90/3

y = 30

Therefore, the value of y is 30.

4. To solve for x, we first simplify the left side of the equation:

3x + 15 = 45

Then, we subtract 15 from both sides of the equation:

3x + 15 - 15 = 45 - 15

3x = 30

Next, we divide both sides by 3 to isolate x:

3x/3 = 30/3

x = 10

Therefore, the value of x is 10.

5. To solve for x, we can multiply both sides of the equation by x:

8 = 2x

Then, we divide both sides by 2 to isolate x:

8/2 = 2x/2

4 = x

Therefore, the value of x is 4.
