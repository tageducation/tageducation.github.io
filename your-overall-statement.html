29. It is a stand-alone statement that can summarize the overall idea of the paper


The statement being referred to above is called the Executive Summary.

 

The Executive Summary pertains to a short document or statement of the key points of a lengthy report or publication.

 

It aims to communicate the whole report or publication in a way that anyone who reads it will easily understand what the report is all about.

 

Ideally, the executive summary of a report/research should be organized by Project Summary, Background, Process, Finding and Conclusions and Recommendations for Action.
