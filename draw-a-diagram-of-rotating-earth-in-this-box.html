9. Let Us Enhance Using Venn diagram, compare and contrast rotation from revolution ROTATION REVOLUTION Rotation and revolution are both movements of the earth. Rotation is the earth's movement on its axis causing day and night. Revolution on the other hand, is earth's movement around the sun causing different seasons.


Answer:

Difference Between Rotation and Revolution

ROTATION

- Rotation is the circular movement of an object around an axis of rotation. A three-dimensional object may have an infinite number of rotation axes.

- The rotation of the Earth is the turning on its axis.

- The Earth takes 24 hours to complete a rotation concerning the sun.

REVOLUTION

- Revolution is the movement of the Earth around the Sun.

- The Earth takes a full year (365 days) for one complete revolution around the Sun

"Rotation" refers to an object's spinning motion about its axis. "Revolution" refers to the object's orbital motion around another object.

For example, Earth rotates on its axis, producing a 24-hour day. Earth revolves around the Sun, producing the 365-day year.

What is the similarities and difference between rotation and revolution?

Rotation is the movement of an object on its axis. On the other hand, revolution is the complete round trip around something, i.e. another object or center. Rotation is when the object spins around an internal axis. Conversely, revolution is when the object travels around an external axis.
