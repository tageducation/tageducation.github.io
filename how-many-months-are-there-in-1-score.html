1. how many months are there in 1 score ​


Answer:

There are 240 months in 1 score.

Step-by-step explanation:

It would be very easy for us to convert how many months are there in 1 score provided that we have a conversion factor.

Some Conversion UnitsDistanceLengthTimeDegreeTemperature

We don't have an actual conversion from a month into a score. However, we know that 1 score is equivalent to 20 years.

We need to convert the score in terms of years first, then convert it to months.

So, since 1 score is 20 years. Then our conversion factor would be [tex]\frac{12\:\text{months}}{1\:\text{year}}[/tex]. We use the conversion factor that has a year unit in the denominator so that we can cancel the year unit.

              [tex]\begin{aligned}20\:\text{years} \times \left(\begin{aligned}\text{Conversion}\\\text{Factor}\end{aligned}\right)&=20 \:\text{years} \times \left(\frac{12\:\text{months}}{1\:\text{year}}\right)\\&=20 \times 12 \:\text{months}\\&=240\:\text{months}\end{aligned}[/tex]
