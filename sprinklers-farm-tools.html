Answer:

FARM TOOLS AND EQUIPMENT

1. Shovel - used for digging, and lifting soils. Commonly made out of steel.

2. Hand fork -used for removing weeds

3. Knapsack sprayer - a type of sprayer where liquid is dispersed using a hand-held nozzle connected to a pressurized reservoir carried on the operator's back.

4. Sprinkler - used to irrigate landscapes, and agricultural crops

5. Disc plow - used to till ground where the crops will be planted

6. Water hose - made out of plastic where you water your lawn

7. Native harrow - used to pound the soil

8. Trailer - a huge vehicle that moves and carries agricultural crops.

9. Grass cutter - as the name suggest, it is used to cut grass

10. Rice harvester - a machine that helps in harvesting rice

11. Hand cultivator - used to remove weeds by hand

12. Tractor - a vehicle used for easier implementing, cultivating, fertilizing, and planting crops

13. Wheel barrow - a two-wheeled cart that's used to carry and transfer dirt, and rocks

14. Crowbar - commonly used for breaking things, and removing nails

a. Sprinkler is much more effective than the hose in watering the plants. A sprinkler enables the the soil soaked with an ample amount of water enough to let the root of the plant to absorb the water to its advantage. In addition, as a sprinkler sprays water all over the plant, water sprays that landed on plant leaves removes dust and dirt, and may rinse away insect pests and fungal spores.
