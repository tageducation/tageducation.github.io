10. Acrostic Poem for Alcohol


An acrostic poem is a poem where the initial letters in each line spell out a word or a phrase. Acrostic poems are used to convey messages to a wide range of people. The poem does not have to rhyme at the end. But of course, as this is an acrostic poem, the letters at the start should correspond to the appropriate words. It is best though, if they also rhyme. Below is an example of an acrostic poem for the word ALCOHOL.

A liquid we ingest happily;

Lowers our inhibitions, makes us bolder

Causes disorientation, hangover and confusion

Overdose and long-term use liver failure is sure.

How do we stop such malign addiction?  

Of course, determination, self-love and sheer will

Looking towards a future with no alcohol in the body!

Quitting the drinking of alcohol is not easy. There is a process associated with it and it is best to consult a doctor and a therapist to ensure that the addiction tapers down.
