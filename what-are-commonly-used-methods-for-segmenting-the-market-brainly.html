What are commonly used methods for segmeting the market

Demographic, psychographic, behavioral and geographic segmentation are considered the four main types of market segmentation, but there are also many other strategies you can use, including numerous variations on the four main types.

[tex]Demographic[/tex]

Demographic segmentation is one of the most common forms. It refers to splitting up audiences based on observable, people-based differences.

[tex]Behavioral[/tex]

Many of the actions you might look at relate to how someone interacts with your product, website, app or brand.

[tex]Geographic[/tex]

Geographic segmentation, splitting up your market based on their location, is a basic but highly useful segmentation strategy.

[tex]Psychographic[/tex]

Psychographic segmentation is similar to demographic segmentation, but it deals with characteristics that are more mental and emotional.
