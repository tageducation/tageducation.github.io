19. Eating fruits daily, Healthy or Unhealthy? and give the reason.


Answer:

EATING FRUIT HEALTHY OR UNHEALTHY

Fruits has been recognized as a good source of vitamins and minerals, and for their role in preventing Vitamin C and Vitamin A deficiencies. The nutrients in fruits are vital for health and maintenance of the body. It is very healthy eating any kinds of fruits every day. It will make our body strong, fit, and flexible.

Health Benefits of fruits:

1. Eating a diet rich in fruit may reduce risk in health problems or diseases.

2. A fruit containing eating pattern is part of an overall healthy diet.

3. One to 2 ½ cups of fruits are recommended each day, depending on how many calories you need.

Some Examples of Fruits and the Diseases or Health Problems that they can Cure:

• Apples – it protects the heart, prevents constipation, blocks diarrhea, improve lung capacity, and cushions joints.

• Avocados – prevent diabetes, it lowers cholesterol, reduce risk for strokes, controls blood pressure, and smoothes skin.

• Bananas – protects your heart, quiets a cough, strengthens bones, controls blood pressure, blocks diarrhea.

• Blueberries – avoid cancer, protects your heart, stabilizes blood sugar, boosts memory, and prevents constipation.

• Pomegranates – avoid risk for heart disease and women’s prostate cancer.

• Cherries – it is an anti- inflammatory medicine.

• Papaya – it helps us relieve gas from indigestion.

• Mangoes – maintains your blood sugar levels.

• Grapefruit – it is an appetite suppressant, lose a few pounds, reduce the risk of certain types of cancer, lower blood pressure, and it can also help to improve the quality of the male’s sperm.

• Watermelon – it helps to keep you hydrated and makes you feel cool in the hottest months of the year, it is high in beta carotene and lycopene that both good in the heart, and also high in citrulline which is converted into arginine in the body.

Arginine – it boosts nitric oxide, which relaxes blood vessels, the same basic effect that Viagra has, to treat erectile dysfunction and maybe even prevent it.

Eating foods like vegetables and some meat can also make us healthy but we must also consider and give importance to the different benefits that we can get in eating fruits. Fruits makes us even more healthier and nutritious, as what the saying says, “An apple a day makes the doctors away”.
