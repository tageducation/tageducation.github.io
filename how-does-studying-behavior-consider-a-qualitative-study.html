6. Please different a qualitative study from a quantitative study


Here are the differences between Qualitative study/research from Quantitative study/research.

Qualitative Research is about exploratory research. It gives information and insight about a certain problem to develop ideas for quantitative research. It focuses on the opinions, reasons, and thoughts that can be used to uncover a problem.

Quantitative Research is about the quantity,number or numerical data to be transformed to a usable statistics.
