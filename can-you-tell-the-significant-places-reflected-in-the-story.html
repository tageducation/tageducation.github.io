Caribbean's Pink Sand Beaches

Pink sand beaches are common throughout the Caribbean thanks to crushed shells, creatures and coral that mix with the native granules.

Pink Sands Beach, BahamasHorseshoe Bay Beach, BermudaPink Beach, BonaireJobson’s Cove, BermudaPink Beach, Barbuda & Antigua

Why Is The Sand Pink?

Tiny single-celled red organisms which is the 'Foraminifera' grow beneath the coral reefs. When these organisms die, they fall to the ocean floor and mix with bits of coral and crushed shells. The sand turns pink when this mixture washes onto the beach.
