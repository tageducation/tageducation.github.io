22. What did you feel after reading the story waiting by ron daruin?what did you feel that way?


"The Waiting" by Ron Darvin is a story which depicts the endless physical and emotional misery of overseas Filipino workers. It revolves around the sacrifice that a mother is willing to endure all for the sake of providing a convenient life to her family. The story felt realistic and relatable. Anyone who has a family member working abroad would surely be left deep in thoughts. Personally, the story also kicked me in the guts because I have relatives working in foreign countries. Most, if not all them, share the same goal - to improve their family members' lives. These people must be commended for doing what flesh and blood can't stand.

The story made me realize how lucky I am to still be able to stay in my own country together with my family.
