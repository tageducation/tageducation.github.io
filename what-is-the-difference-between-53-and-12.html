First, you must find the LCD of the denominators.

3 = 3, 6, 9, 12, 15

5 = 5, 10, 15

Next, you must find the equivalent fraction with the LCD as the denominator.

2/5 = 6/15   1/3 = 5/15

Lastly, subtract the greatest fraction with the least fraction.

6/15 - 5/15 = 1/15
