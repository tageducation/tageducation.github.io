Reduce the fraction to the lowest term

[tex] \frac{4}{5} \times \frac{5}{12} [/tex]

Reduced all the numerator and denominators that can be reduced

[tex] \frac{1}{1} \times \frac{1}{3} [/tex]

Numerator multiply between numerators,and denominator multiply between denominators

[tex] \frac{1 \times 1}{1 \times 3} [/tex]

Multiplying any number by 1 does not change the value
