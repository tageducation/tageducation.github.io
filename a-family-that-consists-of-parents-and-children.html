2. A family that consists of parents and children


The answer is Nuclear Family.  

What is Nuclear Family?

Nuclear Family is consists of two parents and their children. It is the traditional type of family structure.  

Other types of family structure are the following:  

Single Parent Family - It is a family structure that only consists of one parent (either a father or a mother) raising his or her own children.  Extended Family - This structure consists of two or more adults who were related (either through blood or through marriage) that were living in the same house. This type of family structure is very common in the Philippines.  Childless Family - It only consists of a husband and a wife.  Step Family - It consists of a parent that chooses to remarry and create a new setup that consists of a husband and his new wife together with her children in the previous marriage (or either way). It is also called a blended family.

19. PAKI EXPLAIN PO KAHIT TAGALOG Nuclear family: A family unit consisting of at most a father, mother and dependent children. It is considered the “traditional” family. Extended family: A family consisting of parents and children, along with either grandparents, grandchildren, aunts or uncles, cousins etc. In some circumstances, the extended family comes to live either with or in place of a member of the nuclear family.

Paliwanag:

Ang mga sumusunod ay uri ng pamilya: nuclear at extended. Kapag sinabing nuclear na pamilya, ito ang tipikal na uri ng pamilya na binubuo ng ama, ina, at mga anak na wala pang mga asawa. Gaano man karami ang anak, ito ay nuclear na pamilya kapag ang lahat kasama ng kanilang ama at ina na nakatira sa iisang bahay. Ang extended na pamilya naman ay yaong nuclear na pamilya at dinagdagan pa ng mga lolo at lola, tiyuhin, tiyahin, o mga pinsan. Kaya ito tinawag na extended na pamilya sapagkat ang bahay ay binubuksan sa iba pang miyembro ng pamilya maliban sa tradisyunal na pamilya o sa nuclear na pamilya.
