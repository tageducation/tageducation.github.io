Organization vision:Reach a wider exposure and communityPermanent organization branch in each town, focusing on local smaller organization that cater to women needsDesigning more field programs dedicated for women empowermentHolding annual conference in the topic of women empowermentConstantly providing assistance for women-related legal cases

All in all, composing a good vision for an organization requires every member of its organization. This way, everyone can discuss what would be the best interest that serve the organization itself and the community they are dedicating themselves to.

14. your supervisor wants you to work overtime every week but you have other commitments outside if work what will you do​


Doing every day of the week and on weekends is unsustainable and can result in exhaustion and burnout. Issue managers have a wide-ranging negative effect. Managerial support for work-life balance has been linked to lower earnings and more on-the-job errors in several studies. Furthermore, a bad manager could be physically causing you to become ill.

Here are some possible solutions to this problem: Don't Allow - Note that your boss is a human, too, no matter how cold and aloof he seems to be in the workplace. His workaholic traits may be a product of his upbringing. His superior's pressure—and praise—might make it difficult for him to understand your need for more harmony at first. Do Communicate Clearly - Approaching a workaholic boss can be daunting, and you don't want to offend or embarrass him or her. Understanding that your boss's decisions are not meant to pick you out or make your life difficult can provide you with the necessary perspective. Don't believe your boss is out to get you; instead, strive for transparent and constructive dialogue. Define clear limits. We all need time in our day that is not dedicated to work. So what if you eat dinner and put the kids to bed at 6 p.m.? Set some limits — and notify your employer that you will be unavailable at that time.

This is a red flag if you have a transparent and fair dialog with your manager and they are unable to consider your point of view or change your hours.
