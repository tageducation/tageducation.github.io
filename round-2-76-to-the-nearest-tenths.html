Step-by-step explanation:

In 8.125;

8—whole number

1—tenth

2—hundredth

5—thousandth

(1 to 4 numbers are rounded down meaning eliminate the numbers on the side.)

(5 to 9 numbers are rounded up so +1 to the given question and eliminate the numbers on fhe side.)

Like for example on 8.125:

Number 8 is the whole number and since the tenths is a lower number, then the answer is 8.

Number 1 is in the tenths and since the hundredths is a lower number, then the answer is 8.1

Number 2 is in rhe hundredths and on its side is the thousandth and the number is bigger (kasali sa 5 to 9 numbers), since the number is bigger then you have to round it up. The answer is 8.13
