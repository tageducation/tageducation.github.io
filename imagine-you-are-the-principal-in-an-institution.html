28. Imagine you took out a loan for P500.The interest is 10% per month.There are no additional fees. If you want to pay the loan in full (principal and interest) after one month, how much will you need to pay?​


Answer:

P550.00

Step-by-step explanation:

Solution:

Principal = P500

Interest Rate = 10%

Interest = Principal × Rate

I = P500 × 0.10

I = P50

Total amount to be paid = Principal + Interest(I)

Total amount to be paid = P500+50

Total amount to be paid = P550
