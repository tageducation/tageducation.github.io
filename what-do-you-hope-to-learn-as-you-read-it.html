In this situation, scrape the leaf to achieve the thinness of the specimen that is enough for the light to pass through it.

Scenario 2: When using a high power objective, it is important to remember NOT TO USE the coarse adjustment knob because it might break the cover slip or the glass slide itself. It would ruin the specimen.

Scenario 3:

With my knowledge on using a microscope, I would study the genetic material of the virus to know how it reproduces and how it can be killed.
