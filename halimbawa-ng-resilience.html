6. Pagkakaiba ng hazard,vulnerability,risk ay resilience


A hazard is an agent which has the potential to cause harm to a vulnerable target.

Vulnerability refers to the inability to withstand the effects of a hostile environment.

Risk is a situation involving exposure to danger.

Resilience is the capacity to recover quickly from difficulties or toughness.
