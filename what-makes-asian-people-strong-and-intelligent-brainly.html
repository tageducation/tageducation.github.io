17. What makes asian people strong and intelligent


Here are some reasons why Asian people are intelligent:

1. Value personal experience

In Eastern parenting systems, it is believed that a ban can stop a child's interest in exploring. So this action also affects the future of children and will make them more creative, they can imagine unique solutions, follow important rules, and respect elders.

2. Not self-interested

For example, Western parents will say, "Don't hurt yourself", while in the East they will say, "Don't hurt others." Before the age of 3 years, children are taught to respect other people, animals, and plants, seek truth, self-control, and care for nature. Children are brought up in such an environment in the East, where they can socialize and live with others, helping each other and considering common interests.

3. Take Early Childhood Education and Sports Classes

In the age range of 2-3 years, children can take lessons in English, math, drawing, acting and singing at the same time. Thanks to this system, all children by the age of 4 are good at playing at least one musical instrument and know the basics of math and grammar. Meanwhile, at the age of 5 years, children are ready to go to school and they are disciplined.

4. Go to school by themselves

In Japan and Korea, children as young as 6 go to school on their own. They were not accompanied by their mother or father. The reason is that they are independent.

By the time children start school, they are usually good at counting, writing, and reading simple books. In Asian countries, many parents believe that teaching numeracy to children at an early age can develop the frontal lobes of the brain and develop their creative abilities.

5. Children determine their own future

Unlike in the West, children in the East are not in a hurry to choose a profession if they are really not ready. Family ties are very important in Eastern culture. A child can live with their family as long as they need. But often, by the age of 14 children already know what they want and can live independently.

This system has existed for hundreds of years in the East, enabling parents to raise bright and talented children who respect tradition, and other people, and work well in a team.

The most important rule of the parenting system in the East is the explanation of decisions, love, and care for parents. Discipline and independence enable them to achieve maximum results in education and work
