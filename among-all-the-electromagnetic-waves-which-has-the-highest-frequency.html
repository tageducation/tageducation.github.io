28. Among the electromagnetic waves which has the highest energy


Gamma rays is electromagnetic waves that have the highest energy.

Explanation :

Gamma rays are an energized form of electromagnetic radiation produced by radioactivity or other nuclear or subatomic processes such as electron-positron annihilation.

Gamma rays are the term for high-energy electromagnetic radiation produced by energy transitions due to the acceleration of electrons. Because some electron transitions make it possible to have higher energies than some nuclear transitions, there is an overlap between what we call low-energy gamma rays and high-energy X-rays.

Gamma rays are a form of ionizing radiation, they are more penetrating than alpha or beta radiation (neither of which are electromagnetic radiation), but less ionizing. Gamma rays can be used as radiotherapy or radiation therapy for cancer. Exposure to these rays can cause damage to the DNA of cancer cells, thus inhibiting the growth and spread of cancer cells.
