DEFINITION:

Words of pride in one's accomplishments can be a source of motivation. Pride makes me try harder to achieve success. With pride, self-confidence will continue to emerge. Pride will also make us practice a sense of satisfaction with what we have achieved, whether big or small.

Often small achievements make the heart more inferior. In fact, a little progress in achievement, you should still be grateful and proud. Remember, success starts from something tiny and small. As the saying goes, "little by little, a little becomes a lot". Words of pride in self-achievement also show how much skill we have. The higher the skill and achievement, the higher the level of pride.

SO, THESE ARE SOME THINGS OR ACHIEVEMENTS THAT I'M PROUDEST OF:

1. Get out of your comfort zone to build your own businesss

Starting a business has become a trend nowadays. But starting a business is indeed an easy thing. Not infrequently the obstacles that stand in my way make my sense of self fade. It is true, in starting a business, it is expected to have entrepreneurial characteristics. And self-confidence plays an important role in determining success in business.

I often attend seminars to enrich my selling principles. I can make successful people as role models, but don't forget the uniqueness of my new business. I always seek advice from people who have been in business and succeeded. These suggestions will be a strategy for the success of your new business as well. If I often ask people who are experts, I feel that the chances of my business failing will be smaller.

2. Get out of your comfort zone to exercise every day

This is related to my commitment to a principle in sports science called FITTE, which stands for frequency, intensity, time, type, and enjoyment. Enjoyment is the most important. I do what I usually do, go to the gym. If I don't have time to exercise outside, I exercise at home, with cardio or weights equivalent to going to the gym.

3. Get out of your comfort zone to invest money and knowledge

What I mean by knowledge here is something that is really useful. Not jokes, jokes, or jokes that are only intended to "get trash" of the mind. In my opinion, there are two investment criteria, we have to spend something that you have, then we will get profit/reward. In my opinion, this investment not only gives me monetary gain, but also gives me an indefinable advantage.

4. Get out of your comfort zone to be more diligent and active in worship.

I believe that the essence, we are creatures of God and in the end return to God. Carrying out obligations is mandatory. Many things beyond our ability, determined by God.

Thanks for myself for persevering this far and as strong as this.

30. what in myself am i proudest of​


THE PROUDEST THINGS OF MYSELF:

1. I am independent and reliable

2. I'm a strong person

3. I'm a sincere person

4. I'm a lucky person and always grateful

EXPLANATION:

1. I am independent and reliable

First, I always remember that in my struggle for survival, there is most of the time I don't rely on anyone. I work hard to get everything I want. I don't like instant things (this was taught by my mom since childhood). Even though it's not as big as other people get, but I did well, and I got it. All of that made me less dependent on others and become a more independent person.

2. I'm a strong person

Being strong is not always synonymous with lifting something heavy, but also feeling like going through a lot of things in life that don't fit, but still getting to the goal. So do I. A lot of things make me stumble when I walk. For example, the support of people around, the limitations of many things, the absence of help. It's all very heavy. To live day after day is excruciating. But, don't we continue to gain strength and reason to survive? To prove to everyone that I too can stand on my own two feet. I maintained that and continued to be a strong human being.

3. I'm a sincere person

Although my success is appreciated by many people, I once asked, doesn't that often make a person thirsty for recognition? Maybe the answer is yes. In contrast to me who does everything based on sincerity, not expecting anything. Even when many people still think of me as a lousy person, I still live your life wisely.

4. I'm a lucky person and

I feel that I have a hard life, I know that in this world a lot of people feel the same way, even worse than I do. Not to bring down other people, but so that I can open my eyes to be grateful for some things that I have, but not for some others. On my mind is that I am a lucky person. Even though the people around me did not provide support, but I was able to get through it with real proof.
