Summarizing definition

Summarizing is the restatement of a long text in a shorter or concise form. In a summary, trimming a text is done by taking the main sentences and important explanatory sentences but not eliminating the idea of the original text.

Summarizing benefits

Summarizing is made to make it easier to understand the contents of the writing. Summary is useful as a tool to help us remember the contents of a book or a very long description.

The summary contains the main ideas that represent each part of the reading or the original text. By reading the summary as if we understand the entire contents of the book as a whole.

Summary features Brief.

A summary is the result of summarizing a piece of writing or discussion, therefore the summary results will be shorter than before they were summarized.

Clear.

The language used in a summary must be clear and easily understood by the readers and the summary writer himself.

Congested

In addition to being shorter and the language clear, a summary of the language must also be concise, not long-winded, and right on target.
