1. what do you think drives you to join or not join the activity​


Answer:

There are different factors why a student would choose to participate or not to participate in a school activity. It is a challenge to teachers to look into the situation of each student to better understand them and not discourage them further.

Explanation:

Reasons why students do not participate Financial constraints Lack of family support and resources Unhealthy mental state Anxiety Deep-rooted fear and trauma Lack of self confidence

Motivations of students to participate When they are recognized When their opinions are heard and considered When they feel safe and secure with the team or other members When they trust the leader or the teacherWhen they know and understand the purpose of the activity

How to Encourage Student Participation Find Out Why Students Don't Participate Show Students Their Fears Are Unfounded Be respectful. Speak loud enough so everyone can hear. Listen to classmates. Don't interrupt who is speaking. Build on your classmate's comments with your comments. Use participation to not only answer questions but to seek help or ask for clarification.
