What are the aspirations/interests of the community participants

A Community Aspiration is a statement that expresses the common aspirations that many people have for the future of their cultures. It is a statement of the future that we are working for, focused on shared values and narratives.

A well-crafted community aspiration opens people's hearts and minds to new opportunities and serves as a "north-star" that guides community action and guarantees that the interests of the community remain a reference point in the development of a common agenda and shared goals.

A strong community aspiration provides a "big tent" in which a wide range of participants can pursue interdependent challenges. Communicating and sharing small wins on the journey of community aspiration helps create a feeling of positive momentum that is contagious.

CHARACTERISTICS OF A STRONG COMMUNITY ASPIRATION

1. It is positive

2. It is based on shared community hopes and values

3. It is ambitious and cannot be achieved by a business-as-usual approach

4. It responds to a wide range of different organizations, groups and individuals

5. It focuses rather than just one

organization or group

6. Various organizations and groups should align their work to support the achievement of community aspirations.

Residents aspire for a community that is:

1. Healthy - One where people are physically healthy, have access to recreational sports, can get medical and mental health services when they need it, enjoy nutritious food and a clean environment.

2. Harmonious - A community where people try to get along with others, where differences are accepted and people love each other.

3. Thriving - A place where everyone's basic needs are met, people can obtain well-paid and exciting jobs, strong public education for every child, and a level playing field with opportunity for success.

4. Vibrant - One with variety, energy and enthusiasm, a balance of work and play, creativity, activity, natural beauty; and leaders who work together to protect what we have and plan for the future.
