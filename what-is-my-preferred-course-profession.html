The scope of discussion in criminology can be divided into:

1. Crime, deviant behavior, and delinquency,

2. Patterns of criminal behavior and causes of crime,

3. Victims of crime,

4. Society's social reaction to crime.

Criminology is the study of crime and its causes in society. Instead of a science, Criminology is a social science that focuses on research on the types, causes, and control of crime.
