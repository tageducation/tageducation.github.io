Topic sentence: The most common reason why there are endangered species is habitat loss, invasive species, and poaching

Controlling idea: Petroleum costs are persisting on rising

19. What is Topic Control?


Topic control -  Sticking to the topic throughout the discussion.

 

When one nominates a topic and the parties begin to talk about the topic and the one, usually the one who nominates the topic, reminds anyone who goes off topic to stick to the agreed one, topic control takes place. In a way topic control is topic restriction.

 

Examples:

May I remind everyone that the topic is ___?

We are talking about ____ here, right?

Let’s go back to the topic.
