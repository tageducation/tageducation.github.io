Answer:

Food Preparation

Here are the answers:

Chilling Refrigerate Cold storage Spoilage Trimming

Explanation:

Food preparation is the first step when cooking food. One must have an extensive knowledge about food preparation if they want to enter the food industry. The priority when it comes to food preparation is making sure that the food is safe and not contaminated.

Here are the terms that one should always remember:

Chilling is the act of refrigerating to reduce the temperature of food. To refrigerate means to keep food cold or cool. The process of preserving food by means of refrigeration is called cold storage. The deterioration of food and other perishable goods is called spoilage. To trim is to remove the parts of a food that are not needed for preparation.
