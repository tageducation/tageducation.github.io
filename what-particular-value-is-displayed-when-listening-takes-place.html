15. B. What particular value is displayed when listening takes place.Give only one and elaborate.​


Answer:

When listening takes place, the value of respect is displayed.

Explanation:

Active listening indicates that you show interest and respect towards the speaker. It is rude not to listen to the person whom we are talking with and could cause misunderstanding. Misunderstanding is rooted in a lack of clear communication.  

Communication doesn’t only imply speaking but also implies listening. Through listening, we draw a connection to the speaker and can provide feedback. As we listen, we evaluate the information, understand clearly and respond accordingly.  

Receiving respect from others is necessary because it helps us to feel safe and to express ourselves. It is one of the ways for us to achieve peace and harmony. When listening takes place, respect is displayed.
