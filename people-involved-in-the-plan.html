6. what is the involvement of the organization 's key people in the human resource planning team? identify them and their role in the formulation of the human resource plan.​


Answer:

Human resources planning ensures the best fit between employees and jobs while avoiding manpower shortages or surpluses. There are four key steps to the HRP process. They include analyzing present labor supply, forecasting labor demand, balancing projected labor demand with supply, and supporting organizational goals.Human resources is about managing employees and their well-being. Planning for HR therefore requires that you, as the small-business owner, match employees with the job functions that best meet their skills and interests. ... Successful organizations are able to manage HR as part of the business's overall strategy plan.There are two types of Human Resource Planning (HRP). Hard Human Resource Planning. Soft Human Resource Planning. HRP based on quantitative analysis in order to ensure that the right number of the right sort of people are available when needed is called Hard Human Resource Planning.

Ensuring that legitimate information on COVID-19 will be received by the residents will help make them confident about getting vaccinated.Holding small-group meetings will not only be safe, but will also make residents feel valued as barangay leaders would go out of their way to help them understand and get vaccinated.
