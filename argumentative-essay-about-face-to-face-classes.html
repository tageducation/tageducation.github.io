7. Write an argumentative essay about the pursuance of face-to-face classes​


Face to Face Classes

Individuals are required to attend school under the traditional face-to-face classes system, which is still employed in many states. Face-to-face learning is superior to online learning because there is less distraction and more opportunity for students to interact with their professors and fellow classmates. Since it enables teachers to pinpoint students' areas of weakness and devise strategies to support the learners in overcoming obstacles, interpersonal interaction between students and their instructors is essential. Teachers can mentor students in dealing with a variety of challenges they might come across in their professional domains and educate them about other facets of society. For instance, teachers can build strong bonds with kids, act as their counselors, and teach them moral principles, therefore, interpersonal communication between students and teachers is a crucial issue that is challenging to implement in online learning.

Students are encouraged to connect with others and develop social skills through face-to-face instruction. People all throughout the world hold a variety of beliefs and values. Learning about other cultures makes it easier for people to communicate with many different people in groups and in the community. It also aids in valuing and appreciating the backgrounds of others. Conflicts in the community have also been sparked by the subject of religion. Face-to-face instruction is essential because it allows kids to interact with peers of different faiths and teach them the value of respecting all religions. Additionally, it should be noted that in-person instruction covers matters such as cultural differences and other problems that people encounter in their communities in addition to academic performance.
