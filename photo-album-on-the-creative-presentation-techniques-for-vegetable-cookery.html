1. Make a photo album on the creative presentation techniques for vegetable cookery.


Photo Album: Vegetable Cookery

Answer:

In making a photo album it is best to first have a planned theme or design that will be consistently presented in each part of your album. You also need to present enough or even more photo since that is the required thing. You can also consider using recyclable materials to save up from buying accessories and other materials for your album.

Things to ponder on before starting your Photo Album:

Prepare first your materials- it will enable you to see what you are lacking or to know that it is complete Have a certain topic/subject to focus on- it is necessary to have a particular topic to focus throughout your album, it will help you avoid overdoing things or putting too much pictures for a small part Research the vegetables you will be putting- for background information, and just to be sure you are putting edible plants

Things to ponder on while making your Photo Album:

Put short descriptions/captions- a photo album isn’t just about pasting pictures, you also need to specify their names, use, recipe use Put titles- your album will appear best if you divide it in parts, like for example: “Vegetables for Frying”  Put short recipes- since it is about vegetable cookery, put recipes for more content
