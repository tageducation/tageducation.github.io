Answer:

Animal Morphology

Animal morphology refers to the body structure of the animals found on earth, and it is diverse.

Here are the answers:

Wings - either butterflies or eagles, but let's pick butterfly on this one, so the answer is A. Butterfly. Fur - F. Horse Beaks - D. Eagle Exoskeleton - E. Lobster Shell - G. Clam Gills - C. Stingray Smooth skin - B. Leech

Understand the terms used in the question:

Wings - limb used by birds to fly. Fur - skin covering of mammals. Beaks - birds' projecting jaws. Exoskeleton - a hard outer layer found on some animals, especially arthropods. Shell - a hard covering of some soft-bodied animals Gills - used by fish to breathe.
