2. Why do teenager smoke?


Smoking among teenagers are usually caused by peer pressure or peer influence.

The teenager stage is a phase where an individual seeks for a sense of belonging in a certain group.  And in order to be involved, they have to do the same things or habits that the group is practicing like smoking cigarettes or using of vape.  They consider it a “cool” habit.

Other factors that lead teenagers to smoke are stress and anxiety.  Teenagers claim that smoking combats stress and anxiety, and most of the time, boredom.  Furthermore, smoking controls body weight.  Other times, since parents smoke, they are also influenced to smoke.
