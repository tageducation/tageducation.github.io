Three type of Plate boundaries:

1. Divergent plate boundary

Divergent plate boundaries are created between the two plates which moves away from each other. As the plates moves away from each other, magma rises up on the surface pushing the crust upward and it solidifies creating new crust or sea floor spreading.

2. Transform Fault Boundary

Transform Fault Boundary occurs when two plates are sliding past each other. Grinding between the plates occur and strong Earthquake will mostly be generated

3. Convergent Plate Boundary

Convergent Plate Boundary occurs when two plates moves towards each other. Collision between two plates creates, mountain ranges, volcanoes, earthquake, and trenches.
