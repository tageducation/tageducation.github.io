25. "When you know a thing to hold that you know it, and when you do not know a thing to allow that you know it-This is knowledge Confucius​


Answer:

Yes, that is a quote attributed to Confucius, a Chinese philosopher who lived in the 5th century BCE. The quote is often interpreted as a call to humility and self-awareness, as it suggests that one should be aware of one's own limitations and not pretend to know something that they do not. It also implies that one should be willing to admit when they do not know something and seek out knowledge in order to fill that gap. This approach to knowledge aligns with Confucius's emphasis on self-improvement and the pursuit of learning and wisdom.

29. Things that I can do.Things that I cannot do.Age0-3 years old4-7 years old8-11 years old12-15 years old16-20 years old​


Answer:

Developmental Milestones are a set of functional skills or age-specific tasks that most children can do at a certain age range.

Explanation:

These are the things that you can and cannot do at the given age brackets:

0-3 years old Things you can do. Crawl, walk, talk, feed yourself Things you cannot do. Go to school, clean up yourself, read and write

4-7 years old Things you can do. Express your emotions more eloquently, play for a longer time, read chapter books, go to school Things you cannot do. Travel distances without a parent or a guardian

8-11 years old Things you can do. Cook, clean up the house, do your own assignment Things you cannot do. Do major cleanings and house repairs

12-15 years old Things you can do. Commute to and from nearby places Things you cannot do. Make big life changing decisions

16-20 years old Things you can do. Enter into a more serious relationship, have part time jobs Things you cannot do. Make a big purchase because most probably your bank account will not suffice
