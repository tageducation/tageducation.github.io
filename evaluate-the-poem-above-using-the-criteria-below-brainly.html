How to evaluate the material

Tomlinson (2003: 15) defines materials evaluation as "a technique for determining the value (or potential value) of a set of learning materials." An evaluation that focuses on the needs of the materials' users and makes subjective judgments about their effects is called a Page 8 6 evaluation.

What is the definition of material evaluation?

Resources evaluation can be defined as a method or system for determining the potential value(s) of materials on learners in relation to their goals.

Evaluating language teaching and learning materials is critical to effective language teaching because it motivates us to be reflective practitioners (Schon 1983) and actively reflect on what we need/want from materials, how they are utilized in practice, and to what efficacy they are used.
