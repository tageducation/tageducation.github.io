16. 5 adaptive features in terrestrial plants and aquatic plants


Answer:

1. Waxy Cuticle: Terrestrial plants with waxy cuticles lose less water through their leaves.

2. Stomata: On the surface of leaves, stomata are minuscule apertures that permit gas exchange.

3. Root System: Terrestrial plants have an extensive root system that helps them to absorb water and nutrients from the soil.

4. Photosynthesis: Photosynthesis allows terrestrial plants to convert sunlight into energy.

5.Terrestrial plants have developed morphologies to assist them survive their environment, such as leaves that are suited to catch more sunlight or a tap root that enables them to anchor themselves in.
