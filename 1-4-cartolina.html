16. Nellie cut 1/3 of a cartolina into 9 equal parts. What percent of the whole cartolina is 6 parts?


22.2222222%

Step-by-step explanation:

If Nellie cut 1/3 of a cartolina into 9 equal parts, then each part represents 1/3 * (1/9) = 1/27 of the whole cartolina.

If 6 parts represent the percentage we are looking for, then it represents 6 * (1/27) = 6/27 of the whole cartolina.

To find the percentage, we multiply 6/27 by 100 which equals 22.2222222%. So, 6 parts is 22.2222222% of the whole cartolina.
