25. how are you going to evaluate the validity of visual information?​


Answer:

To evaluate the validity of visual information, you should consider the source of the information, the accuracy and completeness of the information, and the context in which the visual information is presented. Additionally, it is important to consider the accuracy and reliability of any data or statistics presented.

Checking for any bias or agenda of the source is also important.

Finally, it is important to consider if the visual information is supported by other credible sources.
