Answer:

a. The bronze medals are identical.

b. Nash can display his awards in 5,040 ways.

c. I used the formula for permutation of objects with repetition, which is n!/(n1!n2!...nk!), where n is the total number of objects and n1, n2,..., nk are the number of repetitions of each type of object. In this case, n=8, n1=3 (for gold), n2=2 (for silver), and n3=2 (for bronze). So the number of ways is 8!/(3!2!2!)=5,040.
