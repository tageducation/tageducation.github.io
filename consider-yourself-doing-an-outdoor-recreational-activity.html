Importance of Recreational Activities

Recreational activities are important to ourselves, our health and our family. These activities help us improve our physical health as well as our mental health. Recreational activities help us manage stress as well as help us improve our self esteem. They also help in reducing our anxiety and depression. Also, recreational activities helps us make our bodies stronger. It helps our brain function well and even increase our cardiovascular endurance.

Recreational activities can also serve as a team building for our family. It helps us to spend more time bonding with each other. It helps us have a deeper relationship with our family as well.

Bring emergency supplies. In addition to a first aid kit, this includes: a map, compass, flashlight, knife, waterproof fire starter, personal shelter, whistle, warm clothing, high energy food, water, and insect protection.

Learn the ABC's of treating emergencies. Recognizing serious injuries will enable you to attend to a victim until medical help arrives.

Before you leave, find out the weather report.

Arrive early. Plan your trip so that you arrive at your actual campsite with enough daylight to check over the entire site and to set-up camp.

Check for potential hazards.

Avoid areas of natural hazards.

Inspect the site. Look for a level site with enough room to spread out all your gear.

Build fires in a safe area.

Make sure your fires are always attended. Be sure you have an area for a fire that cannot spread laterally or vertically - a grill or stone surface is ideal.

Pitch your tent in a safe spot.

Dispose of trash properly. Remember to recycle - use the proper recycling bins if available.

Be cautious when using a propane stove. Read the instructions that come with the stove and propane cylinder. Use the stove as a cooking appliance only - never leave it unattended while it's burning.

Watch out for bugs. Hornets, bees, wasps, and yellow jackets are a problem at many campsites.

Beware when encountering wildlife.

Beware of poisonous plants.
