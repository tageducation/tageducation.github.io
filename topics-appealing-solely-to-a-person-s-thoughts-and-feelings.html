13. element that appeals to our sense of feel​


Answer: Touch

Explanation:

There are many elements that can appeal to our sense of feel, but perhaps the most commonly thought of is the element of touch. Touch is a sense that is mediated by the skin, which is made up of several layers of cells that contain receptors that are sensitive to mechanical stimuli such as pressure, temperature, and texture. These receptors send signals to the brain, which interprets them as the sensation of touch.
