TITLE OF THE PLAN Steps to complete the plan: People involved in the plan: Picture representing the plan: Reasons indicating how this plan will help the community:


Answer:

Title of the Plan:  Encouraging Residents of Barangay <Rosas> to Undergo COVID-19 Vaccination

Explanation:

Steps to Complete the Plan

The members of the Barangay Council together with the SK officials shall meet to discuss the activities to be undertaken for the project.  This include having small-group meetings with head of the families and strengthening vaccination campaign through the barangay's social media page.Both officials shall be assigned tasks in the implementation of the activities.The barangay secretary shall schedule a meeting between 1 barangay official and 8 residents who are heads of the family.  Every official shall have their assigned time and date for the meeting.The barangay chairman shall make daily announcements, every 5 PM, on their social media page to discuss the status of vaccinated residents, the importance of getting vaccinated, as well as the status of COVID-19 infection in the barangay.The SK officials shall be tasked to create publicity materials on the importance of vaccination and to review the authenticity of COVID-19 information uploaded by the barangay page. They shall be instructed to make the campaign material understandable even by ordinary people.Residents who will agree to get vaccinated shall be coordinated by the barangay to the city health office for schedule of vaccination.

People Involved in the Plan

Barangay and SK Officials as (policy members/implementers)Barangay employees (to provide assistance and coordination)Residents (beneficiaries /stakeholders)

Picture Representing the Plan

    Draw a picture of officials having meeting to discuss the plan and draw people having a small-group meeting and discussing COVID-19 vaccination.

Reasons Indicating How this Plan will Help the Community

Ensuring that legitimate information on COVID-19 will be received by the residents will help make them confident about getting vaccinated.Holding small-group meetings will not only be safe, but will also make residents feel valued as barangay leaders would go out of their way to help them understand and get vaccinated.
