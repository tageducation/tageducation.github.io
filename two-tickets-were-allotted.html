let Y be the number of children's ticket

X + Y = 450 (tickets sold)

Y = 450 - X <Equation 1>

200X + 150Y = 75, 750 <Equation 2>

200X + 150( 450 - X) = 75, 750

200X + 67500 - 150X = 75, 750

50X = 75, 750 - 67, 500

50X = 8250

X = 165 tickets for the adults were sold

X + Y = 450

165 + Y = 450

Y = 285 tickets for the children
