2. advantage and disadvantage of cloth pads ​


Answer:

Pros

You don’t need to be a cloth pads user to see the obvious pros of cloth pads. Their size makes them small enough to fit in a tiny pocket or in the palm of your hand, so they’re convenient and discreet (not that menstruation is anything to be ashamed of).

Other cloth pads pros:

You can swim in them.

You don’t have to worry about them being visible.

You can’t feel them when they’re in properly.

Cons

They're not sterile.

You need to wet them before insertion

You need to rinse them every 3 hours.

They must be thoroughly cleaned and dried before storing after your cycle

They can rip or pull apart when you're removing them.

You need to fish them out with your fingers, which can be quite messy.


3. disadvantages of using cloth pads​


Answer:

Takes time to wash and dry the pads compared to throwing away disposables.

Handwashing can be time consuming and difficult (though you can machine wash them).

More expensive to purchase initially.

Usually bulkier than many “ultrathin” style disposable pads.

Not as easily available (not stocked in supermarkets).

Cloth Pads can be Hard to MaintainCloth pads are best when hand washed first, but they need to be machine washed for extra safety. Once washed and dried, cloth pads must be stored safely. This might be time-consuming for many, but it eventually fits right into a routine.

18. 5 advantages and 5 disadvantages of washing clothes using a machine​


Answer:

Advantages

Time

This is an advantage you must not ignore, especially if you are a busy person. Compared to hand-washing, it saves a large amount of time, and you can also engage in a different task without any supervision. It works a lot faster than hand-washing and adds as many cycles as you want, not worrying about that mysterious new pile of dirty laundry!

Effort

It is a well-known fact that hand-washing requires much more effort than washing them with a washing machine, especially if there are stubborn spots that need vigorous scrubbing. Washing machines need the ‘massive’ effort of just putting laundry, adding detergent, and pushing a button! Even adamant spots and stains are easier to remove using various options to operate your washing machine. Why fear when there is an agitator?

Size

There are a variety of sizes in which a typical washing machine can be availed. It goes on from portable forms to large ones that hook to the water line in the laundry room (or basement). The smaller ones that can hook to the sink can be used in apartments. Apart from the place you live in, the number of family members and the number of large garments (comforters and bedsheets) can also decide the size of the washing machine.

Cycles

Different kinds of cycles can be opted in a washing machine according to different materials. This segregation that varies from larger garments, silk, cotton, delicates, bags, inner garments like underwear and bras can save your clothes from damage if you use an appropriate cycle for your load.

Cost

The good news is that a well-reputed washing machine has cheap purchase and maintenance costs.

Maintenance

Although washing machines ought to make your everyday life easier, if not properly maintained will cause a major hassle. Appropriate maintenance is a must, and good servicing centers are proved necessary.

Disadvantages of Using Washing Machine

Plusses come with minuses. The advantages are bound to have disadvantages as well. Although washing machines get the upper hand in all-round convenience, some sacrifices must be made in case of a budget.

The three disadvantages are:

Water

Additional water is required keeping in mind the different cycles one can opt for in a typical household. However, the difference is negligible.

Electricity

It needs extra money to pay additional electricity bill costs for the operation of the washing machine.

Detergent

As it requires detergent ‘powder’ in place of a detergent bar in hand-washing (which is more experience).

In the end, although machines come with their pros and cons, the disadvantages don’t become a big deal, and you can balance them if you think about it. It is still easier and convenient compared to hand-washing by reducing the time and effort.
