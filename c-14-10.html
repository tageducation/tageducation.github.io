10. 10. 2/7 x 1/4 = A. 5/14 B. 4/14 C. 3/14 D. 1/14 A 10​


Answer:

D. [tex]\frac{1}{14}[/tex]

Step-by-step explanation:

Multiplication of Dissimilar fractions

Dissimilar fractions are fractions with different denominators. Dissimilar fractions follow specific rules in different mathematical operations of addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Multiplication of dissimilar fractions is simply multiplying the numerators and the denominators of the fractions and simplifying the answer.

In this problem,

[tex]\frac{2}{7}[/tex] x [tex]\frac{1}{4}[/tex] = [tex]\frac{2 x 1}{7 x 4}[/tex] = [tex]\frac{2}{28}[/tex]

We can further simplify the answer by using the factor of 2 for both the numerator and the denominator. Hence,

[tex]\frac{2}{28}[/tex] = [tex]\frac{1}{14}[/tex]

Therefore, the answer is [tex]\frac{1}{14}[/tex].
