2. how do these contribute to the development of our literature? ​


Answer:

Writers of literary works from different regions have enriched our Filipino culture and these help in introducing and exploring our humanity as Filipino. And in order to preserve the heritage of our country’s literary arts, we should discover the writer’s origin and their works and continue patronizing and treasuring them.

          When we say literature, it is not always written in a piece of paper or any form of medium but of course, it may follow oral tradition that dates back to the pre-colonial period. And nowadays, some people of Cordillera, Mangyan, and Tagbanwa of Palawan and other ethic groups in the Philippines are still practicing this long storytelling tradition.

       Thereafter, modernization came, the domination of the printed word over the tradition way emerged. Filipino writers began to write their works. They have already write novels, short stories, poems, and other form of literary. These may provide the necessary information we need in our daily lives. To gather these, we may have a tour throughout our community in search of some literary gem. This will help us to find the significance of literary works from our local community in our life. In order to have a systematic research, we may take the following steps:

I.Prewriting
