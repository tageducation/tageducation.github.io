15. Give some examples of;law inertia,law of acceleration and law of interaction


Law of Inertia

(Inertia of Rest)

1) If pulled quickly, a tablecloth can be removed from underneath the dishes. The dishes have the tendency to remain still as long as the friction from the movement of the tablecloth is not too great.

Law of Acceleration

1) A football tends to travel in the direction of the line of action of the force of a kick, at a speed directly proportional to the force and inversely proportional to the mass of the football.

2) Try pulling a wagon that weigh slightly less than you do, it will start moving but the acceleration will be limited because the force is limited. If you are weighing more than the wagon, then acceleration will be decreased by half because the mass has doubled.

Law of Interaction

1) Flying motion of birds. A bird flies by use of its wings. The wings of a bird push air downwards.

Explanation of the Example of the Law of Interaction:

Since forces result from mutual interactions, the air must also be pushing the bird upwards. The size of the force on the air equals the size of the force on the bird; the direction of the force on the air (downwards) is opposite the direction of the force on the bird (upwards). For every action, there is an equal (in size) and opposite (in direction) reaction. Action-reaction force pairs make it possible for birds to fly.
