2. List 10 examples of health products​


Health product

Health products are all forms of goods and products that are used by the public for health purposes. Health products are very important for daily life because they are related to health so it is highly recommended to have some health products at home with the aim of preventing and guarding our health. some examples of health products such as vitamins, drugs, hand sanitizers and other products.

Here are 10 examples of health products:

1. Hand Sanitizer

Hand sanitizer is an effective hand sanitizer as a substitute for water and hand soap. Hand sanitizer can prevent germs, bacteria, and viruses that may be found on hands after direct contact with other objects.

Hand sanitizer is useful for killing germs and is used as an instant hand sanitizer.

2. Vitamins

Vitamins are nutrients that are needed by the body, because they play a role in helping the body's normal metabolic processes. This vitamin is taken in fairly small amounts and is useful in many metabolic processes of the body. We need vitamins in our diet, because our bodies cannot synthesize them fast enough to meet our daily needs.

3. Eucalyptus Oil

Eucalyptus oil is the result of the process of extracting the leaves of the eucalyptus plant. The leaves and twigs of the eucalyptus plant are distilled to produce the final product in the form of oil. Eucalyptus oil is a health product that has many benefits such as a remedy for headaches and nasal congestion.

4. Clinical Thermometer

A clinical thermometer is a device for measuring temperature, designed for clinical use in humans or animals. The function of the thermometer is to measure body temperature when you or a family member has a fever.

5. Inhalers

Inhalers are spray-type drugs to treat asthma symptoms. This inhaler is equipped with a small tube containing the drug that is inserted into a small spray body with a funnel at the end. This funnel will deliver the drug directly into the respiratory system. Inhalers work to help children cope with asthma symptoms.

6. Medicine

Drugs are materials or substances derived from plants, animals, minerals or certain chemical substances that can be used to reduce pain, slow down the disease process and or cure disease.

7. Tensimeter

Tensimeter serves to measure blood pressure, thus helping to monitor blood pressure properly and anticipate early if blood pressure numbers are outside normal limits. Both too high and low blood pressure can lead to dangerous conditions.

8. Soap

Soap is an ingredient (substance) that is used together with water for washing and cleaning. The main benefit of soap is to protect our body's skin. Germs, bacteria and viruses that are all around us threaten the health of the body all the time. For that we need extra protection so that we are protected from various diseases caused by germs and bacteria.

9. Milk

Milk is a good source of vitamins and minerals, especially calcium. Milk has long been recognized and plays an important role in bone health. Calcium in milk is the most important substance for bone health. Calcium is not only good for bone growth in children, but also in adults is needed to maintain bone strength and prevent the risk of osteoporosis.

10. Antiseptic

Antiseptics are chemicals that function to destroy disease-causing microorganisms. one of the benefits of antiseptics in health is used to treat wounds, bites, stings, abrasions, and is used as an antiseptic hand sanitizer.
