What is mindanao folk songs?

Indigenous Philippine Folksongs are greatly influenced by sacred rituals and practices. Most mindanao folk songs are sung in:

wedding feast (they use small and large kettle drums as accompaniment to dancing)courtship songs (such as sua-sua, and usually used in modern troupe performances)war dance (such as sagayan, portrays the gallantry of Muslim combatants while little girls perform a festival dance wherein they wear hollow copper anklets and ring little cells to produce certain sounds)rice harvest ( such as tud-ob)
