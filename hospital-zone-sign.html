30. You may see this road sign near your school. This road sign shows the speed limit of 30 km/h for school zones. Suppose a vehicle is traveling on a freeway with no traffic. As the vehicle approaches a school zone, how would you describe its acceleration?​


Answer:

If the vehicle is traveling on a freeway with no traffic and then approaches a school zone, its acceleration would be negative because the vehicle needs to decelerate or slow down in order to comply with the speed limit of 30 km/h for the school zone.

The rate of deceleration would depend on factors such as the initial speed of the vehicle, the distance to the school zone, and the braking ability of the vehicle.
