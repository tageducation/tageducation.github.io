11. What does being bias mean?​


Being biased means having a preconceived preference or inclination towards a particular opinion, perspective, person, or group, often to the exclusion of others. Bias can affect the way someone perceives information and influences their beliefs. Bias can be based on factors such as personal experiences, cultural background, social and economic status, and more. It can also be unconscious and result from ingrained attitudes and beliefs that have developed over time.
