Three birds competing for a worm is an example of intraspecific competition. This can have both beneficial and harmful effect. Beneficial effect will be the most competitor individual will somehow able to survive and reproduce its strong gene. The harmful effect will be non competitor organism will not survive and die.

It is a natural process in nature "natural selection". Intraspecific competition is more intense compared to interspecific competition since the same species are competing for limited resources.
