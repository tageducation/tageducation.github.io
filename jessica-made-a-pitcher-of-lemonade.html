5. 9. Jessica made a pitcher of lemonade. What can she do to dilute if she thinks itdoesn't taste right?A. Add waterB. Add sugarC. Boil the lemonadeD. Put it in the refrigerator​


Answer:

Dilution

If Jessica made a pitcher of lemonade and it turned out too concentration, what she can do to dilute the solution is to add water, therefore, the answer is letter A.

Explanation:

The term used to refer to the process of decreasing the concentration of a solution in a particular solution (like the lemonade made by Jessica) is dilution. It is usually performed by mixing the concentration solution with more solvent, without adding additional solute. Normally, the solvent added is water.

Dilution happens a lot especially in cooking. When cooks find out that the meal they are creating has a sauce that is too concentrated, all they need to do is to add more water to balance its taste.
