24. DEFINITION OF:1) CHANT2) CAPPELLA3)HARPSICHORD4)NEUME5) MONOPHONIC​


Answer:

1. chant - a short musical passage in two or more phrases used for singing unmetrical words; a psalm or canticle sung to such music.

2. Capella - singing without instrumental accompaniment

3. harpsichord - a stringed instrument resembling a grand piano but usually having two keyboards and two or more strings for each note and producing tones by the plucking of strings with plectra.

4. nueme - a note or group of notes to be sung to a single syllable.

5. monophonic - using only one channel of transmission.
