12. Chris bought 68 cartons of canned food. Each carton contained 36 cans. He repacked them into boxes of 8 cans and sold each box for PHP75.00. How much money did he receive from selling all the canned food?​


Answer:

22,950.00

Step-by-step explanation:

Chris bought a total of 68 x 36 = 2,448 cans of food.

He then repacked the cans into boxes of 8, so he had a total of 2,448/8 = 306 boxes of canned food.

If he sold each box for PHP75.00, then his total revenue from selling all the canned food was 306 x PHP75.00 = PHP22,950.00.

Therefore, Chris received PHP22,950.00 from selling all the canned food.
